WWE Live Event Results From Champaign (2/9) - Del Rio Faces Champion, Fandango's Finisher

By Raj Giri February 10, 2013

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Patty Torres for sending in these results from last night's SmackDown live event in Champaign, IL:

Only a few side comments will be put here. They didn't sell seats in the high up area, the assembly hall was 85-90% full for the sections they were selling seats for. Security guards were amazing, usually I get trampled by kids leaning over the rails and people standing up, that didn't fly last night.

Wade Barrett cut a promo on the tron.

They had us live vote what the main event could be a 2 out 3 falls or a no disqualifications via text/Twitter

Alberto Del Rio defeated Wade Barrett in a no disqualifications World Heavyweight Championship match. Tables, kendo sticks and chairs were weapons used in the match. Wade was going for a flying elbow and Alberto hit a bulldog through the table for the winning pin.

No word on the upcoming PPV in June for Chicago was given and no advertisement for their next show was given.
