Jack Swagger breaks Ricardo Rodriguez's ankle

BY WWE.COM STAFF

March 18, 2013

Following an attack at the hands of Jack Swagger, WWE.com can report that Ricardo Rodriguez has suffered a broken ankle. Rodriguez had been at ringside for a match between his friend, World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio, and Cody Rhodes, when Swagger struck. The self-proclaimed "Real American" trapped Rodriguez in his Patriot Lock maneuver and refused to release him. Rodriguez was taken to the locker room area, where doctors diagnosed the beloved manager with a broken ankle. Michael Cole later confirmed the news to the WWE Universe. Stay with WWE.com for updates on Rodriguez's condition.
