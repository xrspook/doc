#WWE #RAW REPORT: THE FUTURE OF THE WWE TITLE WILL BE DETERMINED AT MONEY IN THE BANK; YOU MIGHT WANT TO WATCH THE FINAL 15 MINUTES OF THIS WEEK'S SHOW

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-06-02 23:02:48

We start off with highlights from last night’s Main Event between Evolution and The Shield. In the end, it was Evolution that was vanquished at the hands of The Shield. What happens tonight? What about the champion who is without his bride after what happened at Payback?

We are in Indianapolis, Indiana and your announcers are Michael ‘Mayflower’ Cole, Jerry ‘Chitwood’ Lawler, and John ‘Larry Legend’ Layfield.

Match Number Eight: Alberto Del Rio versus Dolph Ziggler in a Money in the Bank Qualifying Match

They lock up and Del Rio with a side head lock and he holds on. Del Rio with a kick to the ribs followed by punches. Ziggler with a rollup for a near fall followed by a kick and punches. Ziggler with a neck breaker for a near fall. Ziggler with the rapid fire elbow drops followed by a leaping elbow drop and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a kick and an arm breaker. Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri to the shoulder and Del Rio gets a near fall.

Del Rio with an arm bar. Del Rio sends Ziggler to the mat and Del Rio wants everyone to be quiet. Del Rio with a splash in the ropes and he chokes Ziggler. Del Rio with a belly-to-back suplex and he gets a near fall. Del Rio returns to the arm bar. Ziggler avoids the tilt-a-whirl back breaker and Ziggler with a drop kick and both men are down.

Ziggler with a cross body and he punches Del Rio. Ziggler leaps into the corner and he tries for the neck breaker but Del Rio escapes and he punches Ziggler in the kidneys. Ziggler avoids a belly-to-back suplex again and Ziggler hits the Fameasser for a near fall.

Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall. Del Rio kicks Ziggler in the back and he punches him in the back. Del Rio goes up top but Ziggler hits a Super X Factor for a near fall. Ziggler tries for the Zig Zag but Del Rio pushes him away. Ziggler avoids the super kick and Ziggler counters the DDT with the float over into the cross arm breaker and Ziggler taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio (Advances to Money in the Bank Ladder Match)
