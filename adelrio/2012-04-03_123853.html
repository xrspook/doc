Alberto Del Rio challenges Sheamus

Sheamus arrives at Raw SuperShow as the new World Heavyweight Champion following a record-setting WrestleMania performance against Daniel Bryan.

But The Great White hasn't even begun to speak when he's interrupted by a visitor ...

... the returning Alberto Del Rio.

The Essence of Excellence congratulates The Celtic Warrior, but the tension thickens instantly when Del Rio warns, "Just as soon as you won that title, you're going to lose it."

Del Rio goes on to name himself as the new challenger for Sheamus' championship.

"Alberto! Great to see you, fella!" replies The Great White, "Because these people don't want to see me ramble on and on and on. They want to see me Brogue Kick someone in the face."

"Relax Sheamus," replies The Mexican Aristocrat, "I'm not looking for a fight, because I've already got one."

Del Rio sneers that Mr. Laurinaitis has granted him a match against Sheamus on SmackDown and, should Del Rio win, he'll be awarded a future World Heavyweight Title opportunity.

He is promptly met with a Brogue Kick for his troubles, and left to stew as The Great White celebrates in the ring.
