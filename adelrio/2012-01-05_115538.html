John Cena vs. Alberto Del Rio - WWE Championship Match

Ricardo Rodriguez announces the arrival of WWE Champion Alberto Del Rio.

The Mexican Aristocrat is ready to defend the WWE Title at Vengeance.

His opponent in San Antonio's AT&T Center is John Cena.

Cena is unable to jump in the ring as usual because it collapsed during the previous World Heavyweight Championship Match.

Despite the nearly-destroyed ring conditions, the Last Man Standing Match goes on as planned.

If Cena can defeat Del Rio, he'll win a record-shattering 11th WWE Title.

But Del Rio will do everything in his power to retain the WWE Championship.

The action spills outside of the ring as Cena attacks Del Rio with the steel steps.

Del Rio and Cena take their fight to the interview area of the AT&T Center.

The WWE Champion tosses his opponent into part of the Vengeance sign!

When the competitors return inside the arena, the set becomes part of the action.

Cena eventually sets up the steel steps to toss Del Rio through the Spanish announce table with an Attitude Adjustment.

Suddenly, Awesome Truth emerges to attack Cena.

The Miz nails Cena with the Skull Crushing Finale.

Del Rio capitalizes and hits his opponent with the WWE Title.

A member of the WWE Universe helps Del Rio to his feet as Cena can't make it to his feet before the official's 10-count.

Alberto Del Rio retains the WWE Championship at Vengeance!
