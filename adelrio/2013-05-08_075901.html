WWE hosts a Be a STAR rally in Knightdale, N.C.: photos | WWE Community

Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez host a Be a STAR rally at Hodge Road Elementary School.

The suburban Raleigh, N.C., students can't wait to see some of their favorite WWE Superstars.

More than 400 students attend the rally at the Knightdale, N.C., school.

The students are in grades 2 through 5.

Del Rio tells the students that WWE, their partners, teachers, parents and guardians are there to help kids who are being bullied.

The kids take the Be a STAR Pledge during the rally.

Rodriguez and Del Rio sign autographs for the children after the rally.
