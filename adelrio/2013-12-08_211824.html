Second Set of WWE House Show Results 12.06.13: Stockton, California

Posted by Jeffrey Harris on 12.07.2013

Attendance around 3,000-4,000. Kids everywhere. 15 perent of the area tapered off and 40-50 percent. Cheap cheap seats were empty. Row one was still available one hour before the start. Tomorrow night they are in Sacramento, only 50 miles away.

John Cena beat Damien Sandow and Alberto Del Rio in a handicap match in 13:10. Ten minutes of promos and intros before the match started with a one minute promo in the middle. Both wore their respective shirts to the ring, swapped shirts with each other, and wore them half the match. No "Cena Sucks" chants.

Justin Roberts (who had quite a bit of cologne on) and the refs wore earpieces at a house show? My, how times have changed.

The show went two hours and 50 minutes with a 15-minute intermission. No return date was announced.
