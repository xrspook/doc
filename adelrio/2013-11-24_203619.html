WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: FINAL SHOW BEFORE SURVIVOR SERIES; REY'S IN RING RETURN; TAG TITLE MATCH; BRYAN VS. HARPER; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-11-22 22:00:08

We are in Atlanta, Georgia and your announcers are John ‘We try to do a hard sell for pay per views on Friday nights’ Layfield and Michael ‘When do the Hawks get a new arena’ Cole.

Renee Young is in the interview area with Alberto Del Rio. She reminds Alberto about what has happened between John and Alberto over the last few weeks.

Renee reminds Alberto about what happened on Monday night when John faked an additional injury to his triceps.

Alberto says that he never saw that coming. If he wanted to injure John Cena, he could have. He has done enough to the man who stole his title. If John’s arm is good enough, congratulations. He tells John to enjoy his night on Raw. Hiding behind a sling will not help on Monday. He will make John pay. He will hurt John and he will hurt him bad. In two days, John Cena will be tapping out. He will take back what is his. He will take back the World Title.
