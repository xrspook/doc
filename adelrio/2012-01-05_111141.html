Raw Money in the Bank Ladder Match

John Cena vs. CM Punk - WWE Championship Match

Alberto Del Rio is Raw's new "Mr. Money in the Bank." But you ... you already know that.

Alberto Del Rio claims it is his destiny to become WWE Champion.

R-Truth aims to silence the "Little Jimmys" of the world by capturing the coveted Raw Money in the Bank briefcase.

Toting a ladder of his own, Alex Riley is ready for action.

After winning last year's Raw Money in the Bank Ladder Match, The Miz is angling for a repeat victory.

Ladders and bodies collide as the match gets underway.

R-Truth and The Awesome One engage in an unorthodox in-ring duel.

Evan Bourne and Kofi Kingston throw caution to the wind as they vie for the dangling briefcase.

The Dreadlocked Dynamo punishes his opponents from above.

Del Rio and Truth ensure that the briefcase remains just out of reach for Rey Mysterio and Bourne.

"Air" Bourne executes a stunning Shooting Star Press from atop a ladder.

The Miz clings to the red briefcase just moments before plummeting to the canvas.

A trainer tends to The Awesome One's ladder-induced injury.

Jack Swagger tries to contain Kingston's controlled frenzy with an ankle lock.

The exciting and energetic Kingston drops the "boom" on the conspiracy theorist.

Mysterio dials up the 619 in incredible fashion.

It's every man for himself as a WWE Title opportunity quite literally hangs in the balance.

Twisted metal and battered bodies lie scattered throughout the arena.

The Mexican Aristocrat rips off Mysterio's mask, momentarily distracting The Ultimate Underdog.

But a moment is all Del Rio needs.

The furious WWE Chairman demands that Alberto Del Rio cash in his Raw Money in the Bank contract immediately.
