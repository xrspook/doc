WWE.com: Week in Photos, August 4, 2012

United States Champion Santino Marella locks up with the No. 1 contender to the World Heavyweight Title, Alberto Del Rio.

The Mexican Aristocrat takes the fight to The Milan Miracle right away.

After his impressive victory, Del Rio declares that he will not compete again until his bout against Sheamus at SummerSlam.

In his first bit of business, Booker schedules Alberto Del Rio to take on Randy Orton, and World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus to battle Tensai!

It is a fast-paced, back-and-forth battle between two of SmackDown’s finest.

When Ricardo interferes in the bout, Del Rio attempts to make an escape, but Sheamus is there to toss The Mexican Aristocrat back into the ring ...

... And right into an angry Viper!
