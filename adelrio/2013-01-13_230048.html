1/12 SMACKDOWN BRAND RESULTS FROM ALEXANDRIA, VA

By Ron Frank on 2013-01-13 09:17:03

Orton beat Rhodes in No DQ match. It had been advertised that Orton would take on Alberto Del Rio.

Sheamus beat Damien Sandow. It was originally announced to be Sheamus vs. Big Show.

Del Rio beat The Big Show. The crowd quiet for Del Rio. I heard a few people asking if ADR was a good guy now.

Sheamus hit a Brougue Kick on Show afterwards.

The Coliseum holds about 8,000 and was about 90% full. This may be the last WWE show for a while as the coliseum is about to undergo renovations and upgraded to 11,000 seat capacity.
