Lucha Underground Results (2/18): Alberto El Patron Appears, Trios Main Event, Big Return

By Sean Ross Sapp | February 18, 2015

Welcome to Wrestling Inc's live coverage of Lucha Underground. Refresh the page momentarily for updates as the show progresses, and join in on the comment board below and let your voice be heard.

We go to a commercial break. We come back and see an interview highlighting Texano, who attacked Alberto El Patron last week.

Super Fly vs. Texano Jr

The two square off after a quick series of reversals that don't see anyone gain an advantage. Texano fires off a leg lariat and a fireman's carry neckbreaker for the win.

Winner: Texano via pinfall (neckbreaker)

Alberto El Patron comes to the ring and attacks Texano as the crowd goes crazy. Alberto gets Texano's rope and beats him with it until security breaks it up. Matt Striker teases that the AAA title may be defended in Lucha Underground.
