WWE.com: Week in Photos, September 7, 2012

With No. 1 contender Alberto Del Rio joining the Raw announce team ...

The Great White traps Swagger in the Cloverleaf, forcing The All-American American to tap out.

When Alberto Del Rio attacks Sheamus, The Celtic Warrior returns the favor.

Sheamus looks to level Del Rio with the Brogue Kick, but Del Rio's personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez, takes the hit for his boss.

John Cena battles Alberto Del Rio in the night's main event, a Falls Count Anywhere Match.

When the brawl spills backstage ...

... An unexpected guest arrives. Punk's interference allows Del Rio to get the win over Cena.

Adding insult to injury, Punk hits a Go to Sleep on the hood of his car.

SmackDown kicks off with a neck brace-sporting Ricardo Rodriguez, who is suffering from "undisclosed injuries" that resulted from stepping in front of a Brogue Kick "destined" for Alberto Del Rio Monday night.
