WWE.com: Week in Photos, July 28, 2012

In the night's first match, World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, Rey Mysterio & Sin Cara take on Alberto Del Rio, Dolph Ziggler & Chris Jericho.

Jericho & Del Rio work well together as they take the fight to Rey.

The night's main event is an epic Fatal 4-Way Match to determine the No. 1 contender for the World Heavyweight Championship.

Rey Mysterio, Daniel Bryan, Alberto Del Rio and Kane battle it with a huge opportunity on the line.

Bryan attempts the "Yes!" Lock on The Mexican Aristocrat, but Kane breaks the hold.

Thanks to a little help from Ricardo Rodriguez, Del Rio pulls off the huge victory, and is now the No. 1 contender to Sheamus' illustrious title!
