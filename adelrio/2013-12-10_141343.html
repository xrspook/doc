Championship Ascension Ceremony: photos | WWE.com

WWE's "Yes!" man replies that his father was a log scaler.

Twenty former WWE and World Heavyweight Champions — ranging from Rey Mysterio to Bret Hart to WWE COO Triple H — enter the ring for the Championship Ascension Ceremony.

The King of Kings, a former holder of both World Titles, presides over the event.

The WWE Universe in Seattle, not far from Daniel Bryan's hometown of Aberdeen, Wash., makes clear that The Beard is a favorite.

Even Shawn Michaels tries to quash the "Daniel Bryan" and "Yes!" chants.

Former World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry acknowledges the moment.

This Sunday at WWE TLC, both World Titles will hang in the balance in a truly historic bout to determine The Champion of Champions.

WWE Champion Randy Orton enters the squared circle.

World Heavyweight Champion John Cena charges to the ring.

Triple H asks both champions to attach their titles to an apparatus in a symbolic gesture.

The Viper claims Cena lacks ruthless aggression and takes a poke at his ego. "You say you don’t care about being the face of WWE. That’s a lie. I know you, John. Image is everything to you.”

The Champ counters, suggesting Daniel Bryan is Orton's polar opposite, in that Bryan had to work for everything he has gotten.

Cena asks if either of Bryan's parents was a Superstar, like Orton's father and grandfather were.

“A guy like you has been given every single thing in the WWE," Cena tells Orton, saying WWE's Apex Predator has been sheltered by Triple H and Stephanie McMahon.

At WWE TLC, "I will be at my very best. I just hope that you are, too," Cena says.

As the World Titles ascend toward the ceiling ...

... Orton fires off a cheap shot, sending the championship ceremony into bedlam.

During the pull-apart, Triple H separates CM Punk and the WWE Champion, leading to Punk getting hold of The King of Kings.

Suddenly, Triple H's best friend, HBK, Superkicks Punk!

Bryan drives into his mentor with a Running Knee Smash.

Orton tries to RKO Bryan, but The Beard shoves him off, inadvertently sending Orton into Stephanie.

Triple H then Pedigrees the WWE Champion ...

... and Cena is left standing with The Authority as Raw comes to a close days before the huge match to determine The Champion of Champions.
