WWE SUPERSTARS REPORT: HEY LADY . . . ON COMMENTARY; TITUS, TRUTH, BIG E, AND DEL RIO IN ACTION; AN AJ LEE INTERVIEW

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-07-03 22:39:00

We are in Hartford, Connecticut and your announcers are Tom ‘You cannot get rid of me’ Phillips and Renee ‘Making My Debut between Canada Day and Independence Day’ Young.

Byron Saxton is with Alberto Del Rio in the locker room. Byron asks Alberto about the return of Chris Jericho. Alberto says that everyone is talking about Chris Jericho but they should be talking about him because he came so close to the World Title. He will be the next Intercontinental Champion at Battleground. Jericho will not stop him on his way to championship glory.

Match Number Two: Alberto Del Rio versus R Truth (with Xavier Woods)

The crowd chants USA as the match starts. They lock up and Truth with a side head lock. Truth with a shoulder tackle and he wants to know ‘What’s Up’. Truth does some dancing and then he does a split followed by a hip toss. Truth with a split and a leg drop to Del Rio for a near fall. Del Rio sends Truth into the turnbuckles and he kicks Truth.

Truth with a kick to Del Rio and he clotheslines Del Rio over the top rope to the floor. Truth goes to the floor and he punches Del Rio and sends him back into the ring. Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri and it knocks Truth off the apron and to the floor.

We go to commercial.

We are back and they are back in the ring. Del Rio with a side head lock and Truth with punches. Truth with a sunset flip for a near fall. Del Rio with a kick and DDT to Truth. Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio punches Truth in the corner and he chokes Truth until the referee warns Del Rio.

Truth with forearms followed by an Irish whip but Del Rio moves when Truth charges into the corner and Truth hits the ring post with his shoulder. Del Rio connects with a kick to Truth’s head on the apron. Del Rio goes up top and he hits a double sledge to the head and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a rear chin lock on Truth.

Truth with a jawbreaker and elbow. Del Rio with a kick and punch to the head. Truth with a leg lariat and clotheslines. Truth with a kick to Del Rio and he hits the scissors kick for a near fall. Truth with a splash into the corner and then Del Rio misses a clothesline. Del Rio with a super kick that sends Truth to the floor.

Del Rio throws Woods into the ringside barrier. Del Rio sends Truth back into the ring and then Del Rio tells Woods to stay down. Truth with an inside cradle for a near fall. Del Rio with a kick but Truth with a rollup for a near fall. Truth misses a splash into the corner and Del Rio runs into boots but Del Rio puts Truth in the ropes and he hits a double stomp.

Del Rio floats over and applies the cross arm breaker and Truth taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
