WWE.com: Cashing in Money in the Bank photos

Alberto Del Rio claims his destiny at SummerSlam 2011, cashing his opportunity in on two-time Money in the Bank winner CM Punk.

After nine months of waiting, Dolph Ziggler cashes in his own battered Money in the Bank contract on the April 8, 2013, edition of Raw at the expense of World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio.

It takes a Famouser and a Zig Zag to put Del Rio away, but Ziggler makes good on his long-standing promise to steal the show and become the World Heavyweight Champion.

CM Punk cashes in on The Ultimate Opportunist himself during Raw in 2008.

The Second City Saint cashes in again the following year on Jeff Hardy at Extreme Rules 2009.

Kane cashes in on Rey Mysterio on the very same night he won it, at the Money in the Bank pay-per-view in 2010.

After capturing the Money in the Bank contract for a WWE Championship Match at Money in the Bank 2012, John Cena redeems the contract for an opportunity at CM Punk's championship at Raw 1,000.

Cena captures the disqualification win thanks to Big Show's interference, but since the title does not change hands via disqualification, he becomes the first Superstar whose Money in the Bank cash-in ends in failure.

The Miz cashes in on Randy Orton during a stunning episode of Raw in 2010.

Jack Swagger surprises Chris Jericho, cashing in on SmackDown in 2010 less than a week after winning the opportunity.

At SummerSlam 2013, Randy Orton emerged, moments after Daniel Bryan had overcome John Cena to win WWE's top prize.

And following special guest referee Triple H's shocking Pedigree ...

... The Viper cashed in and once again became WWE Champion!

Daniel Bryan cashes in on Big Show at WWE TLC in 2011.
