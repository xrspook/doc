Dolph Ziggler Tested At RAW, Is He Cleared To Return?

By Raj Giri | June 04, 2013

F4WOnline is reporting that Dolph Ziggler took an imPACT test at RAW yesterday and has been cleared to return to the ring.

Ziggler had suffered a concussion after an errant kick by Jack Swagger at the May 7th SmackDown taping in Raleigh, NC. He had been out of action since, although he appeared at this past weekend's SmackDown events in Pennsylvania and New York.

The report on F4WOnline noted that a match between Ziggler and Alberto Del Rio for the World Heavyweight Championship will likely be added to the Payback pay-per-view on June 16, 2013 at the Allstate Arena in Rosemont, Illinois.
