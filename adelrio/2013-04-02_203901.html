WWE WrestleMania 29: Alberto Del Rio vs Jack Swagger “World Heavyweight Championship” Wallpaper

Posted by Dez on Apr 2, 2013

Alberto Del Rio vs Jack Swagger | WrestleMania 29

Pride. Freedom. Honor. And The World Heavyweight Championship! Now, this feud may not be billed as the primary attraction of the night, but if you look closely it’s a very unique and interesting kind of rivalry. One, because the ‘guys on the side’ seem to be doing more to bring that much-needed intensity into this ongoing battle, and two, because Zeb Colter’s weekly rants could really strike a nerve if taken seriously. This angle is a very ballsy, controversial move by WWE, and one that could be very interesting if it takes a ‘edgier’ tone post-WrestleMania.

I guess the biggest issue here is that the focus isn’t really on the World Heavyweight Championship. Swagger and his newfound brainwasher, Zeb Colter, have made it their mission to make an example out of Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez. Swagger’s recent attacks on Ricardo’s ankle and Zeb’s heinous jabs at the “non-true Americans” show – very clearly – that they’re more interested in making a statement than capturing the gold.

It’s all good. I guess it makes the feud more personal and intense. But then again if they made the championship the centerpiece, it would be all the more heartwrenching if someone like Swagger should happen to win it. Then, seeing the World Heavyweight Championship – a title that shows no bias towards the color of any champion’s skin – hanging on the shoulder of a mentally-contorted superstar like Jack Swagger would be a terrible thing to endure.

To be honest though, I see Del Rio winning this one…only to have Dolph Ziggler cash-in and steal a win. Predictable? Yes. But it’ll be awesome to see nonetheless!

Anyway, my anticipation is pretty much at its peak right now since we’re less than a week away from WrestleMania 29! You can expect a couple more wallpapers within the week, and then we’re off to kickstart the big one on Sunday. Download and enjoy, folks!
