Rey Mysterio vs. Alberto Del Rio -- 2-out-of-3 Falls Match

Alberto Del Rio touts himself before facing Rey Mysterio.

Del Rio says his destiny is to win the Royal Rumble ...

... and main-event WrestleMania.

Del Rio is ready to battle Mysterio in a 2-out-of-3 Falls Match.

Del Rio gets the first fall by making Mysterio tap out to his cross armbreaker.

Del Rio continues to punish The Master of the 619.

There is no love lost between Del Rio and Mysterio.

After a roll-up pinfall by Mysterio, the score is 1-1.

Mysterio crashes down on Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez.

As Mysterio tries to get back in the ring, Rodriguez grabs his leg.

Mysterio is count out by the ref, giving Del Rio the winning fall.

Del Rio savors his victory.

After the match, Mysterio dishes out some payback.
