SmackDown Five-Point Preview: July 25, 2014

BY Ryan Pappolla

July 25, 2014

Can The Mexican Aristocrat slow down a roaring Reigns?

Following a tumultuous 24-hour stretch for Roman Reigns spanning from WWE Battleground to Raw, he must now re-focus as he squares off with Alberto Del Rio on SmackDown.

Reigns was a second away from the WWE World Heavyweight Championship on multiple occasions on Sunday, and followed that by making a huge impact on Raw, when he not only defeated Randy Orton and Kane in a Handicap Match, but then attacked Orton during Triple H’s SummerSlam announcement.

However, he cannot overlook Del Rio, who after coming incredibly close to both the WWE World Heavyweight & Intercontinental Championships in recent months, is surely looking to make a statement against the likes of someone of Reigns’ caliber.
