Kofi Kingston vs. Drew McIntyre - Elimination Chamber Qualifying Match

Kofi Kingston interrupts Alberto Del Rio's Royal Rumble celebration

Drew McIntyre prepares to face Intercontinental Champ Kofi Kingston

in the first of four Elimination Chamber Qualifying Matches of the night.

In the Royal Rumble Match, Kofi entered at No. 26 and lasted 24 minutes.

Now, he faces the Superstar that he beat for his second Intercontinental Title.

Both Superstars hope to win a spot in the Elimination Chamber

... but also at headlining WrestleMania.

The Intercontinental Champ counters his adversary to hit an incredible DDT.

After a distraction by Alberto Del Rio, McIntyre hits the Future Shock DDT.

Drew McIntyre is going to WWE Elimination Chamber.

Could the "Chosen One" be on his way to WrestleMania as well?

Alberto Del Rio comes before the WWE Universe

to celebrate winning the biggest Royal Rumble Match in history.

The aristocratic Superstar wants Hornswoggle to try and break a pinata.

He entices him with promises of candy.

Blindfolded, ‘Swoggle can not see that Del Rio hasn't lowered his target.

When Del Rio attacks Hornswoggle

Kofi Kingston rushes to the ring.
