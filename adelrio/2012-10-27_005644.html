The ‘WWE NXT’ show must go on

Weather will not deter fans, officials, talent in West Palm Beach

With Hurricane Sandy in the Atlantic Ocean and a careful examination of the storm's projected path, WWE NXT officials concluded the weather conditions will not be significant enough to cancel Friday night’s show in West Palm Beach.

NXT will debut in West Palm Beach on Friday, Oct. 26 at the West Palm Beach National Guard Armory. The show will be highlighted with a special guest appearance by WWE superstar Alberto Del Rio who will sign autographs for fans prior to bell time. WWE NXT talent will also be signing autographs and posing for photos.

There will be eight exciting matches featuring the future superstars and divas of WWE. Bell time is 7:30 p.m.

VIP ticket holders receive access to the autograph session with Del Rio at 6 p.m. Other ticket levels receive access at 6:30 p.m.

WWE NXT is the official developmental system for WWE. Tickets range from $10 to $35 and are available at the West Palm Beach National Guard Armory, 3193 Gun Rd., West Palm Beach, Fla. 33406, participating Buddy’s Home Furnishings and online at www.NXTWrestling.com. Visit www.fcwwrestling.com.
