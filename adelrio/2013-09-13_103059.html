WWE Magazine's List Issue

BY WWE Magazine

September 12, 2013

Out Now—WWE Magazine’s October Issue

Daniel Bryan Is Making Everyone Submit…

With his career reaching heights previously unheard of, cover athlete Daniel Bryan sat down with WWE Magazine to chat about becoming the new face of WWE, the harshest criticism he’s heard, and if he’ll ever take a moment to relax. Plus, in honor of the Submission Specialist, and this being the “List Issue,” we rank the 10 most iconic and important tap-outs in sports-entertainment history.

The 75 Greatest Matches Of The 21st Century

We’re only 13 years into the 21st century, but the WWE locker room has created hundreds of instant classics. With that in mind, the editors of WWE Magazine embarked on the near-impossible task of naming the 75 Greatest Matches Of The 21st Century (so far), from iconic WrestleMania moments to standout Raw bouts. Relive these mat classics!

The 51 Greatest Villains Of All Time (Re-Ranked)

Seven years ago, WWE Magazine singled out 51 of the worst, most despicable Superstars in sports-entertainment history. With so many new names to add to the list, we re-ranked our guide to the vilest villains, which features a shocking new No. 1!

Today’s WWE Superstars … Yesterday

Half a decade ago, we took a camera to WWE’s development territory, Florida Championship Wrestling, and photographed the entire roster. The sheer number of familiar Superstars in this picture will stun you.

Plus, in-depth interviews with:

  • Jack Swagger

  • Kofi Kingston

  • Fandango

  • Dolph Ziggler

  • Alberto Del Rio

  • Layla

  • And … The Bellas Twins!

Bonus!

The Shield open up in their first official interview about what “justice” means exactly, and which dream team they’d each love to face.

Another year, another race toward the Vince McMahon Trophy: Check out the rosters for the 2013 WWE Superstar Fantasy Football League. Plus, tips on surviving your fantasy league from CBSSports.com’s Nathan Zegura.

Kofi Kingston names the 12 most jaw-dropping moments in his six-year career, and what he has to do to take the next step in WWE.

As WWE Hell In A Cell approaches, look at these nine Superstars who’ve gone through hell—in some way, shape or form—and learn how to create your own Cell at the office.

WWE COO Triple H takes you through the next must-have DVD for your collection, “Thy Kingdom Come,” available Sept. 24th. Find out how he learned to play The Game.
