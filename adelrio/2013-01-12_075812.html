SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Jan. 11, 2013

BY Michael Burdick

January 11, 2013

2) How exactly does Big Show lose the World Heavyweight Title?

On Tuesday night, a breaking news report from WWE.com revealed that Alberto Del Rio had successfully captured the World Heavyweight Championship from Big Show in a SmackDown Last Man Standing Match!

But the brutal details of how it all went down still remain a mystery. Last Man Standing showdowns are always unique and completely ruthless, as the only way to win is to devastate your opponent to such an extent that they can no longer answer the 10-count. This begs the question: What did the now-three-time World Champion Del Rio do to topple the mighty giant? Find out tonight at 8/7 CT.
