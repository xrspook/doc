The Tennessee State Fairgrounds in Music City is the site of this week’s loaded edition of Ring of Honor Television.

We kick things off with a follow up from last week’s main event. The Briscoe Brothers take on arch rivals The Kingdom: Matt Taven & Michael Bennett with Maria Kanellis. Jay Briscoe appeared to be finished with The Kingdom after winning Fight Without Honor at Final Battle 2014 against Kingdom member Adam Cole. Now Matt Taven, Mike Bennett and Maria Kanellis have put the Briscoes on notice that there is a whole lot more coming their way. Weary of the constant interference of Maria Kanellis, Jay Briscoe has promised to have a surprise for Kanellis and the Kingdom!!

This week’s Top Prospect Tournament match-up features the 300 hundred pound mean and nasty Beer City Bruiser against the young, good looking, athletic, and all American boy, New England standout Mikey Webb.

The much heralded Ring of Honor debut of the international superstar Alberto El Patron has arrived. The Mexican aristocrat makes his Ring of Honor Television debut, going one on one with ROH legend " The Fallen Angel” Christopher Daniels. As one half of the Addiction, Daniels (stepped up recently and signed on to welcome El Patron to Ring of Honor. Alberto is hungry to prove himself in ROH and quickly accepted the challenge. Can Daniels spoil the debut of El Patron or will Alberto start his ROH career on the winning track? Do not miss this opportunity to see a true dream match on Ring of Honor Television!
