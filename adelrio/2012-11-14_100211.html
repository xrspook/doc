Published on Nov 13, 2012 by Chris Van Vliet

Emmy winning entertainment reporter Chris Van Vliet from WOIO in Cleveland interviews Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez. They talk about being there when Jerry "The King" Lawler had his heart attack, their thoughts on The Rock demanding a title shot, Ricardo's in-ring abilities and whether or not WWE will break away from the PG era now that Linda McMahon lost her bid for senate.
