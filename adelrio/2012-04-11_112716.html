Sheamus & Gene Okerlund vs. Daniel Bryan & Alberto Del Rio

Great White recovers to take the fight to his opponents.

Does Daniel Bryan look ready to compete in the SuperSmackDown main event? YES!

Ricardo Rodriguez seizes the attention of the capacity crowd in Hampton, Va. to announce ...

... the arrival of Alberto Del Rio.

Sheamus seeks to hold the line against The Mexican Aristocrat ...

... but the momentum quickly swings against The Celtic Warrior.

The devious duo of Del Rio and Bryan draw "Mean" Gene Okerlund into the ring.

The WWE Hall of Famer quickly realizes that he's outnumbered ...

... just as the rest of the WWE legends in attendance arrive to even the odds at ringside.

Pat Patterson and Roddy Piper make their presence known to the feisty vegan ...

... and the reigning World Heavyweight Champion capitalizes with a pin of the befuddled Bryan.

The WWE legends proceed to overwhelm a frightened Ricardo Rodriguez.
