SmackDown Five-Point Preview: July 26, 2013

BY Alex Giannini

July 26, 2013

Will the World Heavyweight Champion ground RVD?

Rob Van Dam comes to SmackDown tonight with one goal in mind — to rise above World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio. In a match that was announced Monday on Raw, RVD will bring the extreme to the blue brand and look to keep rolling along on his winning streak.

Since returning to WWE at Money in the Bank, the ECW Original is 3-0 in singles competition, including an impressive victory over the first-ever Undisputed WWE Champion, Chris Jericho.

Can “Mr. Monday Night” drop the titleholder, or will the devious Del Rio find a way to ground the highflier? Find out tonight on SmackDown, at 8/7 CT, only on Syfy!
