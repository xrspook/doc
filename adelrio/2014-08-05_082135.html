WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: NEW MATCHES FOR SUMMERSLAM; DO LAYLA AND SUMMER CONTINUE TO FRUSTRATE FANDANGO?; WITH FRIENDS LIKE PAIGE . . . ; JERICHO GAINS AN ADVANTAGE FOR SUMMERSLAM; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-08-01 21:55:21

We are in Corpus Christi, Texas and your announcers are Michael ‘Have you seen my Totino’s videos yet’ Cole and John ‘Cashin’ In’ Layfield.

We are back and Miz is at the announce table for the next match.

Match Number Five: Alberto Del Rio versus Dolph Ziggler

Del Rio attacks Dolph as the bell rings and he kicks Dolph. The referee warns Del Rio. Ziggler with a drop kick and elbow drop followed by punches. Ziggler with a kick and then Del Rio sends Ziggler into the air and he lands face first on the mat. Del Rio with a boot to the chest for a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock.

Ziggler with a jawbreaker and then he avoids a splash in the corner by Del Rio. Ziggler with a clothesline followed by a splash in the corner and a neck breaker. Del Rio avoids a Fameasser and Del Rio with a German suplex and bridge for a near fall. Del Rio misses a super kick and Ziggler with a hesitation DDT for a near fall.

Miz gets on the announce table and he has a mic. He wants to interrupt the match so he can thank a few more people. He thanks his mom and dad. He thanks his dogs and cats and wife.

Del Rio with a rollup for a near fall. Del Rio misses a step up enzuigiri and Ziggler with a Fameasser for a near fall.

Miz thanks Dolph for giving him the opportunity to beat him for the Intercontinental title. Miz says that Dolph isn’t as good as him.

Dolph goes after Miz but Miz goes into the crowd. Del Rio with an enzuigiri when Dolph gets on the apron. Del Rio with the cross arm breaker and Dolph taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
