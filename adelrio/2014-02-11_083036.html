WWE Live Event Results From Fresno (2/9): Cena Teases RAW Surprise, Orton Vs. Cena

By Raj Giri | February 10, 2014

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader VinDaddy for sending in these results from last night's WWE live event in San Jose, CA:

Mark Henry, Xavier Woods and Rey Mysterio defeated The Real Americans and Alberto Del Rio. Zeb Colter really is working the crowd, it was the most entertainment of the night. Pretty amazing to think only Woods and Cesaro are the only competitors in the match that are not former world heavyweight champs. Lots of fan interaction got the crowd going. Mysterio hits the 619 on Jack Swagger, followed by the Worlds Strongest Slam for the pin.
