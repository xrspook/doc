SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Feb. 28, 2014

BY Jake Grate

February 28, 2014

What will an unchained Animal say to the WWE Universe?

Moments after WWE World Heavyweight Champion Randy Orton interrupted and cost Batista his match against Alberto Del Rio on Monday’s Raw, The Face of WWE and his No. 1 contender exchanged harsh words. While The Animal guaranteed he will emerge from WrestleMania 30 with The Viper’s title, Orton instead focused on his opponent’s testy relationship with the WWE Universe.

It’s certainly true that in recent weeks, a good portion of WWE fans have taken to mercilessly booing the winner of the 2014 Royal Rumble Match, and he’s booed them right back. But that apparent disdain could be moving to a whole new level. Tonight, Batista will address the WWE Universe on SmackDown and will hopefully shed some light on the status of his relationship with fans. What, exactly, will he say tonight on SmackDown at 8/7 CT on Syfy?
