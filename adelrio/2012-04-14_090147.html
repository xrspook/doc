Alberto Del Rio makes his entrance in the Dublin arena.

Lilian Garcia introduces the next competitor's personal ring announcer.

Ricardo Rodriguez takes the microphone from Lilian Garcia to perform his ring announcing duties.

Yoshi Tatsu is excited to perform in Ireland.

Yoshi Tatsu eyes up Alberto Del Rio before their match.

Yoshi Tatsu looks on as Alberto Del Rio teases the crowd with his T-shirt, only to throw it to Ricardo at ringside.

Alberto Del Rio stomps away at Yoshi Tatsu in the corner.

Alberto Del Rio applies the cross armbreaker and makes short work of Yoshi Tatsu.

Ricardo Rodriguez raises Alberto Del Rio's hand in victory after defeating Yoshi Tatsu.

A victorious Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez mock the Dublin crowd.

Ricardo Rodriguez shushes the crowd as Alberto Del Rio prepares to speak.

Ricardo Rodriguez corrects Alberto Del Rio: they're in Ireland, not England.

Alberto Del Rio doesn't care what country he and Ricardo Rodriguez are in. Ireland, England, Scotland, Wales, it's all the same to him.

Ricardo Rodriguez addresses the crowd after they've been berated by Alberto Del Rio.

Ezekiel Jackson makes an impromptu appearance.

Alberto Del Rio teases a fan in the front row over his crowd sign.

Ezekiel Jackson is ready to do battle with Alberto Del Rio.

Ezekiel Jackson punishes Alberto Del Rio in the corner.

Ezekiel Jackson is in control of Alberto Del Rio.

Alberto Del Rio turns the tide by hitting Ezekiel Jackson with a dropkick from behind.

Alberto Del Rio takes to the air against Ezekiel Jackson, en route to a submission victory.
