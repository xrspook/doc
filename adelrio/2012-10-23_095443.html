John Laurinaitis announces the WWE Championship Match at WWE TLC

Daniel Bryan vs. Alberto Del Rio

John Cena kicks off Raw SuperShow, thanking WWE Hall of Famer "Rowdy" Roddy Piper for having him as a guest on "Piper's Pit" last week.

The leader of the Cenation addresses the WWE Universe's cheers and boos.

Cena is ready to give a message to CM Punk, that he wants the WWE Championship back.

But Alberto Del Rio interrupts and says a WWE Title Match is "mine, mine!"

Del Rio points out that last week Punk won because of the exposed turnbuckle.

The Mexican Superstar says Punk purposely and illegally cheated.

Vickie Guerrero says neither Cena or Del Rio deserve a title match.

Dolph Ziggler thinks he's the only Superstar worthy of facing CM Punk for the WWE Championship.

"Which one of you two is the longest reigning champion in the entire WWE?" the showoff asks.

Then, The Miz comes to the ring, ready to prove his worth.

"You are looking at the man who took out both R-Truth and John Morrison," The Miz says.

"You are looking at the 2011 Superstar of the Year and the next WWE Champion," The Awesome One predicts.

John Laurinaitis emerges to restore order and says there will be a contract later tonight and Ziggler, Del Rio and The Miz must compete to get into the WWE Title Match, while Cena will be part of a social experiment.
