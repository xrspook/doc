WWE Live Event Results From Cairo, Egypt (10/18) - NXT Title Defended, Sheamus Teams W/ Ryder

By Raj Giri October 18, 2012

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com readers Ahmed Gamal and Hussein Elwan for sending in these results from tonight's WWE live event in Cairo:

Sheamus and Zack Ryder defeated The Big Show and Alberto Del Rio in a tag team lumberjack match. Sheamus tried to Brogue kick Show but Show stopped Sheamus and tried to knock him out with the WMD, but Sheamus dodged the punch and Show hit Del Rio accidentally. Sheamus then Brogue kicked Show and then pinned Del Rio for the win.
