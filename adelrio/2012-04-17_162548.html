What Happened After RAW Went Off The Air, Sheamus Booed, More Off-Camera Notes

By Raj Giri | April 16, 2012

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com readers Andy Vale and Ashudeep for sending in these notes from tonight's RAW Supershow in London, England:

The dark match after RAW went off the air was CM Punk defending the WWE Championship against Chris Jericho. The match ended in a disqualification when Daniel Bryan came out to interfere while Punk was attempting to hit the GTS on Jericho. Sheamus came out and nailed Bryan with a Brogue kick.

Cody Rhodes then came out, followed by the Big Show and Alberto Del Rio. Randy Orton was out next. Bryan took off to the back as the faces nailed the remaining heels with their finishers.

The crowd was hot for the event, with "YES!" and "Daniel Bryan" chants throughout the night. The crowd popped big when Bryan dodged the Brogue kick during the show, and booed Sheamus when he kicked Bryan during the aftermath of the dark match main event.

The crowd also really liked Brock Lesnar's promo, and chanted "We want JR" during one of the breaks.

After Cena lost his match to Lord Tensai, he was helped to the back by referees as the crowd sang, "Na Na Na Na. Na Na Na Na. Hey Hey Hey. Goodbye!"

Biggest Pops:
Daniel Bryan
CM Punk
"Fish and Chips"

Most Heat:

Lo sucedido una vez Raw Supershow finalizó.

16 abril 2012 por Tinguili

Cuando Raw Supershow del día 16 de abril del presente año finalizo en su proyección en vivo. Chris Jericho y CM Punk se enfrentaron en el Dark Match Main Event y el título se encontraba en juego. Punk se encontraba a punto de aplicar una GTS a Chris Jericho pero Daniel Bryan interfirio para que la batalla terminara en DQ.

Para finalizar Cody Rhodes, Alberto del Rio, Sheamus y Randy Orton interfirieron. Punk y Chris Jericho se quedaron luchando en el ring mientras que los “babyfaces” aplicaban su finisher a los rivales.
