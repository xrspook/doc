Alberto Del Rio in Milan and Madrid photos

Alberto Del Rio stands in front of the Duomo di Milano (Milan Cathedral) in Milan, Italy.

Football fan Del Rio visits the world famous San Siro football stadium, home of AC Milan and Inter Milan.

Some of the greatest football matches in history have taken place in San Siro.

Del Rio received special access to the stadium. When full, San Siro seats more than 85,000 fans.

Here, Del Rio is loving every moment of sitting in the owner's box.

Del Rio relaxes in Paolo Maldini's seat in the AC Milan changing room. Maldini is one of Milan's all-time great players.

Taking in some of the local cuisine during a lunch break in Milan.

Del Rio makes the rounds with the Italian media, including the Sky Italia TV station, to discuss WrestleMania and WWE's upcoming international tour in April.
