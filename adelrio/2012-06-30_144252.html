WWE.com: Triple Threat World Heavyweight Title Match: photos

With every moment, the match becomes more and more intense.

Alberto Del Rio emerges ...

... declaring that he will walk out of SmackDown the World Heavyweight Champion ...

... when he battles the current titleholder Sheamus and Dolph Ziggler in a Triple Threat World Title Match.

Despite slapping Ziggler prior to the match, Vickie Guerrero emerges to introduce The Showoff.

The Great White enters the impending melee, completely unfazed by the odds.

Although Sheamus comes out swinging, things can turn around in no time in this type of match.

Del Rio and Ziggler form an alliance, but it does not last for long.

The Celtic Warrior finds his own way to seperate them.

After flattening The Showoff with the Brogue Kick for the win.

Once again, The Great White proved his SmackDown dominance.
