一大早看到这个新闻，我不寒而栗，但多看几遍，思考过后就觉得这不过是个后台政治手段而已，是一个谈合同、谈薪水、谈待遇的筹码。WWE现在如果没有Alberto Del Rio照样转，不像我这些D丝会比较桑心而已。

昨天Raw的表现和今天SD的录影事实证明，ADR的身体没有任何问题，所以，NWO的临时变阵是高层的一个下马威？

如果要终结掉ADR，直接让他在NWO上跟Sheamus来一场，最后输得一败涂地，17秒？那么就可以彻底断绝他跟神马顶级的关系了，不过呢，貌似我这个想法太正派。从现在的安排看来，用USC jobber，用IC jobber，这不是捧又是啥？天知道ADR和WWE高层脑子里到底想的是啥，可能只是要高薪水罢了。

釜底抽薪，以退为进，Alberto Del Rio已经不是第一次用这招了。2010年头，他在FCW，他希望进入到Smackdown或Raw，于是就以“辞职”为要挟，拽爆换取当年SummerSlam之后的debut。2年后，他要再来一回？狼来了两次真的可以？

Alberto Del Rio这个角色需要发展空间！我觉得这就是ADR不满他现在角色的原因。你们觉得他的promo无聊，但剧情组得给力啊，要不你让他自己想，上台直接吹西班牙语去。我经常不明白WWE高层，捧是捧了，但从来不狠狠地，不下猛药，总是软绵绵地顶一下就泄气掉，再顶一下再泄气掉……

如果WWE同期失去Randy Orton和Alberto Del Rio会怎样呢？地球照样转，不过我会长长地叹气惋惜而已。

WWE News: Problems Brewing Between Alberto Del Rio and Management?

By Sharon Glencross (Featured Columnist) on June 19, 2012

A recent report indicates that former WWE Champion Alberto Del Rio may be clashing with WWE brass, and there could be some doubt hanging over the star's future in the promotion.

In this week's F4W Newsletter, Bryan Alvarez goes into the current problems brewing between Del Rio and WWE management over the past few weeks, writing:

One source in WWE said Del Rio was frustrated with his role in the company and was willing to leave at any time. Another said it wasn't actually that big a deal, rather wrangling over a contract issue. A third source said it was probably a little of both.

Alvarez also noted that many in the company doubted the concussion injury to Del Rio. The injury led to Del Rio being pulled from his No Way Out title match against Sheamus, and Dolph Ziggler getting the spot instead.

However, in what was very strange timing, Del Rio was perfectly fine to work last night's episode of Raw, in which he took on comedy wrestler Santino Marella, crushing him in a quick squash match.

One person in WWE Monday night expressed skepticism that Del Rio ever had a concussion to begin with, noting that he was "too injured" to work No Way Out Sunday but apparently fine to work the following night on Raw. They suggested the concussion could have been a cover story for issues that came up between Del Rio and WWE that necessitated pulling him out of the match with Sheamus.

Interestingly, this is not the first report discussing Del Rio's less-than-smooth relationship with WWE. AAA wrestler and matchmaker Konnan recently talked about the star almost walking out of the company before he was ever called up to the main roster.

Del Rio, it seems, had grown frustrated with being stuck down in Florida Championship Wrestling, WWE's developmental territory, and had threatened to quit, citing financial and personal issues.

Per Konnan, there was also frustration from the WWE side towards Del Rio regarding his inability (or unwillingness) to learn a more American style of wrestling.
