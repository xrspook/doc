COMPLETE OLD SCHOOL WWE RAW REPORT: WHO GETS TO TRY TO END THE STREAK?; DOES BROCK LESNAR PLAY THE GAME?; ROCK AND CENA GET IN THEIR FIRST SHOTS; A LOT OF LEGENDS; AND EVEN SOME WRESTLING

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-03-04 22:11:49

This week’s show starts off with a GONG and it is time for the Undertaker to make his annual pre-Wrestlemania return to WWE. The Wrestlemania logo appears on the TitanTron.

We are in Buffalo, New York and your announcers are Michael ‘Will anyone mind this Brett Hull sweater’ Cole and Jerry ‘How about some Buffalo Wings to go with last week’s Sonic’ Lawler?

We are back and Josh Mathews wants to know how Jack can be so disrespectful. Jack says that this is his America. Standing up for the honor of the Americans who were taken down in the ring, it is Mexican Alberto Del Rio who attacks Jack.

Match Number Seven Wade Barrett versus Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez)

Barrett with a kick and punches. Del Rio with an Irish whip and Del Rio puts Wade in the ropes and he connects with forearms and hits a lungblower. Barrett rolls to the floor while Swagger and Colter make their way into the arena.

Del Rio rolls Barrett back into the ring and Barrett with the Black Pool Slam and he gets a near fall. Barrett with elbow drops to Del Rio and he gets a near fall. Barrett with a side head lock and Del Rio with a knee. Del Rio with an Irish whip but Barrett sends Del Rio to the apron. Barrett with a punch and he chokes Del Rio in the ropes.

Del Rio with a cross arm breaker in the ropes and he releases at four. Barrett with a boot to Del Rio to knock him off the apron. Barrett puts Del Rio over the apron and Barrett with knees to the head and then he connects with a boot to the head and Del Rio falls to the floor. Barrett with a near fall.

Barrett misses an elbow drop from the turnbuckles when Del Rio moves. Del Rio with two clotheslines and he avoids a clothesline and hits a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Del Rio sets for the super kick and he connects for a near fall.

Del Rio charges into the corner but Barrett moves. Barrett with a super kick and then he tries for a pump handle slam. Del Rio escapes and Barrett tries for another Black Pool Slam but Del Rio counters with the float over into the cross arm breaker and Barrett taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

WWE Monday Night RAW Results 3/4/13

By Marc Middleton

Mar 4, 2013 - 11:06:37 PM

Tonight's WWE RAW opens up with the old school intro and graphics. We're live from Buffalo, New York as the gong hits and The Undertaker's entrance begins.

Zeb Colter and Jack Swagger are backstage. Zeb says Swagger took out 3 Legends. Josh Matthews asks for comment but Swagger gets attacked from behind by Alberto Del Rio. They brawl and eventually are separated.

Wade Barrett vs. Alberto Del Rio

We go to the ring and WWE Intercontinental Champion Wade Barrett is in the ring. Ricardo Rodriguez begins the introduction for World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio and out he comes.

Back and forth to start. Del Rio with multiple shots to the kidney. Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter come out to the entrance way. Del Rio sends Barrett to the floor and brings him back in. Barrett catches Del Rio with Winds of Change for a 2 count. Barrett drops elbows for another pin attempt. Barrett with a headlock now. More offense from Barrett until Del Rio wraps up his arm on the ropes. Del Rio catches a big boot to the head and falls back out to the floor.

Barrett with knees and a big kick to the head. He brings it back in the ring for another 2 count. Barrett goes to the middle rope but misses an elbow drop. Del Rio with a kick to the face and a pair of clotheslines. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl backbreaker. Del Rio hits the superkick for a pin attempt. Del Rio sees Zeb and Swagger now. Del Rio misses a move and catches a big kick to the face. Barrett gets countered twice. Del Rio locks in the cross armbreaker for the win.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

We get a "Political Correctness" video from Zeb Colter and Jack Swagger. Zeb says real Americans refuse to use political correct language. In Jack Swagger's America, there will always be a group of Americans ready to defend what our forefathers fought for.
