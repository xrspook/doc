Alberto Del Rio throws a Retirement Party for Edge

Captain Charisma looks to send a strong message to Del Rio ...

Ricardo Rodriguez makes the introductions ...

... as Alberto Del Rio emerges to throw a Retirement Party "in honor " of Edge.

Del Rio describes the insulting gifts he has put together for The Rated-R Superstar ...

... including adult diapers and a grandfather clock to watch the moments of his life tick away.

He introduces a "heavier" version of former WWE Diva "Lita" for companionship.

Rodriguez rolls in on The Ultimate Opportunist's new mobility scooter ...

... complete with handicap parking pass.

But the one thing Del Rio didn't plan for was a visit from the retired Superstar himself.

The uninvited Guest of Honor gets the WWE Universe rolling with laughter ...

... as he insults his party host with one hilarious dig after another.

Del Rio soon sends Brodus Clay to take care of the 11-time World Champion.

Christian emerges from under the ring, clocking Clay with a ladder.

... just nine days before he will face him for the vacant World Heavyweight Championship ...

... in a Ladder Match at Extreme Rules.

Christian then moves to the ring, climbing the rungs of destiny to grab the hanging World Title.

Is this a sign of things to come at Extreme Rules?

Can Christian carry on the legacy of his best friend?

Or will Extreme Rules finally be Alberto Del Rio's moments of destiny?
