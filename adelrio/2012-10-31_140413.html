SPOILERS More Details From Last Night's WWE SmackDown Tapings In Fayetteville

By Marc Middleton | October 31, 2012

The backstage segment with Alberto Del Rio and Randy Orton with Matt Striker getting put face first into a cake started with Striker interviewing Del Rio when Orton attacked him. They brawled around backstage and into catering. Orton poured coffee and soup on Ricardo Rodriguez before putting Del Rio through a table. Striker then caught the cake. Orton vs. Del Rio in a Falls Count Anywhere Match was announced for the next tapings that come from England.
