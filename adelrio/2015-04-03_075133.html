Autograph Signing This Saturday in San Antonio

Ring of Honor returns to the San Antonio Shrine Auditorium for a huge national television taping this Saturday night. Prior to the beginning of the event there will be an autograph signing and we can now confirm who will take part ….

  • The Briscoes - ROH World Champion, Jay Briscoe & Mark Briscoe
  • Reborn Matt Sydal
  • International star Alberto El Patrón

Pricing is as follows ...

  • $20 for Sydal & Patrón - you will receive one autographed item and a posed photo
  • $30 for the Briscoes - you will receive one autographed item and a posed photo

Doors open at 6pm and the autograph signing will run from 6:15pm to 7:30pm.
