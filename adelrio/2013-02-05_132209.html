WWE Live Event Results From Augusta (2/3) - Fandango In Action, Del Rio Defends, More

By Raj Giri February 04, 2013

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Chris Cushman for sending in these results from last night's SmackDown live event in Augusta, GA:

Alberto Del Rio (w/ Ricardo Rodriguez) defeated Big Show in a No DQ match. This was a fan's choice match, that saw the use of Kendo sticks, chairs, and tables. Crowd favored Del Rio, although Show is popular here considering he grew up about 15 minutes away in Aiken, SC (my hometown). After the match, Del Rio led a Si chant and had Rodriguez announce him as the winner.
