WWE Press Conference

I’ve been wrestling fan since I was a little kid, so imagine my excitement when I got the ok from the PR contact at WWE that I could come to the conference. Not sure which route to take from there: I could gush about being up close and personal with my favorite wrestler, the best in the world, CM Punk. I could gush about the joy of being there in general – while yes, I was legitimately there as press, on the outside I did my job – on the inside I was squealing like a 13 yr old girl. I could go on and on about the economic impact an event like Wrestlemania has: production costs, revenue generated, taxes, jobs that get created. Or their philanthropic efforts, like their anti-bullying campaign. I could gush about how fucking nice all the wrestlers are, not that I expected them to be otherwise. As a fan, this experience gave me a glimpse at the other side of wrestling and it deepened my respect for them and what they do and what they endure to provide entertainment.
