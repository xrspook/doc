Matt Hardy vs. Alberto Del Rio

Hardy takes charge, executing a picture-perfect Side Effect.

Ushered in by his own personal ring announcer...

...Alberto Del Rio cruises into action, driving a white Bentley.

Two weeks ago, Del Rio took out Rey Mysterio with a wrist injury.

Last week, he spilled champagne all over Mysterio's friend, Christian.

This week, he squares off with another friend of Mysterio, Matt Hardy.

Hardy introduces his smiling opponent to the canvas.

Del Rio takes control of the match.

He shows why he has made such a huge impact in such a short time.

He looks to be on his way to handing his aristocratic opponent his first loss.

But, Del Rio recovers with authority.

Hurling Hardy into the ring post, Del Rio triumphs with the cross armbreaker.

When Del Rio continues his attack on Hardy after the bell, Christian emerges.

Del Rio informs Christian he will pay at a different place and time.
