ALBERTO & HARDY VS. YOUNG BUCKS AND MORE: NORTHEAST WRESTLING WRESTLEFEST RESULTS FROM WATERBURY, CT

By Mike Johnson on 2015-03-07 08:45:00

Northeast Wrestling held one of their biggest shows of the year, their annual Wrestlefest in Waterbury, CT last night. Results from the show, which drew in the 2,000-2,3000 range:

Alberto el Patron & Matt Hardy defeated The Young Bucks. It was supposed to be Hardy against Patron, but they ran an angle where the Bucks attacked Patron and Hardy made the save. I was told the bout was changed in part, due to Patron having an ankle injury. Hardy did the lion's share of the work here and well, it was The Young Bucks show, so if you like what they do, you probably would have liked this match.
