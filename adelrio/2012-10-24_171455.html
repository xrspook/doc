John Morrison & Mark Henry vs. Sheamus & Alberto Del Rio

Momentum for his team is literally inches from The Shaman of Sexy's reach.

With John Morrison in his corner, Mark Henry sizes up his tag team bout foe.

The World's Strongest Man tussles with Sheamus then blasts Alberto Del Rio.

But The High King of WWE delivers a highly effective boot to Henry's head.

King Sheamus maintains control of the tag bout, this time against Morrison.

Though a (massive) house on fire, Henry is extinguished.

Equals in underhandedness, Sheamus & Del Rio make a suitable match.

Del Rio manages to capture Henry in his painful cross armbar submission.
