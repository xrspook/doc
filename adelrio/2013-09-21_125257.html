Every 30 Years of WrestleMania Match!

By Aubrey Sitterson on Sep 16

If you’ve been paying any kind of attention, you know that the focus of WWE 2K14 is our all-new 30 Years of WrestleMania Mode, highlighting the best matches from the entire storied history of the Greatest Spectacle in Entertainment.

Check back here every day between now and Friday, as we unveil the full match list for each of the mode’s five eras, along with a screenshot for every one of these iconic contests.

And obviously, if you haven’t already, make sure to pre-order WWE 2K14 to make sure you receive the Ultimate Warrior pre-order bonus character when the game comes out on October 29 in North America, November 1 internationally.
