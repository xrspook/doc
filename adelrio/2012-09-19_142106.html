Main Events Revealed For WWE's European Tour

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 09.18.2012

WWE's upcoming European tour that runs from October 30th through November 5th has the following main events and appearances advertised:

Raw shows:

  • Anything Goes WWE Title Match: CM Punk vs. John Cena
  • US Title Match: Antonio Cesaro vs. Santino Marella
  • Also appearing: Daniel Bryan, Kane, Tensai, Brodus Clay, Zack Ryder, AJ Lee, Layla, Eve

Smackdown shows:

  • World Title Triple Threat Match: Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio vs. The Big Show
  • Fan's Choice Match: Dolph Ziggler vs. Randy Orton
  • Also appearing: Rey Mysterio, The Miz, Kofi Kingston, R-Truth, Cody Rhodes, Wade Barrett, Sin Cara, Natalya
