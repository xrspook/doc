WWE Live Event Results From Springfield (3/29): Who Teamed W/ Cena & Sheamus?, Ryback - Henry

By Raj Giri March 30, 2013

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Matthew Bailey for sending in these results from last night's WWE live event in Springfield, MA:

Alberto Del Rio defeated U.S. Champion Antonio Cesaro via disqualification after Jack Swagger came out to attack Del Rio. Kofi Kingston came out to Del Rio's rescue. Vickie Guerrero came out and made Del Rio and Kofi vs. Swagger and Cesaro.

Alberto Del Rio & Kofi Kingston defeated Antonio Cesaro and Jack Swagger.
