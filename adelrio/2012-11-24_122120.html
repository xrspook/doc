WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: MIZTV TRIES TO GET TO THE BOTTOM OF THE AJ/CENA SITUATION . . . BUT THIS ONE IS BOTTOMLESS; SHEAMUS GETS TO WATCH A HANDICAP MATCH; A MATCH IS ANNOUNCED FOR TLC; INTERCONTINENTAL TITLE MATCH; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-11-23 21:59:58

Previously on As the WWE Turns, Vickie Guerrero did her best to try to expose the relationship that does not exist between AJ Lee and John Cena . . . or is there a relationship? CM Punk has lasted an entire year as the WWE Champion. Did the NXT Three enter into a working relationship with CM Punk and Paul Heyman or do they have an ulterior motive? Big Show used the referee as a human shield to avoid the Brogue Kick at Survivor Series. What is next for these two? Randy Orton and Alberto Del Rio are not done either.

This week, Smackdown is in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Your announcers are John ‘Godzilla versus King Kong’ Layfield and Josh ‘Why do I have to dress up like Fay Wray, I look more like Jessica Lange or Naomi Watts’ Mathews.

Match Number Three: Sin Cara versus Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez)

Del Rio with a kick to the leg followed by a snap mare and kick to the back. Del Rio goes for a splash but Cara moves and Del Rio goes through the ropes. Cara with a suicide dive onto Del Rio and Ricardo. Cara with a kick to Del Rio’s back. Cara gets a near fall. Cara misses a running kick to the head but Del Rio does not miss with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall.

Cara bounces off the ropes and hits an arm drag. Del Rio sends Cara shoulder first into the post. We go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio with a rear chin lock. Cara with a rollup for a near fall but Del Rio with a kick and he sends Cara into the turnbuckles. Del Rio with kicks to the back followed by a step up enzuigiri to the back for a near fall. Cara with a rana for a near fall. Del Rio with a running boot to the head. Del Rio with a suplex and a near fall.

Del Rio misses a splash into the corner and both men are down. Del Rio with a take down and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with head butts but Cara with a spinning arm drag to Del Rio. Cara is sent to the apron and he hits an enzuigiri. Cara with a springboard cross body for a near fall. Cara hits a springboard back elbow and then he goes up top. Del Rio with an arm drag from the turnbuckles and then he floats over into the cross arm breaker and Cara taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Ricardo Rodriguez goes after Orton and Orton with an RKO. Alberto stays on the apron.

Meanwhile, on the stage, John Cena attacks Ziggler from behind and applies the STF. Officials try to separate Cena and Ziggler as we go to credits.
