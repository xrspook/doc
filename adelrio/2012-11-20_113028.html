COMPLETE WWE RAW REPORT: HAPPY PUNKIVERSARY; AJ AND JOHN CENA GIVE VICKIE SOME EVIDENCE; TWO OUT OF THREE FALLS; SHEAMUS WANTS A PIECE OF BIG SHOW AND HE HAS THE CHAIR TO PROVE IT; JOHN CENA HAS AN ACHILLES HEEL; AND ROLLINS, AMBROSE, AND REIGNS MAKE AN IMPACT

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-11-19 20:06:22

Previously, in the WWE . . . Did Big Show successfully defend the title or did Sheamus win back his title? Is Scott Armstrong okay? Is CM Punk up to Day 365? Who were the Men in Black who attacked Ryback? What about Vickie and her allegations against AJ Lee?

Tonight’s Raw comes to us from Dayton, Ohio and your announcers are Michael ‘Is this the Age of the Fall’ Cole and Jerry ‘I hope William Regal is okay because I don’t want to face Dean Ambrose’ Lawler.

Match Number Five: Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) versus Randy Orton in a Two Out of Three Falls Match

Fall Number One

Orton blocks a punch and he connects with a few punches. Del Rio with an Irish whip but you don’t do that to Orton because he comes out of the corner with a clothesline. Del Rio with kicks and a snap mare followed by a kick and Del Rio gets a near fall. Orton with a standing drop kick for a near fall.

Orton with a European uppercut but Del Rio sends Orton into the turnbuckles. Del Rio with a suplex. Orton with a kick and forearm to the back followed by a suplex of his own. Del Rio works on Orton’s left arm. Del Rio tries to float over but Orton sends Del Rio to the floor. Del Rio pulls Orton’s shoulder into the rope and then he wrings it into the ring post a few times. The referee makes the five count and he has been disqualified.

Winner of Fall: Randy Orton One Fall to None

We go to commercial.

Fall Number Two

The fall is joined in progress and Del Rio with a back heel kick and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with an arm bar. Orton with a head butt and a belly-to-back suplex. Orton with two clotheslines and then he avoids a clothesline and he hits a power slam. Orton looks around and he tries to send Del Rio to the apron but Del Rio applies the cross arm breaker and Orton taps.

Winner of Fall: Alberto Del Rio One Fall Each

Fall Number Three

The referee checks on Orton and Alberto is told something by Ricardo before the fall starts. Orton is on defense and then they lock up. Del Rio backs Orton into the ropes. Orton with a rollup for a near fall. Del Rio with a kick and then he wrings the injured arm into the mat. Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri into the arm and Del Rio gets a near fall.

Orton with a back breaker to Del Rio. Ricardo trips Orton and the referee sends Ricardo to the back. Del Rio with a lungblower for a near fall. Del Rio twists to the mat and he mocks Orton. Del Rio tries for an RKO but Orton avoids it and Orton sends Del Rio to the apron and hits the IEDDT. Orton twists to the mat but he remembers that his left arm is injured. Orton tries for the RKO but Del Rio blocks it and he floats over for the cross arm breaker and after Orton fights to prevent it from being applied, Del Rio applies it. Orton rolls Del Rio over for a near fall. Del Rio misses the step up enzuigiri and Orton hits the RKO for the three count.

Winner of Fall: Randy Orton

Winner: Randy Orton (Two Falls to One)
