WWE.com: Week in Photos, May 26, 2012

As Del Rio celebrates with Ricardo on the ramp, Sheamus attacks with a clear message: "I can’t wait to kick your head off at No Way Out!"

World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, Alberto Del Rio, Chris Jericho and Randy Orton compete in an epic Fatal 4-Way Title Match at WWE Over the Limit!

Sheamus is locked in The Walls of Jericho!

Del Rio breaks up the cover attempt.

The Great White connects with a flying shoulder off the top rope!

United States Champion Santino Marella gets into a verbal spat with Alberto Del Rio’s personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez as the two duel over who can roll their "R"s better.

Randy Orton and Alberto Del Rio lock up in the center of the ring, and The Mexican Aristocrat targets The Viper's shoulder.

When The Viper appears to have things in hand, Chris Jericho appears out of nowhere and hits three consecutive Codebreakers on Orton!

In Raw's first-ever 2-on-3 Handicap Lumberjack Match, John Cena & Sheamus face off against Tensai, Dolph Ziggler & Jack Swagger.

When the lumberjacks storm the ring, chaos ensues!

The Mexican Aristocrat arrives to tell Eve that "There’s no one better than Alberto Del Rio" to be the No. 1 contender to Sheamus' World Heavyweight Championship ...

... But Randy Orton disagrees.

So, too, does Kane. The Devil's Favorite Demon reminds Eve that tonight's show episode No. 666 of SmackDown!

Eve schedules a Triple Threat No. 1 Contender's Match for later in the night to determine who will face Sheamus at No Way Out!

In the night’s unlikeliest bout, United States Champion Santino Marella takes on Ricardo Rodriguez!

Santino toys with Alberto Del Rio’s personal ring announcer, but after both men competitors lose their balance, things get weird.

Santino hits the Cobra and it’s all over!

Fighting for a championship match against Sheamus at No Way Out, Orton takes it to Del Rio!

Kane chokeslams Del Rio ...

The Viper goes for the pin, but Del Rio breaks the cover, sneaks in and pins Kane to win the bout and No. 1 contendership!
