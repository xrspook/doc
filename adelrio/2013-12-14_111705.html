SmackDown Results: Orton apologized to Triple H, looking for a little TLC support

BY Michael Burdick

December 13, 2013

Kofi Kingston vs. Alberto Del Rio ended in a No Contest

After being savagely attacked by Alberto Del Rio in a pre-match assault one week ago, Kofi Kingston returned to the blue brand, looking for retribution against the Essence of Excellence.

But The Wildcat would again be engaged before the bell, as longstanding rival The Miz blasted Kingston with a Skull Crushing Finale onto the ringside floor.
