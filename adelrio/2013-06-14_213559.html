SmackDown Five-Point Preview: June 14, 2013

BY Jake Grate

June 14, 2013

Will The Showoff get tripped up before his pay-per-view showcase?

After being out of action for more than a month due to a concussion, World Heavyweight Champion Dolph Ziggler returns to compete tonight on SmackDown, teaming up with his “heavy,” Big E Langston, against Chris Jericho & Alberto Del Rio.

With an extremely difficult first title defense against Del Rio at WWE Payback looming, will this tag team showdown be a momentum-gaining “warm-up” match for The Showoff? Or will “ring rust” and the lingering effects of his injury leave him reeling against his No. 1 contender and Y2J – another dangerous Superstar gearing up for a huge match against a returning CM Punk this Sunday?
