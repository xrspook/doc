WWE SUPERSTARS REPORT: BATTLE FOR MEXICAN SUPREMACY; FANDANGO VERSUS ZIGGLER; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-03-13 16:00:09

We are in Little Rock, Arkansas and your announcers are Tom ‘Terrific’ Phillips and Byron ‘You might remember me from the Raw Pre Game Show’ Saxton.

Match Number One: Sin Cara versus Alberto Del Rio

Alberto might have his Mexican flags in the ring posts, but Sin Cara gets his lighting as the match begins.

They lock up and Del Rio with a side head lock and shoulder tackle. Cara tries for a leap frog but Del Rio stops short and Del Rio with a punch followed by kicks. Del Rio with an Irish whip but Cara with a head scissors and drop kick that sends Del Rio to the floor. Cara with a suicide dive onto Del Rio. Cara sends Del Rio into the apron and then he sends Del Rio back into the ring.

Cara with a slingshot hesitation baseball slide. Del Rio sends Cara’s legs into the ropes and Cara with a double stomp to Cara. Del Rio with a suplex for a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock but Cara with punches. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio returns to the reverse chin lock.

Del Rio sends Cara into the turnbuckles and he connects with shoulders. Del Rio runs into an elbow for Cara and Cara with a tornado DDT and both men are down. Cara with a head scissors followed by a handspring back elbow for a near fall. Cara goes to the apron and Del Rio misses a splash. Cara with an enzuigiri and then he goes up top.

Cara misses a senton from the turnbuckles but Del Rio with a super kick for the three count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
