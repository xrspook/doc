WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: A TRIBUTE TO MAE YOUNG; PUNK VERSUS THE WORLD; NUMBER CONTENDER DETERMINED?

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-01-17 22:00:20

We begin with a graphic In Memory of Mae Young.

We are in Worcester, Massachusetts and your announcers are Michael ‘3 Day Weekend’ Cole, John ‘Did you see me on Varney & Company this morning’ Layfield, and Miz.

We are back and Alberto Del Rio is in the ring and we take a look back at what happened on Raw between Rey Mysterio and Alberto Del Rio.

Match Number Two: Alberto Del Rio versus Rey Mysterio

Before the match, Rey gets on the mic and he says that Alberto got the best of him on Raw, but Alberto is worried about Batista returning to Raw. Rey says that Alberto should be worried about him tonight.

Del Rio kicks Rey and then he wraps the arm in the rope before the referee warns him. Del Rio misses a splash into the ropes when Rey moves. Del Rio goes to the floor and Rey with a kick and a sliding splash onto Del Rio on the floor. Rey sends Del Rio into the ring and then Rey with a shoulder and forearm. Rey goes for a springboard move but Del Rio hits Rey in the legs and Rey gets caught in the turnbuckles.

Del Rio with a running kick to Rey and we go to commercial.

Del Rio with a snap mare and kick to the back for a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Del Rio with a body scissors on Rey. Rey punches Del Rio in the leg to get out of the hold. Del Rio blocks a kick but Rey with an enzuigiri and Del Rio falls into the ropes. He suckers Rey into trying for the 619 and Del Rio with a clothesline for a near fall.

Del Rio punches Rey. Del Rio puts Rey on the turnbuckles and he goes for the mask and we can see part of Rey’s face. Del Rio puts Rey in the tree of woe again. Del Rio goes for a shoulder but Rey moves and Del Rio hits the post with his shoulder. Rey goes up top and Rey with a senton for a near fall.

Del Rio with a running kick into the corner and then he hits a KneeDT for a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Rey hits Del Rio in the head but Del Rio sends Rey into the turnbuckles. Del Rio puts Rey on the turnbuckles for a superplex but Rey blocks it and he sends Del Rio to the mat. Rey with a seated splash followed by a head scissors that sends Del Rio into the ropes but Del Rio escapes the ropes and hits a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall.

Del Rio with a head butt and he tries for a sunset flip but Rey rolls through and Rey with a kick to the head for a near fall. Rey goes to the turnbuckles but Del Rio with an enzuigiri and Rey falls to the mat again. Del Rio with a near fall. Del Rio looks around and signals for the cross arm breaker. Del Rio floats over but Rey escapes. Rey with a kick and tornado DDT for a near fall.

Del Rio with an arm breaker and he floats over but Rey floats over. Rey with a head scissors and Del Rio falls into the ropes and Rey hits the 619. Rey goes for the dive from the turnbuckles but Del Rio gets his knees up. Del Rio sets for the super kick but Rey ducks and Rey with a rollup for the three count.

Winner: Rey Mysterio

After the match, Del Rio attacks Rey and kicks him. Del Rio floats over into the cross arm breaker and Rey taps while the referee tries to get Del Rio to release the hold. Del Rio takes his time releasing the hold but he eventually lets go.
