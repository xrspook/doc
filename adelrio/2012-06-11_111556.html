WWE.com: Week in Photos, June 09, 2012

With just nine days to go until WWE No Way Out, No. 1 contender for the World Heavyweight Championship Alberto Del Rio kicks off SmackDown with some choice words for Sheamus.

But The Celtic Warrior has little time to celebrate ...

... As his No Way Out opponent, Alberto Del Rio, attacks him on the stage.

Del Rio locks in an excruciating Cross Armbreaker on the stage.

But when The Great White’s music plays, things get a little strange as Ricardo Rodriguez enters, dressed as The Celtic Warrior!

The real Great White comes out, and he’s not happy. Sheamus clears the ring, but thanks to some help from Ricardo, Del Rio gets the better of his No Way Out foe.

Per Mr. Laurinaitis, who isn’t at SmackDown, as he’s busy preparing for his job evaluation from Mr. McMahon on Monday ...

... In the spirit of "fairness, excitement and most of all, 'People Power,'" two matches have been made for the night. First up, The Great Khali takes on Alberto Del Rio!

A timely distraction from Ricardo leads to Del Rio taking the advantage in the bout.

The Mexican Aristocrat locks in the Cross Armbreaker, to which Khali taps out!

Ricardo rushes into the ring, causing a disqualification, as Del Rio looks on from the ramp.

Ricardo meets a similar fate, as Sheamus sends a message to a seething Del Rio.
