WWE Raw 2013-04-29 非官方战报

作者: xrspook

日期: 2013-04-30 10:50:30+08:00

分类: 点点文本

标签: alberto_del_rio, wwe, 2013, raw, ricardo_rodriguez, 战报, jack_swagger, zeb_colter, dolph_ziggler, big_e_langston, aj, antonio_cesaro

COMPLETE WWE RAW REPORT: WORLD TITLE MATCH STIPULATION; CAN RYBACK TRUST JOHN CENA?; TUG OF WAR; DANCE OFF; THE SHIELD IN ACTION; SOMEONE HAS A SECRET ADMIRER (BUT IT IS NOTHING LIKE NXT'S ANGLE); AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-04-29 20:04:47

Now that the WWE is back in the United States, what is next on the road to Extreme Rules? After defeating the Tag Team Champions and the Undertaker, what is next for The Shield? What will happen between Ryback and John Cena? What is next for Triple H and Brock Lesnar? Will Teddy Long make any decisions when he has no authority?

We are in Columbus, Ohio and your announcers are Jerry ‘Two Hours from The Jake’ Lawler, John ‘We do a lot of talking on Monday nights’ Layfield, and Michael ‘Is Tonight The Raw is War’ Cole.

Match Number One Ricardo Rodriguez (with Zubaz and Alberto Del Rio) versus Big E Langston (with Dolph Ziggler and AJ Lee) versus Zeb Colter (with Jack Swagger) in a Winner gets to allow their person to pick the stipulation for the World Title Match at Extreme Rules

Colter goes to the apron when the bell rings. Langston backs Ricardo into the corner and Langston with a shoulder tackle. Langston with a forearm to Ricardo’s chest. Ricardo with punches and kicks but Langston with three back breakers to Ricardo. Langston with punches to the midsection.

Langston with a running shoulder into the corner and then he rolls Ricardo to the corner. Zeb tags in and Langston charges into the ring post. Ricardo with punches and he hits a drop kick. Colter with punches to Langston.

Ricardo yells at Colter and he wants a piece of Colter while the fans chant USA. Langston with a clothesline to Ricardo followed by a punch and knees to the midsection. Colter pushes Langston to the side and he punches Ricardo.

Zeb claims to have hurt his knee and Swagger checks on it. Ricardo is given the bucket by Alberto and Langston does not feel it. Ricardo throws it at Langston and Langston with a belly-to-belly suplex.

The straps come down and Alberto with an enzuigiri to Langston. Ziggler with a drop kick to Alberto. Swagger sends Ziggler over the top rope to the floor. Langston clotheslines Swagger over the top. Colter covers Ricardo and AJ breaks up the cover. Ricardo with a rollup on Colter for the three count.

Winner: Ricardo Rodriguez

Match Number Six: Antonio Cesaro versus Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez)

They lock up and go to a stalemate. They lock up again and Cesaro with a wrist lock and Del Rio with a reversal and arm bar. Del Rio with a hammer lock into a side head lock. Del Rio with a shoulder tackle for a near fall. Del Rio with punches to Cesaro followed by punches. Cesaro blocks a suplex and he connects with a suplex of his own.

Del Rio with a shoulder and kick in the corner. Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri to the arm and he punches Cesaro in the arm and he continues with the arm bar. Cesaro pulls Del Rio down by the hair and then he applies an arm bar. Cesaro pulls the tights and takes Del Rio back to the mat.

Cesaro with European uppercuts but Del Rio with a head scissors take down. Del Rio puts Cesaro in the ropes and he connects with forearms to the kidneys followed by a lungblower. Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker but Cesaro escapes and he goes to the floor.

We go to commercial.

We are back and Cesaro with a chin lock on Del Rio. We see footage of Cesaro with a back drop driver onto the apron. Del Rio with a punch and he gets a near fall with a sunset flip but Cesaro with a leaping double stomp for a near fall. Cesaro with a dead lift side salto for a near fall. Cesaro with a running European uppercut into the corner for a near fall. Cesaro with another near fall.

Cesaro with a rear chin lock on Del Rio. Cesaro sends Del Rio face first into the mat and then he returns to the rear chin lock. Cesaro sends Del Rio sternum first into the turnbuckles and then he hits a belly-to-back suplex despite Del Rio’s attempts to punch his way out of the hold.

Cesaro with European uppercuts but Del Rio with a kick and two clotheslines. Cesaro misses a clothesline and Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Del Rio has a super kick blocked and Cesaro with a near fall on an inside cradle. Del Rio with a German suplex and bridge for a near fall.

Cesaro sends Del Rio to the apron but Del Rio with a punch and then he goes for a double sledge from the turnbuckles but Cesaro with a European uppercut for a near fall. Cesaro misses a running shoulder into the corner and he hits the post. Del Rio with a super kick for a near fall. Del Rio tries for the cross arm breaker but Cesaro counters with a rollup but Del Rio is able to turn it into a cross arm breaker and he gets Cesaro to tap out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Alberto gets the mic. He says that thanks to his compadre, he can select the stipulation for the World Title Match at Extreme Rules. It is going to be a ladder match.
