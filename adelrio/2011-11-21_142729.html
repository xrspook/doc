CM Punk vs. Alberto Del Rio – WWE Championship Match

Alberto Del Rio's personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez, introduces the WWE Champion.

CM Punk comes prepared with a ring announcer of his own: WWE Hall of Famer Howard Finkel.

"30 Rock's" Judah Friedlander has a front row seat for the all the Survivor Series action.

The Survivor Series showdown is Punk's one-on-one rematch ...

... from when Del Rio cashed in his Money in the Bank briefcase at Summer Slam.

Neither "The Voice of the Voiceless" nor the WWE Universe have any love for the Mexican aristocrat.

But, just because Del Rio isn't cheered, that doesn't mean he's not dangerous.

The WWE Champion brings the pain, hoping to soften up his prey for the Cross Armbreaker.

When he finally locks in his signature finisher, it looks like it could be over.

Luckily, Punk is able to think on his feet.

Punk drops the big elbow off the top rope.

The Second City Saint attempts to execute the GTS on the WWE Champion.

Rodriguez gets up on the apron, but the challenger gives him the boot.

Punk locks in the Anaconda Vise and the WWE Universe is on the edge of their seats.

Del Rio tries to escape but has no choice but to tap out.

CM Punk in the new WWE Champion!

Punk celebrates winning his second WWE Title with the WWE Universe in Madison Square Garden.
