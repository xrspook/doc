WWE.com: Mr. McMahon celebrates the 20th Anniversary of Raw: photos

Harkening back to Jan. 11, 1993, WWE Chairman Mr. McMahon opens the 20th anniversary episode of Raw with a thunderous intro: "Welcome everyone to the 20th Anniversary of Monday Night Raw!"

After joking that he deserves full credit for Raw's success, Mr. McMahon says WWE's flagship program has stood the test of time thanks entirely to the WWE Universe.

After announcing that John Cena and Dolph Ziggler will compete in a Steel Cage Match tonight, the WWE Chairman is cut off by Big Show.

The World's Largest Athlete claims SmackDown General Manager Booker T wronged him by scheduling a World Heavyweight Championship Last Man Standing Match against Alberto Del Rio on short notice.

Noting his no-cut, ironclad contract, Show says the situation on last Friday's SmackDown must be addressed, and he suggests stripping Del Rio of the World Heavyweight Title.

Led by Ricardo Rodriguez, Albert Del Rio makes his way to the ring.

"Big Show, for someone who talks about being a giant so much, you come off very small," the new World Heavyweight Champion says.

The Mexican Aristocrat then challenges Big Show to a title rematch tonight, much to the approval of the loyal WWE Universe in Houston. Show refuses, explaining the rematch will be on his terms — at Royal Rumble on Jan. 27.

After Big Show threatens to knock out Del Rio at Royal Rumble, Ricardo gets under the giant’s skin by tossing red, white and green confetti on him.

A brawl ensues ...

... with Del Rio getting the better of it.
