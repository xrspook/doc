WWE.com: Teddy Long's Top 10 Tag Team Matches

Edge found himself at a disadvantage when Alberto Del Rio and Brodus Clay cornered him in the ring.

The Nexus made a rare appearance on SmackDown in 2010, interrupting a contest between Edge and Alberto Del Rio.

SmackDown's Superstars rushed to their colleagues' aide.

Teddy Long informed The Nexus that they would be taking on Edge, Del Rio, Kane, Big Show and Rey Mysterio in a huge 10-Man Tag Team Match.

The Nexus took Rey Mysterio out of action before the match. He was replaced by Kofi Kingston.

Edge won the match for the SmackDown squad after taking out David Otunga with a Spear.

After Christian evened the odds, Teddy Long thought it would be a good time for Edge and Captain Charisma to team up once again.

Edge & Christian showed they hadn't lost a step in the 10 years since their last team-up, defeating The Mexican Aristocrat and his massive bodyguard.
