3/9 WWE RESULTS FROM JONESBORO, ARKANSAS

By Brian Thompson on 2014-03-09 22:56:00

I just got home from the WWE house show in Jonesboro, AR this evening. Here are the results.

WWE ran the Arkansas State University Convocation Center in Jonesboro, AR Sunday evening with a crowd in the 6,000 range. Here are the results:

Dolph Ziggler defeated Alberto Del Rio.

Overall, a really hot crowd all night.

No CM Punk chants or at least any that were audible if there were.

Cena was over HUGE here. Bryan was next as far as a loud pop with Orton and the Usos next.

Good heat on Batista for his turn against Cena in the main just like MSG. Fair reaction when he first came out.

Lots of kids in the audience.

SECOND 3/9 WWE IN JONESBORO, ARKANSAS LIVE REPORT

By James Cohorn on 2014-03-10 08:02:00

WWE 3/9 Jonesboro, Arkansas report:

Alberto Del Rio vs. Dolph Ziggler- They fought out of the ring and up the ramp. The came back down to ringside and Ziggler was slammed into the guardrail by Ziggler. The action returned to the ring. The match went back and forth and was hard fought. Ziggler wins with the Zig-Zag.

Notes: I would estimate the crowd at around 3,000....This was pretty much the same show as at MSG.....Cena received the biggest ovation & then Daniel Bryan.
