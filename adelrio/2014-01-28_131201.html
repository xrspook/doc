WWE Raw 2014-01-27 非官方战报

作者: xrspook

日期: 2014-01-28 13:12:01+08:00

分类: 点点文本

标签: alberto_del_rio, wwe, 2014, raw, kofi_kingston, ppv, royal_rumble, batista, 战报

WWE RAW REPORT: FOUR MEN ANNOUNCED FOR ELIMINATION CHAMBER MATCH; NEW INDUCTEE ANNOUNCED FOR HALL OF FAME; WHAT TO DO WITH SIX MEN WHO DON'T LIKE EACH OTHER IN FEBRUARY?; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-01-27 23:01:34

In case you missed the Royal Rumble, we saw Bray Wyatt set his sights on John Cena, giving him Sister Abigail after Cena’s loss to Randy Orton.

We are in Cleveland, Ohio and your announcers are Jerry ‘How many days until pitchers and catchers report’ Lawler, John ‘Winking Lizard’ Layfield, and Michael ‘Did you know that the WWE Network is coming in 4 weeks . . . we have instructions on the app’ Cole.

Alberto Del Rio is in the Goldman Box. He says that Batista will not make it to Wrestlemania.

Match Number Five: Kofi Kingston versus Alberto Del Rio

They lock up and Del Rio backs Kofi into the corner and he kicks and punches Kofi in the corner. Del Rio with a snap mare and kick to the head. Del Rio chokes Kofi in the ropes. Kofi with kicks to Del Rio followed by punches. Kofi with a drop kick. Kofi leaps into the corner and he punches Del Rio before rolling back to the mat. Del Rio with boots to Kofi and Kofi goes down.

Del Rio with a clothesline for a near fall. Del Rio with a forearm to Kofi but Del Rio misses a splash into the ropes and Del Rio goes to the floor. Kofi with a suicide dive onto Del Rio. Kofi sends Del Rio into the ring and Del Rio with an enzuigiri to Kofi while Kofi is on the apron. Del Rio pulls Kofi to the floor and he sends Kofi into the ringside barrier. Del Rio gets a near fall.

Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Kofi with punches but Del Rio pulls Kofi to the mat by the hair. Del Rio with a double sledge from the turnbuckles and then he kicks Kofi. Del Rio sends Kofi into the turnbuckles. Del Rio punches Kofi and the crowd chants for JBL and JBL acknowledges them and Del Rio yells at JBL.

Del Rio and Kofi fight on the turnbuckles and both men fall off the turnbuckles. We go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio with a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. We see footage during the break when Del Rio hit a step up enzuigiri to the back of the head. Back to live action and Kofi with a jawbreaker and sunset flip for a near fall. Del Rio with a German suplex for a near fall.

Del Rio looks at the Wrestlemania sign and he charges into an elbow from Kofi followed by a kick. Kofi with a tornado DDT. Kofi with chops and a drop kick but Del Rio with a kick. Kofi with a rollup for a near fall. Kofi with a flying clothesline and then he hits the Boom Drop.

Kofi sets for Trouble in Paradise but Del Rio moves. Del Rio goes to the apron and then Kofi goes for SOS and hits it but Del Rio gets his foot on the rope to stop the count. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Del Rio points to the sign and he signals for the cross arm breaker. Del Rio tires for the float over and Kofi counters with an attempted Trouble in Paradise. Del Rio ducks and hits a lungblower for a near fall.

Del Rio goes up top and Kofi crotches Del Rio. They fight on the turnbuckles while Kofi goes for a superplex but Kofi gets caught in the ropes and Del Rio with a double stomp. Del Rio sets for the super kick and he connects. Del Rio gets the three count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
