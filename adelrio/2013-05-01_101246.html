SPOILERS Ongoing SmackDown Results For This Friday

By Raj Giri | April 30, 2013

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Jennifer McCallister for sending in these results from tonight's SmackDown taping in Detroit, MI:

Ricardo Rodriguez cut a promo backstage about his victory on RAW.

Zeb Colter vs. Ricardo Rodriguez is next. Jack Swagger interferes, and then Alberto Del Rio gets involved. Teddy Long comes out and makes it a tag team match.

Zeb Colter & Jack Swagger vs. Alberto Del Rio & Ricardo Rodriguez is next. Team Ziggler is at ringside and interferes. It is now a triple threat tag team match.

Zeb Colter & Jack Swagger vs. Alberto Del Rio & Ricardo Rodriguez vs. Big E. Langston & Dolph Ziggler is up now. Del Rio and Rodriguez got the win via submission.

Complete WWE Smackdown Taping Results (SPOILERS)

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 04.30.2013

Renee Young interviewed Ricardo, who spoke really fast in Spanish. Alberto Del Rio calmed him down but I didn't really pick up what they were talking about.

Ricardo Rodriguez vs. Zeb Colter ended in a no-contest when Jack Swagger interfered. Alberto Del Rio cleared Swagger of the ring and Teddy Long came out and..... You guessed it, he made a tag team match.

Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez vs. Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter ended in a no-contest. Ziggler and Big E interfered. Teddy came out and made it a Triple Threat tag team match.

Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez defeated Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter, and Dolph Ziggler and Big E Langston in a Triple Threat tag match. Del Rio made Ziggler tap to the Cross Armbreaker. Good match.
