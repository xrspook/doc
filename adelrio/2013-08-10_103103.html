WWE.com: Christian vs. Alberto Del Rio: photos

A furious Del Rio argues he kicked out before the three-count, and when he tries to storm the ring, Christian levels him with the Killswitch!

A mere nine days before their title match at SummerSlam, Christian and World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio lock horns in an intense non-title contest.

Christian slides under the ropes and goes for a kick on the World Champion, as the action spills over to ringside.

Del Rio shows off his intensity and brutality on Captain Charisma.

Christian shows some of his high-flying moves off, as he leaps off the top rope.

The World Heavyweight Champion brutalizes his No. 1 contender with vicious kicks to the midsection.

Del Rio drops Christian with a massive kick to the head, but he can't put Captain Charisma away.

Del Rio nails a backstabber in the back-and-forth battle.

Christian gets the best of the Mexican titleholder — rolling Del Rio up for the huge pinfall victory.

With the World Heavyweight Champion downed, "Mr. Money in the Bank" Damien Sandow suddenly emerges with his briefcase in hand. But before he can cash his contract in ...

... his former best friend Cody Rhodes appears out of nowhere and goes off the top rope, flooring Sandow!

Rhodes nails "Mr. Money in the Bank" with Cross Rhodes, which gives Del Rio time to recover and ends any chance at Sandow cashing in.

With all four of these fiery Superstars battling at SummerSlam in nine days, are Friday night's wild events a sign of things to come?
