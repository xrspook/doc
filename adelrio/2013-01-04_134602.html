WWE's 10th annual "Tribute to the Troops"

The Norfolk Scope played host to more than 7,500 service members and their families at World Wrestling Entertainment’s (WWE) 10th annual “Tribute to the Troops,” Dec. 9. The event was the culmination of four days of special visits by the WWE Superstars, Divas and Legends to military installations throughout Hampton Roads.
