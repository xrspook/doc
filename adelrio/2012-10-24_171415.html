Mark Henry vs. Ted DiBiase - Over-the-Top Rope Challenge

Will the Mexican aristocrat win the biggest Royal Rumble Match in history?

A furious Ted DiBiase issues an open challenge to the Raw locker room.

The "Fortunate Son" says he can throw anyone over the top rope.

The massive Mark Henry gladly accepts DiBiase's challenge.

The World's Strongest Man makes short work of the trust-funded Superstar.

The exciting Yoshi Tatsu enters the fray, but is turned back by Henry.

William Regal and Tyson Kidd team up to topple Henry.

R-Truth, Primo, David Hart Smith and Zack Ryder hit the ring as well.

Exiled former Nexus member Darren Young also joins the melee.

Sheamus sends Smith and R-Truth flying over the top rope.

Henry tosses The Celtic Warrior through the ropes, but not over them.

Alberto Del Rio makes yet another stylish entrance.
