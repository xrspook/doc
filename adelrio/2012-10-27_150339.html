CM Punk & Alberto Del Rio vs. The Miz & R-Truth

Superstars at the Solidarity Rally before Raw SuperShow

WWE Champion Alberto Del Rio arrives in style in his BMW 6-Series convertible.

The Mexican Superstar will face CM Punk in the Raw SuperShow main event.

The Straightedge Superstar has had a score to settle with Del Rio since WWE Hell in a Cell.

Del Rio defeated Punk in a Triple Threat Match at WWE Hell in a Cell to win the WWE Championship.

Raw's new Interim General Manager John Laurinaitis interrupts the match, telling the competitors to stop.

Laurinaitis announces he's re-hired The Miz & R-Truth and they're on their way to the ring!

The match is now a tag team contest with Punk & Del Rio facing Miz & R-Truth.

"Awesome Truth" is back in action, ready to make their presence known on Raw.

The WWE Champion suddenly becomes "injured," leaving Punk to fend for himself.

Punk suddenly finds himself in a 2-on-1 match.

The Straightedge Superstar puts up a strong fight for a few minutes.

But the match quickly descends into chaos, as The Miz & R-Truth lose by disqualification when they double-team Punk.

Triple H runs down to the ring to help CM Punk.

Punk and The Game quickly send R-Truth fleeing.

The Straightedge Superstar takes control of the situation as "Awesome Truth" run from the ring.
