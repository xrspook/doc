SmackDown Five-Point Preview: July 18, 2014

BY Ryan Pappolla

July 18, 2014

Who in the Battleground Battle Royal can make a bold statement?

With WWE Battleground’s Intercontinental Championship Battle Royal just two days away, every single competitor involved in the match is aiming to make a statement heading into Sunday.

The Mexican Aristocrat, Alberto Del Rio, and Kofi Kingston will square off in singles competition, while United States Champion Sheamus will team with Dolph Ziggler to take on the pairing of The Miz & Fandango. All six men will have their sights set on gaining invaluable momentum as they attempt to obtain the prestigious Intercontinental Title on Sunday. Tune in to SmackDown to see who picks up steam, tonight at 8/7 CT on SyFy!
