SmackDown Five-Point Preview: August 2, 2013

BY Michael Burdick

August 02, 2013

Who will be Del Rio’s Daniel Bryan?

Several weeks ago, newly crowned Raw General Manager Brad Maddox gave WWE Champion John Cena the opportunity to pick his No. 1 Contender at SummerSlam – paving the way for The Champ to create a highly anticipated clash between himself and Daniel Bryan.

Tonight, SmackDown General Manager Vickie Guerrero will give World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio the same opportunity to pick his opponent for the upcoming pay-per-view! But, who will it be? The list of former World Champions and up-and-coming Superstars on the blue brand is long and the possibilities are endless. Tune into Friday night's best show at 8/7, only on Syfy, to find out!
