TNA Wants Former WWE Champion For Wednesday's Impact, Big Deals Offered To Alberto Del Rio

By Marc Middleton | January 05, 2015

Big money is reportedly being offered to Alberto Del Rio by TNA, Lucha Underground and Bellator MMA, which is owned by former TNA partner Spike TV's parent company Viacom.

Regarding a return to MMA, it's said that Del Rio can make really good money if he wants to fight but at least word he had not decided and that could be a strong indication that he's sticking to pro wrestling.

Dixie Carter reportedly wants Del Rio for this Wednesday's live Impact Wrestling premiere on Destination America. TNA really wants a big name that's new to TNA for the Destination America premiere but there's been no word of any major signings and the event is this Wednesday night.
