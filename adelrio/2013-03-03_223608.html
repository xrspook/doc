SHOW RESULTS - 3/2 WWE in Kalamazoo, Mich.: LMS main event, A.J. vs. Paige Divas bout, Ziggler & Big E. challenge for Tag Titles

Mar 3, 2013 - 1:15:53 AM

WWE Smackdown house show results March 2, 2013 Kalamazoo, Mich.

Report by Kristi Burchett, PWTorch correspondent

The last match was a Last Man Standing match between Big Show and Alberto Del Rio.

(8) World Hvt. champion Alberto Del Rio beat Big Show in a Last Man Standing match to retain the World Title. Del Rio used a Kendo Stick, but Show took it and broke it into tiny pieces. Show tried to use a chair, but Del Rio took it and used it on him. Then, tables came into play. Del Rio leaned one in a corner and almost got put through it, but then reversed Show's move and threw him through it. Show then went to the other side of the ring and got out another table. He set it up and went after Del Rio, but Ricardo interfered. Show threw him from one side of the ring toward the entrance ramp. Del Rio then kicked Show hard enough to push him off the side of the ring and through the table. The ref reached a count of ten and Del Rio won!

The crowd went home happy and it was a very good show!

WWE Live Event Results From Kalamazoo (3/2) - NXT Diva Debuts, Barrett Vs. Dallas, Orton

By Raj Giri March 03, 2013

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Shannon for sending in these results from last night's WWE live event in Kalamazoo, Michigan:

The main event was The Big Show vs. Alberto Del Rio in a last man standing match. Del Rio defeated Big Show after throwing him over the ropes through a table. He had also thrown him through a table earlier in the match and beat him with a chair and kendo stick early on. At the beginning of the match fans were calling Big Show "fat," and he took the mic and said, "You think I'm fat? Yeah, I'm fat, I have a fat wallet. So shut up before I buy your house and kick you out of it."
