Six-Man Tag Team Match

Their third tag team partner is Mr. Money in the Bank, Alberto Del Rio.

The Miz is certain he'll steal the show at SummerSlam.

R-Truth arrives for the match talking about conspiracies.

Rey Mysterio is welcomed by the West Coast crowd in Los Angeles.

The action kicks off with Kofi Kingston against The Miz.

John Morrison is looking for retribution against his rival, R-Truth.

R-Truth and The Miz double-team Morrison in the Six-Man Tag Team Match.

Del Rio takes no mercy against his opponent.

Mr. Money in the Bank and R-Truth reach into the ring, hoping to tag in for The Miz.

Mysterio nearly hits the 619 on two of his opponents.

Working well as a team, Kingston then takes out The Miz.

Morrison soars over the ropes to take out Del Rio.

Mysterio hits a giant Frog Splash against R-Truth, securing the victory.

Mysterio will face the Undisputed WWE Champion tomorrow on Raw!
