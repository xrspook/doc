LEE COLLAPSE, CENA TEASES MANIA AT WEMBLEY, PUNK & BRYAN TEAM AND MORE: 11/15 WWE IN LONDON RESULTS

By Gregory Queen on 2013-11-16 09:12:49

I see you already wrote about what happened to AJ Lee, but didn't have the complete results of the show. Here they are. Beyond the obviously scary few minutes with AJ, it was a good show. *Primetime Players beat Hunico & Camacho.

World champion John Cena defeated Alberto Del Rio. Cena got booed as much as he was cheered. There was a ref bump so no one could rule that Alberto tapped out to an STF. Cena went to check on the ref but Del Rio attacked him. Cena made the comeback and hit the Attitude Adjustment for the pin.

Cena cut a promo after, teasing the idea of bringing Wrestlemania to Wembley Stadium. YES!
