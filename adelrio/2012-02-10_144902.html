Alberto Del Rio Hinted to Appear at Raw Taping

Posted by George H. Sirois on 02.09.2012

Per Ricardo Rodriguez...

- The recently injured Alberto Del Rio is being advertised by WWE for this weekend's Smackdown house shows in Northern California.

Ricardo Rodriguez hinted at Del Rio's return on a tweet this past Wednesday. He also suggested that Del Rio could be at the RAW TV taping in Southern California this Monday. "Thinking of going to California this weekend. Hmm. Thinking of going from Northern California and driving the coast to the SoCal.. Still debating."
