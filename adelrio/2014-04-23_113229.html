WE MAIN EVENT REPORT: BIG E IN ACTION; ONE OF THE USOS IN ACTION; THE SMALLEST FEUD CONTINUES; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-04-22 20:50:16

We are in Hershey, Pennsylvania and your announcers are Tom ‘Krackel’ Phillips and Byron ‘Kit Kat’ Saxton.

Match Number One: Big E versus Alberto Del Rio in a Non Title Match

Del Rio with a kick and punches but Big E with a shoulder tackle and he clotheslines Del Rio over the top rope to the floor. Del Rio drops Big E on the top rope and then Del Rio hits a tornado DDT and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a kick to the back and a reverse chin lock. Big E with punches to Del Rio and he sends Del Rio into the turnbuckles. Big E with an Irish whip but he runs into boots from Del Rio.

Big E sends Del Rio into the air and then he hits two clotheslines and ducks a clothesline from Del Rio. Big E tries for a belly-to-belly suplex but Del Rio with shots to the arm and then he kicks Big E and gets a near fall. Del Rio goes up top and he is caught by Big E and Big E hits a belly-to-belly suplex.

Big E with an Ultimate Warrior splash and the straps come down. Big E gets Del Rio up for the Big Ending but Del Rio escapes and he sends Big E into the turnbuckles and hits an enzuigiri to the back of the head and Del Rio gets a near fall.

Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker and he tries for the float over but Big E sends Del Rio into the turnbuckles and then Del Rio kicks Big E away. Big E blocks a tornado DDT and Big E hits the Big Ending for the three count.

Winner: Big E

After the match, Bad News Barrett rises on the Bad News Silo. Barrett says that he’s afraid he’s got some bad news. His days as champion are numbered. Twelve days left in fact. Let’s face it, you have been a truly horrible champion. You have no personality, you are one dimensional, and you teeter on boredom. Don’t forget his complete lack of intellect. Barrett says that Big E reminds him of this audience.

Barrett says that he is going to ruin Big E’s professional life and Big E will have to sit in the crowd with these people. Some of these people have crappy lives, but most of them have no lives whatsoever.
