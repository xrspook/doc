WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez attack Sheamus: photos

WWE.com: 20-Man Great American Bash Battle Royal: photos

Alberto Del Rio struts confidently to the ring weeks ahead of his World Heavyweight Championship opportunity against Sheamus at Money in the Bank.

The Mexican Aristocrat accuses members of the WWE Universe in Corpus Christi, Texas, of coming to the U.S. illegally.

Del Rio inquires about one fan's "papers."

The Celtic Warrior stops Del Rio dead in his tracks.

After a distraction by Rodriguez, Del Rio catches the outnumbered Celtic Warrior with a kick and repeatedly slams the hood of his vintage Rolls-Royce onto Sheamus.
