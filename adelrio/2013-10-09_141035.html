Alberto Del Rio Says Rey Mysterio Is Needed Back, Talks Upcoming Match With John Cena

By Raj Giri | October 08, 2013

ABC 6 out of Columbia, OH interviewed Alberto Del Rio this morning, who was promoting tonight's SmackDown taping at the Nationwide Arena. During the interview, which you can watch here, Del Rio noted that Rey Mysterio would be returning in the next couple of weeks and that he is needed.

"We're waiting for him, we need him, he's a great Superstar," Del Rio said. "He's a legend, and he'll be back soon."

Del Rio also talked about facing John Cena at the Hell In A Cell pay-per-view later this month.

"He's [Cena] been the face of WWE for years and years, and now he's coming back and he's after my title."
