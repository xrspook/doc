Published on Feb 2, 2013

Arda Ocal (@arda_ocal) of The Score Television Network and the Baltimore Sun spoke with former WWE Superstar Bret "Hitman" Hart (@BretHart) on the set of a new TV pilot in which Bret is co-starring. Bret talks about his relationship with "Rowdy" Roddy Piper and whether or not they are actually related. He also comments on his appearance at the Montreal Screwjob panel at the Royal Rumble and if he's sick of telling that story. Finally Bret clarifies some controversial comments he recently about Triple H, whom he considers ""not even among the thousand greatest wrestlers ever."
