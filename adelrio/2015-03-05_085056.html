Main Event Confirmed For 4FW ‘Global Takeover 2015′

作者: xrspook

日期: 2015-03-05 08:50:56+08:00

分类: 点点图片

标签: alberto_del_rio, el_patron, global_takeover_2015, 4fw, swindon, doug_williams

Posted on March 4, 2015 by Wrestle Ropes

4FW have confirmed the main event for their May 23rd show, ‘Global Takeover 2015′ at the M.E.C.A. in Swindon.

It has been announced that the current 4FW Heavyweight champion, Doug Williams will face former WWE World Heavyweight champion, ‘El Patron’ Alberto Del Rio at the M.E.C.A. on May 23rd at ‘Global Takeover 2015′.
