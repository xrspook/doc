9/8 SMACKDOWN RESULTS FROM HAMILTON, ONTARIO

By Phil Marquis on 2012-09-09 10:42:03

WWE in Hamilton, Ontario live report:

BOOKER T VIDEO PROMO: Booker T wants the fans to tweet to vote for the match type for the main event. Will it be a street fight or a 2 out of 3 falls match between Sheamus and Alberto Del Rio for the World Championship?

SHEAMUS (c) defeated ALBERTO DEL RIO in a fan's choice street fight for the World Heavyweight Championship (Sheamus can't use the Brogue Kick). Pre-match, Ricardo tried to announce but couldn't (he had a neck brace). Del Rio gave him the night off and says the crowd doesn't deserve to hear his beautiful voice anyways. He says we all like Sheamus because we are peasants just like him. Sheamus comes out. Del Rio tears a fan's sign and Sheamus says he is the one without class for doing so.

Copps Coliseum was...4/5 full? The crowd seemed to love the whole show except for some boring bits like Reigns/Slater.
