SummerSlam Axxess VIP Superstar schedule

BY WWE.com staff

July 12, 2013

The VIP Superstar schedule for SummerSlam Axxess – the ultimate WWE fan experience of the summer – has just been announced. The event takes place Aug. 17 and Aug. 18 at the Nokia Plaza at L.A.
