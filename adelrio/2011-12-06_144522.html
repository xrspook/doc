WWE Raw 2011-12-05

John Laurinaitis announces the WWE Championship Match at WWE TLC
Daniel Bryan vs. Alberto Del Rio
Contract signing for the WWE Title Match at WWE TLC

After all three competitors sign the contract, Laurinaitis asks them to pose for a photo for WWE.com.

But Alberto Del Rio interrupts and says a WWE Title Match is "mine, mine!"

Del Rio points out that last week Punk won because of the exposed turnbuckle.

The Mexican Superstar says Punk purposely and illegally cheated.

"Which one of you two is the longest reigning champion in the entire WWE?" the showoff asks.

"You are looking at the 2011 Superstar of the Year and the next WWE Champion," The Awesome One predicts.

The Straight Edge Superstar takes a look at the contract before deciding if he'll sign.

"No matter what happens at WWE TLC, you're not walking out WWE Champion," The Miz says to Punk.

Punk calls Laurinaitis' contract signing boring, and says, "if you want anything done right, I guess you have to do it yourself," before going on the attack.

Thanks to the WWE Champion, the contract signing erupts into a brawl!

Punk gives Del Rio a taste of what he might experience at WWE TLC.
