SPOILERS For This Week's WWE SmackDown

By Marc Middleton | May 07, 2013

Thanks to Phil for the following WWE SmackDown spoilers from tonight's tapings in Raleigh, NC:

Backstage interview with Zeb Colter and Jack Swagger. Zeb says Swagger vs. Big E Langston will be a real American vs. affirmative action.

Alberto Del Rio comes out for commentary. Langston vs. Swagger is up next. They brawl with ladders. Del Rio and Dolph Ziggler also get involved. The match ends with Del Rio posing with the World Heavyweight Title on top of the ladder.
