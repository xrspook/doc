WWE NXT REPORT; FACE OFF BETWEEN CHAMPION AND NUMBER ONE CONTENDER; SEASON FOUR OF NXT GETS CLOSURE; TAG TITLE MATCH; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-11-29 16:06:24

Daniel Bryan says that tonight marks the return to the show that started it all for him, NXT. Since he left, he has accomplished a lot in WWE. He launched a successful line of t-shirts, he won the World Championship, he has grown an immaculate beard, and he am the Tag Team Champions. He has a few requests. He demands that no one say yes when he comes to the ring. He demands that no one calls him a goat face. He also demands that Kane stays out of his way.

Kane tells Daniel that he am the Tag Team Champions and he tells Bryan to stay out of his way.

Daniel and Kane have a duck season/rabbit season yes/no argument over being the Tag Team Champions. Daniel storms away in disgust.

We return to Full Sail University in Winter Park, Florida for the latest episode of NXT. Your announcers are Jim ‘Buddy Jack is with Bam Bam’ Ross and Tom ‘Tom’ Phillips.

Match Number Two: Xavier Woods versus Memo Montenegro

Woods does some dancing before locking up and Woods with a side head lock and take down. Memo gets back to his feet but Woods takes Memo back to the mat. Memo with an escape and a shoulder. Memo runs into a back elbow from Woods. Xavier with a side head lock take down.

Woods with another side head lock take down and he continues to work on the neck. Memo with punches but Woods with punches of his own. Woods blocks a hip toss and then he hits a drop kick to the chest. Woods with the somersault head spring into a clothesline for the three count.

Winner: Xavier Woods
