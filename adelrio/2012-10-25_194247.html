Alberto Del Rio腹股沟撕裂伤休期间原来去这里了

作者: xrspook

日期: 2012-10-25 19:42:47+08:00

分类: 点点文本

标签: alberto_del_rio, wwe, 2012, fort_lauderdale, 腹股沟, 撕裂, 伤休, pablo_marquez, memo_montenegro

所以说，如果Alberto Del Rio退役了，或者说不在WWE干了，他可以继续留在South Florida，合伙或自己开个馆子什么的，他会是个好教练，毕竟给后辈们传授经验的经验，他可是丰富的。

外插一句哈，Alberto Del Rio现在家安在Fort Lauderdale（这个采访听回来的）。所以为啥他会去Pablo Marquez那里呢，哈哈哈，近呗。当然了，他们也是好朋友，毕竟，很粘他的弟弟也在那里练习。

“Same as Alberto Del Rio’s little brother. I had him for six months before he got signed, Memo Montenegro. When Alberto had his groin surgery, he would come to my school and workout with my students. The students saw this was a star and a WWE champion. It doesn’t get any better than that. It made me look good.”

Montenegro is currently with FCW WWE NXT, the developmental group for WWE based in Tampa.

The Main Event Training Academy is hosting its next student show 8 p.m. Saturday, Oct. 6 at its facility, 312 E. Oakland Park Blvd., Fort Lauderdale. Cost is $7 for tickets. A boxing event will occur at 6 p.m., and fans can attend both for $15. For show and training information, visit www.ccwevents.com, call 954-548-5779 or email Pablo Marquez at pablomarquez94@yahoo.com.
