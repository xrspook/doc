NEW Alberto Del Rio wallpaper!

Posted on October 11th, 2010 at 6:02 pm by Mr. Kupy

Alberto Del Rio lately has been branded as a Mexican JBL. It’s true they have similar entrances, lifestyle and inflated ego..but the similarities end there. Alberto is on a different level, a much higher one. Wrestling-wise, Alberto trumps JBL in every way. He also has a mysterious aura in him, a mean streak (and a really mean one) he hides behind that deceiving smile of his. It’s like you don’t want to cross him or else, you’re done for good, just like what happened recently to his two victims, Rey Mysterio and Christian. Even though Rey came back and pinned him, their feud is just starting. And it’s a damn good feud to watch for.

There are a lot out there who predicts this guy to be the World Champion as soon as 2011. Well, I wouldn’t be surprised if that happens next year. If Sheamus can win the big one as a rookie, then there’s no reason WWE should hold back on pushing Alberto Del Rio as the WWE or World Heavyweight Champion come 2011. I believe this guy will make a HUGE impact on WWE SmackDown! sooner or later.

Kupy Wrestling Wallpapers brings to you this brand new wrestling wallpaper featuring Alberto Del Rio..and his famous Rey Mysterio chihuahua! =P
