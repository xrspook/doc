WWE.com: Mr. McMahon reveals that Booker T is the new General Manager of SmackDown: photos

WWE.com: Randy Orton vs. Alberto Del Rio: photos

Always dangerous, Del Rio momentarily turns the tables on Orton.

Just more than two weeks away from SummerSlam, SmackDown emanates from Indianapolis.

Mr. McMahon returns to SmackDown for the first time in nearly three years to announce the new General Manager of the best show on Friday nights ...

... six-time World Champion Booker T!

A sneering Alberto Del Rio crashes the party and is welcomed by showers of boos from the WWE Universe.

The Mexican Aristocrat offers Booker his "congratulations" and threatens his SummerSlam opponent, World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus.

The Great White appears to congratulate Booker and confront the arrogant proclamations of Del Rio.

Booker T makes his first two matches as new GM ...

... in the main event, Del Rio will square off with a returning Randy Orton, and Sheamus must battle the powerhouse known as Tensai.

After two months away, Randy Orton returns to SmackDown to the roar of the WWE Universe in Indianapolis.

Alberto Del Rio prepares to battle The Viper, just a little more than two weeks before he challenges World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus at SummerSlam.

Orton shows very little ring rust, taking the fight right to the former WWE Champion.

Trapping Del Rio against the ropes, WWE's Apex Predator unleashes a vicious barrage of punches.

Before Orton can finish his opponent, Del Rio's personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez, interferes in the match, bringing about a disqualification.

World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus emerges to toss Del Rio back into the ring, right to a waiting Viper.

Orton lands a crushing DDT and one of the most devastating moves in all of WWE, the RKO.

Randy Orton stands tall to end an explosive episode of SmackDown.
