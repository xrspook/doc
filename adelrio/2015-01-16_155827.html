Alberto El Patrón will be in San Antonio

Saturday, April 4th, 2015 - San Antonio Shrine Auditorium

Huge News for the return of Ring of Honor to the Shrine Auditorium in San Antonio, TX on Saturday, April 4th!

We can confirm that this will be a National TV Taping. San Antonio fans have been begging to have a TV Taping at the Shrine and ROH officials have heard the fans. The cameras will be rolling and the action will be hotter than ever. You’ll see all the fallout from the huge Supercard of Honor event the weekend before right before your eyes. Ring of Honor has never been hotter and San Antonio fans have supported ROH like few other cities. ROH is excited to present a TV Taping in San Antonio!

But a big announcement that ROHWrestling.com confirmed with matchmaker Nigel McGuinness is that San Antonio’s own, ALBERTO EL PATRÓN, has been signed to compete on Saturday, April 4th! His debut in ROH was spectacular but what will “The Boss” have in store for his hometown?

More ROH Stars set to appear will be announced shortly, right here at ROHWrestling.com

We expect a huge turnout at the Shrine Auditorium on Saturday, April 4th so get your tickets now right here!
