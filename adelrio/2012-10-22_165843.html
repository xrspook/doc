Unstoppable force

October 01, 2010

OKLAHOMA CITY -- Just two nights before Hell in a Cell, The Nexus invaded the season premiere of SmackDown live on Syfy, and The Undertaker once again demonstrated that he has fully harnessed the powers of darkness.

Rey Mysterio returned to SmackDown

While holding a Chihuahua wearing a Rey Mysterio mask, Alberto Del Rio mocked his fellow SmackDown Superstar by talking -- and kissing -- the dog as if it were Mysterio. Next, the real Master of the 619 arrived, shocking the WWE Universe and charging after Del Rio. A stunned Del Rio was able to avoid the brunt of Mysterio's onslaught, but his personal ring announcer was not so lucky. Ricardo Rodriquez was the unlucky recipient of a hard-hitting 619.
