Daniel Bryan Added To WWE TV Tapings, Alberto El Patron Wrestling For Title This Week, Sabu

By Raj Giri | January 05, 2015

Alberto El Patron, f.k.a. Alberto Del Rio, will be challenging for the World Wrestling League World Title at this Tuesday's WWL Guerra de Reyes event in Puerto Rico. El Patron will battle champion Shane Sewell and AAA's Ricky Banderas (former Judas Mesias in TNA) in a triple threat match. The event will take place at the Roberto Clemente Coliseum in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Also on the card, Sabu will defend the WWL Extreme Title against Monster Pain (w/ Mistress Glenda Lee).
