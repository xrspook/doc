WWE.com: “Miz TV” descends into chaos with special guests Randy Orton and Big Show: photos

Tension and emotion runs high as the two powerhouse teams look to gain precious momentum heading into Sunday's highly anticipated event.

With Extreme Rules just two days away, The Miz kicks off an "extreme" episode of SmackDown with "the most must-see show in WWE," "Miz TV."

The Awesome One's guests are two Superstars who will battle in a ferocious Extreme Rules Match Sunday, Big Show and Randy Orton.

The rivals exchange words, as the tension builds for their highly anticipated clash at Sunday's pay-per-view.

The situation grows even more volatile when Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter crash the party.

Alberto Del Rio - who will face Swagger in an "I Quit" Match for the No. 1 contendership to the World Heavyweight Title on Sunday - and Ricardo Rodriguez emerges to deliver a message to his Extreme Rules opponent.

The World's Strongest Man, Mark Henry, is the next Superstar with a message to deliver.

Henry's Extreme Rules opponent, Sheamus, doesn't want to miss out on the fun.

The Celtic Warrior - who battles Henry in a brutal Strap Match on Sunday - dives into the ring with a strap, scattering Swagger, Big Show, Colter and Henry.

Sheamus, Rodriguez, Del Rio and Orton stand tall in the ring.

Senior Advisor to the SmackDown General Manager Theodore Long schedules a huge pre–Extreme Rules Six-Man Tag Team Match: Sheamus, Orton & Del Rio vs. Big Show, Swagger & Henry!

SmackDown hurtles toward a main event for the ages: a Six-Man Tag Team Match between imminent pay-per-view foes.

Mark Henry and Jack Swagger team with Big Show to face Sheamus, Alberto Del Rio and Randy Orton.

The Viper goes to work on a "Real American," as momentum shifts back and forth in the intense matchup.

Swagger grabs Del Rio to prevent his Extreme Rules adversary from tagging in The Celtic Warrior.

Del Rio delivers an impressive high kick to The World's Strongest Man.

WWE's Apex Predator, Randy Orton, hits a searing RKO on Swagger and picks up a huge victory for his team.

Will Alberto Del Rio, Sheamus and Randy Orton be standing tall after their respective Extreme Rules matches in a mere two days? Tune in Sunday, May 19, at 8 ET/5 PT!
