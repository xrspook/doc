Smacktalk Radio Live - January 25, 2013

Jan 26th, 2013 by smacktalkradiolive

It's go-home week for the WWE as they present the 'Royal Rumble' PPV this Sunday from Phoenix, AZ. Tonight, we preview one of their most entertaining shows of the year and chat with someone who perhaps hopes to make a big spash in the 30-man Rumble, WWE's own Ricardo Rodriguez. We find out about the day he met Alberto Del Rio, how he is enjoying the boos turning to cheers from the fans and what his favorite Rumble moment ever is. We also run down the latest fallout from this past Monday Night Raw, talking all things CM Punk, The Rock, John Cena, Dolph Ziggler and more. By the fans - for the fans, this is wrestling talk radio.
