Exclusive WrestleMania XXX Travel Packages available next Monday, Aug. 12!

BY WWE.com staff

August 05, 2013

WrestleMania XXX takes over New Orleans' Mercedes-Benz Superdome on April 6, 2014, and travel packages for the biggest week in WWE history go on sale next Monday, Aug. 12, at 3 p.m. ET!

Don’t miss out on your chance to celebrate 30 years of The Showcase of the Immortals with tickets to WrestleMania, Monday Night Raw, the WWE Hall of Fame ceremony, WrestleMania Axxess and more.

This year, WWE fans will have five different tiers to choose from, with packages starting at $795 per person. In addition to tickets to WrestleMania Week activities and various options for hotel room accommodations, every package includes a $50 gift card to the world-famous Pat O’Brien’s restaurant in the French Quarter!

Be sure to check back on Monday, Aug. 12, for details on all the packages and your first opportunity to save your place at The Showcase of the Immortals!
