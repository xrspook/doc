WWE.com: Week in Photos, August 11, 2012

In the night's second match, Christian takes on No. 1 contender to the World Heavyweight Championship, Alberto Del Rio.

The Mexican Aristocrat connects with a vicious kick in the middle of the ring.

Del Rio locks in the Cross Armbreaker and wins the match.

But there's no celebration for Del Rio, as World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus appears on the TitanTron, seated in Del Rio's prized Ferrari!

As Sheamus heads out for a joyride, Del Rio is furious in the ring.

Sheamus apologizes to Booker, to the WWE Universe and to Alberto Del Rio for taking his car. But Del Rio isn't satisfied.

When Del Rio threatens to file a police report, Sheamus has a better idea - the two will meet later in the night with the World Heavyweight Championship on the line!

But before the bout can begin, "police officers" surround the ring.

Del Rio claims that he never filed the police report, but then smiles and yells, "go!" to the "officers" and they attack The Great White!

With the damage done, Del Rio levels The Great White in the corner.

It is an orchestrated setup by Del Rio, who leaves The Great White in a weakened state.
