CM Punk vs. Alberto Del Rio

The Mexican Aristocrat proves he is not intimidated to step inside The Devil's Playground.

Alberto Del Rio hopes to reclaim his destiny and win the WWE Championship at WWE Hell in a Cell.

CM Punk immediately goes on the offensive, applying pressure to Del Rio's head and neck.

The Mexican Aristocrat doesn't allow The Voice of the Voiceless to build any momentum.

WWE Champion John Cena joins Jim Ross, Booker T and Michael Cole on commentary.

Del Rio continues to use the ropes to his advantage, keeping Punk in the corner.

The cunning Mexican Superstar tries to lock in his patented cross armbreaker.

The Second City Savior manages to fend off Del Rio's submission attempts.

CM Punk turns the tables with a kick to the head and secures the victory.

After Punk's impressive victory, Hell in a Cell descends from the rafters.

Cena manages to trap Ricardo Rodriguez inside Satan's Structure.

Trapped inside the cell, The Second City Savior hits Rodriguez with the GTS ...

... followed by an Attitude Adjustment from The Champ.

Alberto Del Rio gets in on the action and attacks both Cena and Punk with a steel chair.
