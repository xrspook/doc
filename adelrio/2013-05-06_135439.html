WWE House Show Results (5/4): Reading, PA

by Nick Paglino

May 05, 2013

Dolph Ziggler with AJ and Big E vs. Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez. for the World Heavyweight Championship

Ziggler was out first with a lot of boos despite a lot of Ziggler shirts in the crowd. AJ was immediately sent to the back because of a Twitter/Text pole asking the crowd to vote if she should stay or be sent to the back. Unfortunately she was sent to the back. Big E got to stay. Very entertaining match and was just as good as any match we've already seen from Ziggler and Del Rio. Ricardo and Big E were very involved and Ricardo was eventually thrown into the ring steps where he stayed most of the match. Alberto eventually locked in his submission but Big E interfered causing Alberto to win by disqualification. Alberto and Ricardo got the last laugh by clearing Ziggler and Big E out of the ring with Alberto standing tall in the end. Great match and should have been the main event.
