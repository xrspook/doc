WWE Superstar Alberto Del Rio and his Aston Martin Vantage on D2Forged Wheels

Aston Martin, Celebrity Cars, D2Forged — By Earlsimx on October 26, 2012 at 1:27 pm

WWE Superstar Alberto Del Rio is not only a good fighter, but the Mexican is also known for showing up with top of the class supercars ranging from Ferrari’s, Maserati’s, Rolls-Royce’s and Bentley’s. At a past Monday Night Raw, D2Forged accompanied the superstar who drove an Aston Martin Vantage featuring their wheels. This is not the first time we have seen this Aston Martin but it’s the first time we see it on Raw.

The New York based wheels manufacturer fitted it with these multi-piece construction design wheels utilizing three separate components assembled together resulting to an impressive allure on the Aston Martin.
