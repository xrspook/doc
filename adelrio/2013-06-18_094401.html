WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio celebrates winning the World Heavyweight Championship at WWE Payback: photos

Ricardo Rodriguez announces Alberto Del Rio as the new World Heavyweight Champion.

Del Rio defends his World Title victory over Dolph Ziggler the previous night at WWE Payback.

"Some of you are saying that the way I won was controversial, but what about the night after WrestleMania? Dolph Ziggler attacked me," Del Rio says.

"You cheered for a coward who attacked an injured man. I gave you everything, but you never gave me the respect I deserved."

CM Punk emerges to confront the new World Heavyweight Champion.

"The last time you and I were in a ring and you were holding one of those was Survivor Series when I beat you for the WWE Title, and I went on to hold that for 434 days," Punk points out.

"I'm banged up because last night while this guy was stealing a title from Dolph Ziggler, I was stealing the show with Chris Jericho," Punk says.

The Straight Edge Superstar wants a match against Del Rio on Raw.

Punk's wish is granted by Vickie Guerrero: "As great as Payback was last night, I can't think of a better way of continuing the momentum than having Raw's main event be Alberto Del Rio vs. CM Punk!"
