WWE Championship Triple Threat TLC Match

Even without his customary luxury car, Alberto Del Rio strides into WWE TLC with the WWE Championship on his mind.

WWE Champion CM Punk arrives for his Triple Threat Tables, Ladders and Chairs Match for the WWE Title.

The Miz makes an "awesome" entrance in his bid to reclaim the WWE Championship.

The Miz starts things off early, kicking Punk while he's down.

CM Punk turns Del Rio's own ladder against him with a well-placed kick.

Punk hits The Miz with a knee to the face.

The Mexican Aristocrat comes crashing down to the ropes.

Punk lays into The Miz with multiple chair strikes to The Awesome One's back.

Del Rio traps Punk's arm in a chair-enhanced Cross Armbreaker.

Del Rio's ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez, tries to trip Punk up en route to the WWE Championship.

With The Second City Saint handcuffed to the ladder, Del Rio lays into the champion with a flurry of stomps.

Rodriguez looks to claim the WWE Championship for his boss.

Punk and The Miz have other ideas, sending the woe-begotten ring announcer to the tables below.

Punk looks to take Miz out with a GTS.

The Miz handcuffs Punk to the ropes, keeping the WWE Championship out of his reach.

Punk works to dismantle the turnbuckle and free himself.

The WWE Champion breaks his bonds while Miz and Del Rio scale the ladders.

One final kick sends Del Rio falling to the mat.

"The Best in the World" revels in his prize.
