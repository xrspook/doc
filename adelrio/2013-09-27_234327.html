SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Sept. 27, 2013

BY Michael Burdick

September 27, 2013

Can Del Rio’s rivalry with RVD get any more Extreme?

For some time now, the high-octane, high-flying, sacrifice-your-body offensive style of Rob Van Dam has been on a continuous collision course with the ruthless, ultra-versatile onslaught of World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio – even before the Mexican Champion got himself intentionally disqualified at Night of Champions.

As a matter of fact, the fallout from this intense rivalry has seen both the resurrection of the explosive Van Terminator and brutal back-to-back assaults by Del Rio on his World Title No. 1 contender in the past week alone.

While Del Rio is already slated to face R-Truth tonight, the fact that the reigning champion and his WWE Battleground challenger will be in the same building can only make increased carnage between them extremely probable. The only question will be, which Superstar will lay the SmackDown? Find out tonight, only on Syfy!
