The last person we met of that session was Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez , Mel loves her some Del Rio so it was only fair. We got in line for them getting to know the employee that was running the handicap line to the point where we were all laughing. It got to our turn, well at least Del Rio came up to us signing one of our sheets and he stopped looking up at Mel and all he had said was ‘Hey,’ when Mel instantly blushed, she was as red as a tomato after just one word and it was so adorable and he just smiled, I on the other hand thought it was adorable yeah but I was laughing so hard.
