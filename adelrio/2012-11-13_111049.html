WWE Raw 2012-11-12 非官方战报

分类: 点点文本

WWE RAW REPORT: TEAM FOLEY IS FINALIZED; MORE EVIDENCE UNVEILED; WILLIAM REGAL WRESTLES AND MORE; JERRY LAWLER RETURNS; TAG TEAMS ARE FEATURED; BRAD MADDOX'S FUTURE IS DETERMINED

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-11-12 20:07:50

Today is the observance of Veterans Day and it is time to honor the men and women who have defended this country.

This week, Jerry Lawler returns to the announce table after suffering a heart attack in Montreal. Will his heart be able to deal with the potential tryst between John Cena and AJ Lee as Vickie Guerrero presents more evidence. Brad Maddox will face Ryback with a contract on the line for the former referee. The main event will see CM Punk face John Cena in a non-title match. Welcome to Day 358 of Punk Holding the WWE Title.

We are live from Columbus, Ohio and your announcers are Michael ‘I abdicate my throne’ Cole and Jim ‘The Heenan to my Monsoon is back’ Ross.

We see footage from Smackdown’s Falls Count Anywhere Match between Randy Orton and Alberto Del Rio that was won by Randy Orton.

Match Number One: Randy Orton versus Dolph Ziggler

They lock up and Ziggler with a side head lock and shoulder tackle. Orton sends Ziggler high into the air and then he sends Ziggler to the apron. Orton tries to suplex Ziggler back into the ring and he turns it into a slingshot suplex and he gets a near fall. Orton with an Irish whip but Orton runs into an elbow from Ziggler. Ziggler with a kick and a drop kick for a near fall.

Ziggler with a neck breaker and a series of elbow drops and then he does the delay super elevated elbow drop for a near fall. Ziggler with a reverse chin lock. Orton with a belly-to-back suplex to escape the hold. Orton with two clotheslines then he avoids a clothesline from Ziggler and hits a power slam. Orton sends Ziggler to the apron and he hits the IEDDT.

Orton looks around and he twists to the mat. Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez come to ringside but Ziggler cannot capitalize. Ziggler misses a Fameasser and Orton with a rollup for te three count.

Winner: Randy Orton

After the match, Ricardo attacks Orton and then Ziggler and Del Rio take over. Kofi Kingston comes to the ring and he takes care of Ziggler and Ricardo and then Del Rio is sent to the floor.

Teddy Long comes out and says that since Vickie Guerrero is involved with the AJ Lee stuff, he is going to do something super because it is a Supershow. We will have a tag match when we come back.

Match Number Two: Kofi Kingston and Randy Orton versus Dolph Ziggler and Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez)

Ziggler and Kofi start things off and Kofi with forearms and kicks to Ziggler. Kofi with a drop kick and Ziggler and Del Rio discuss strategy as we go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio kicks Kofi in the back and he works on the neck. Kofi punches Del Rio and Del Rio sends Kofi into the corner and Ziggler tags back in. Ziggler tries for a neck breaker but he takes too long and Kofi with a back slide for a near fall. Ziggler with a drop kick for a near fall.

Ziggler with a reverse chin lock and he does a headstand to add more pressure and then he bridges. Kofi gets back to his feet. Ziggler misses a splash into the corner and both men are down. Del Rio and Orton make the tag and Orton with two clotheslines and then he punches Ziggler off the apron. Del Rio with a savate kick and he tries for the float over into the cross arm breaker but Orton counters and he hits a power slam. Orton gets Del Rio on the apron but Alberto gets into the ring.

Kofi makes the tag and hits a springboard cross body for a near fall. Kofi with SOS but Ziggler breaks up the cover. Orton clotheslines Ziggler over the top rope and Kofi sets up for Trouble in Paradise. Ricardo distracts Kofi and Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri for the three count.

Winners:Alberto Del Rio and Dolph Ziggler

Ricardo Rodriguez makes his way to ringside and he has something for Rosa Mendes. Epico tells Rosa to ignore him.

Miz is on the phone and he says that he will find a way to impact Survivor Series. Dolph says that it is a matter of time before Miz begs to be a part of Team Ziggler. Dolph says that he let Del Rio take victory over Kofi and Orton.

An ambulance backs into the arena and unfortunately for Maddox, Alberto Del Rio does not emerge from the driver’s seat.
