WWE MAIN EVENT REPORT: SHEAMUS VERSUS WYATT; PAIGE IN ACTION; THE FUTURE OF THE BROTHERHOOD; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-04-29 20:45:35

We are in Kansas City, Missouri and your announcers are Tom ‘I-70’ Phillips and Byron ‘Royal Flush’ Saxton.

Match Number One: Goldust (with Cody Rhodes) versus Alberto Del Rio

Del Rio misses a splash in the corner when the bell rings and Goldust with punches. Del Rio with kicks and punches but the referee pulls Del Rio out of the corner. Goldust with an Irish whip but he misses a splash in the corner and Del Rio with a lungblower for a near fall. Del Rio chokes Goldust in the ropes and the referee warns him. Del Rio with punches to Goldust followed by a slam and he gets a near fall.

Del Rio with a rear chin lock and his knee in the back. Goldust with punches but Del Rio with a punch of his own and a kick to the leg and a shot to the back of the leg. Goldust with a drop down uppercut and a power slam and both men are down.

Del Rio pushes Goldust into the corner and Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri for a near fall. Del Rio with a belly-to-back suplex and then he pulls down the knee pad for the super kick but Goldust moves and Goldust with a dragon screw leg whip. Goldust hits the Final Cut for the three count.

Winner: Goldust

After the match, Cody celebrates in the ring with his brother.

Renee Young enters the ring to interview Goldust. She asks him if they are going to continue to be a tag team or go their separate ways.

Goldust is shocked and he says nay nay. The Brotherhood is intact. Although they might compete in singles matches, they will always have each other’s backs.

Renee asks Cody if he is proud that his brother beat Alberto Del Rio.

Cody asks Renee if she is trying to say that Goldust is better because he beat Del Rio and he couldn’t. Cody apologizes. He says that he is not himself these days. The Brotherhood is together and the Brotherhood will stay together.

They shake hands and hug.

Tom mentions that today is World Wish Day.

We see footage of John Cena’s most recent wishes that were granted.

The Dean Ambrose title defense on Smackdown is now a Fatal Four Way Match with Roman Reigns and Seth Rollins banned from ringside.
