Alberto Del Rio to respond to Jack Swagger's "State of the Union" on SmackDown

BY WWE.COM STAFF

February 20, 2013

World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio has issued a response to the “State of the Union” address given by his No. 1 contender Jack Swagger — and his compatriot Zeb Colter — this past Monday on Raw. Via a Tout, Del Rio said he will rebut the Monday night tirade with a “State of the Union” of his own, this Friday on SmackDown on Syfy.

In his Raw rant, Colter took direct aim at the World Champion as Del Rio looked on backstage, calling the Mexican Superstar a “man who only came into this country to reap the rewards of our motherland.”

Now it’s Del Rio’s turn to take to the mic. What will the titleholder have to say in response to his WrestleMania opponent? Tune in Friday night at 8/7 CT on Syfy to find out.
