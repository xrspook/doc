WWE.com: CM Punk vs. Alberto Del Rio: photos

As ordered by Senior Advisor to the SmackDown General Manager Teddy Long, Paul Heyman makes his way to the announce table for the main event match.

CM Punk prepares to battle World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio.

With a long and tumultuous history between the two Superstars, the match quickly turns into an intense battle of wills and skills.

Del Rio, a dangerous submission specialist, targets Punk's arm with vicious attacks.

The World Heavyweight Champion goes for his signature high kick on "The Best in the World."

Del Rio uses all his power and submission prowess to brutally torque Punk's arm.

Focusing his attention on the former WWE Champion's prone appendange, Del Rio slams his opponent into the ring post.

When Del Rio starts to dismantle the announce table, Heyman tries to talk to him ...

... And is attacked by the World Heavyweight Champion for his troubles!

Seeing his friend attacked, Punk snaps, viciously attacking Del Rio as the WWE Universe cheers.

The Straight Edge Superstar continues his assault until until the referee counts out both Superstars, leading to a No Contest.

Unsatisfied with the double count-out, Punk hits a searing GTS on Del Rio!

Punk checks on his downed friend at ringside and helps him to his feet as an explosive and intense episode of SmackDown goes off the air.
