Raw Five-Point Preview: November 11, 2013

BY ANTHONY BENIGNO

November 10, 2013

Three-way dance

John Cena has already backed up his World Heavyweight Championship victory at WWE Hell in a Cell with several more wins, including a grueling title defense against Damien Sandow. But with not one but two Superstars circling The Champ’s prize – Sandow himself and former champion Alberto Del Rio – can the Cenation leader, still recovering from surgery, rise above a pair of rabid dogs hell-bent on taking his gold?
