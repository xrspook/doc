1/30 ROH RESULTS FROM DEARBORN, MI

By Mike Kelly on 2015-01-31 01:07:22

In the pre-show match, Cheeseburger beat Stokely Hathaway.

Jay Briscoe beat Mike Bennett (w/Maria Kanellis) to retain the ROH World Title.

Hanson beat BJ Whitmer.

Tommaso Ciampa beat Tyson Dux.

Jimmy Jacobs beat Tadarius Thomas.

Matt Sydal beat Matt Taven.

Romantic Touch beat Drew Onyx. via leap-frog leg drop.

Michael Elgin beat Mark Briscoe. Jay, Ciampa and Hanson got involved. Jay was busted open.

Alberto Patron beat ACH.

Jay Lethal (w/Truth Martini) beat Roderick Strong to retain the TV Title.
