WWE Raw 2012-12-17 非官方战报

日期: 2012-12-18 12:44:07+08:00

COMPLETE WWE SLAMMYS RAW REPORT: A NUMBER OF OLD FACES RETURN; SIX MAN TAG MATCH GETS EXTREME; IS THE SHIELD FAIR TO FLAIR?; YES, NO, MAYBE, WOOOOOO, DAMN; TENSAI GETS SHOCKING; A BIG ENDING TO THE SHOW

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-12-17 23:02:54

Previously in the WWE, the Slammys has been a feature of the WWE over the years. Who will win this year? Who is liked the most by the WWE Universe who downloaded the WWE App? Will they talk about TLC at all?

This week, Raw is in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and your announcers are Michael ‘Pat’s’ Cole, John ‘Wharton’ Layfield, and Jerry ‘Where is Dennis Miller’ Lawler.

Santino Marella and Tensai are on the stage to present the Slammy for LOL Moment of the Year. Tensai pulls a Shockmaster as he trips on the curtain with an ice pack on his head.

Santino cannot stop laughing at Tensai. Santino tells Albert it is his turn. Santino says that Tensai in Japanese means ‘Fat Albert’. Santino says that is on the teleprompter but Tensai says there is no teleprompter.

The Nominees are: The Rock’s Boston promo; the Anger Management Segments; Randy Orton attacking Alberto Del Rio and throwing coffee and chili on Ricardo followed by sending Matt face first into a cake; and Vickie Guerrero’s attempts to be Elaine Benes.

We go to commercial.

We are back and the Winner is The Rock.

Santino accepts the award on behalf of the Rock.

Daniel Bryan comes out and he chants no and says that it should be his Slammy. Kane carries Bryan to the back.

Miz is in the ring and he says that they surprised everyone with the Brooklyn Brawler as their partner. Their partner is not just awesome, he takes it to the extreme. He brings out the heart and soul of ECW . . . Tommy Dreamer.

Match Number Seven: Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal, and Drew McIntyre versus Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez), Miz, and Tommy Dreamer

Drew and Del Rio start things off and Drew with a kick and punches in the corner. Del Rio with kicks to Drew followed by a step up enzuigiri. Dreamer tags in and Drew gets away from Del Rio. Slater tags in and he has something to say to Dreamer.

Slater pushes Dreamer and Dreamer mocks Slater’s dance and he punches Heath. Dreamer sends Slater into the mat and then all six men get in the ring. 3MB are sent to the floor. Del Rio with a suicide dive onto 3MB. Miz with a kick to Mahal. Dreamer goes up top and he hits a splash onto 3MB.

We go to commercial.

We are back and Dreamer with a Saito suplex and then Miz and Slater tag in. Miz with a back body drop and then he hits the Awesome Clothesline. Miz with a boot to Mahal but Slater knocks Miz of the apron. Mahal with a boot to Miz on the floor while Slater distracts the referee.

Slater with an Irish whip and he tags Drew in. Drew with a suplex for a near fall. Drew with an arm bar but Miz with punches. Miz with a sunset flip for a near fall. Drew with an elbow. Mahal tags in and hits a leaping knee drop and gets a near fall. Mahal with a rear chin lock. Miz with the Reality Check on Mahal.

Slater tags in and he applies a front face lock and does a gator roll. Miz tries to get to the corner but Slater holds Miz out of the corner and sends Miz into his corner. Miz tries to fight out of the corner but Drew tags in and he connects with an elbow. Drew with a boot to the chest. 3MB triple team Miz. Slater kicks Miz in the midsection.

Slater with a clothesline and he keeps Miz from making the tag. Mahal tags back in and gets a near fall. Mahal chokes Miz in the ropes. Mahal with a knee drop to Miz and he chokes Miz. Drew tags in and gets Miz on his shoulders and he drops Miz to the mat. Drew gets a near fall. Drew stomps on the hand. Slater tags in and punches Miz and gets a near fall.

Miz tags in Del Rio and he connects with clotheslines and a tilt-a-whirl back breaker. Alberto knocks Drew off the apron and then Del Rio with a super kick but he can only get a near fall. Del Rio sets for the cross arm breaker but Slater escapes. Del Rio with the step up enzuigiri and Del Rio sees Dreamer on the apron and he tags in Tommy.

Tommy with a DDT for the three count.

Winners: Tommy Dreamer, Alberto Del Rio, and Miz

After the match, they celebrate in the ring.
