SmackDown Five-Point Preview: June 20, 2014

BY Scott Taylor

June 20, 2014

Can WWE World Heavyweight Title Ladder Match partners co-exist?

Tonight on SmackDown, every member of WWE Money in the Bank’s unprecedented WWE World Heavyweight Championship Ladder Match will compete in a huge 4-on-3 Handicap Match. Randy Orton, Bray Wyatt, Cesaro & Alberto Del Rio will battle John Cena, Roman Reigns & Sheamus in an explosive confrontation that could reach chaotic levels.

Moreover, with the climb for WWE’s most prestigious title fast approaching and each championship contender eyeing an opportunity to grab greatness, will these Superstar-studded teams be able to stay in sync with their Money in the Bank foes? Don’t miss this massive match tonight at 8/7 CT on Syfy!
