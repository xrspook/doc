**SPOILERS** For This Week's WWE SmackDown

By Marc Middleton | April 24, 2012

Thanks to Emilio for tonight's WWE SmackDown spoilers from the tapings in Grand Rapids, Michigan:

* Daniel Bryan opens the show and is interrupted by Alberto Del Rio. Del Rio talks about winning the World Heavyweight Title after Extreme Rules until Big Show interrupts and cleans house. Show chokeslams Ricardo Rodriguez.

* Alberto Del Rio beat Big Show in a non-title match when Cody Rhodes interfered.

*SPOILERS* SmackDown Taping Results For This Friday, 4/27

Posted by James Rolls on April 24, 2012

* The show opened with Daniel Bryan coming down to the ring. Bryan discussed AJ, his job as referee on RAW on Monday and how he would win the World Heavyweight Title back from Sheamus in a 2 out of 3 falls match at Extreme Rules.

* Alberto Del Rio interrupts Bryan and says that while Bryan has a title shot this Sunday, he has the next one. Del Rio says that it does not matter who walks out of Extreme Rules as champion, because it is his destiny to become the new World Heavyweight Champion.

* WWE Intercontinental Champion Big Show comes out and enters the ring, clearing out everyone else but Del Rio’s personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez. Show delivers a chokeslam to Ricardo. Apparently, Show faces Del Rio in a rematch from last week next.

* Alberto Del Rio b. WWE Intercontinental Champion Big Show in a non-title match when Cody Rhodes interfered. After the match, Cody does what he did last week and escapes through the crowd while an angry Show looks on.
