John Cena is ready for his SummerSlam rematch with CM Punk.

Following the controversial events at SummerSlam, both of these ring warriors say they deserve a WWE Title rematch.

The Straight Edge Superstar prepares to send Cena to Dreamland with a Go to Sleep.

The Second City Savior clutches the rope to evade Cena's Attitude Adjustment.

The Cenation leader locks in his dreaded STF submission maneuver ...

Punk nearly seals a pinfall victory, but Cena gets the shoulder up.
