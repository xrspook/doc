WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio unleashes a major assault on Big Show in the parking lot: photos

WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio vs. Dolph Ziggler: photos

Mr. Money in the Bank, Dolph Ziggler, gets a good luck kiss from AJ Lee before he battles Alberto Del Rio in SmackDown's main event.

The clash between two of WWE's most talented Superstars quickly turns into a hard-hitting, high-intensity affair.

The World Heavyweight Champion hits The Showoff with a crushing kick to the skull, as the momentum shifts back and forth in the wildly exciting match.

After Big E Langston interferes to stop a Del Rio top rope attack, he is ejected from ringside.

The Mexican Superstar latches on his signature submission maneuver, the devastating Cross Armbreaker, and torques Ziggler's arm until he taps.

Del Rio's celebration is cut short when Big Show appears on the TitanTron, holding an injured Ricardo Rodriguez.

The irate giant wants revenge for Del Rio’s attack earlier in the night, so he torments the injured ring announcer before hitting him with the brutal KO Punch as SmackDown goes off the air.
