Complete WWE Smackdown Taping Results (SPOILERS)

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 11.26.2013

Notes: Overall, it was a good taping, despite the ridiculous hour of filler during the first hour of Smackdown. The Ziggler vs. Del Rio match on tomorrow's Main Event is must see, and the last hour of Smackdown on Friday is going to be extremely fun with all the tag team matches. Notably, there was no John Cena or Authority during the show, and very little progression of that storyline in general. Not that I'm complaining, the stupid filler was better than that probably would have been.
