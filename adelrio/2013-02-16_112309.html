WWE.com: Chris Jericho vs. Big Show: photos

World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio emerges to share an intense stare with his imposing No. 1 contender.

Before he faces World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio in two days at Elimination Chamber, Big Show prepares to face a different foe in SmackDown's main event ...

... the highly dangerous six-time World Champion and WWE fan favorite Chris Jericho, who is competing himself in the Elimination Chamber Match Sunday.

The two decorated veterans tear into each other, with Big Show displaying his incredible power and raw strength.
