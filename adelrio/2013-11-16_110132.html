John Cena vs. Alberto Del Rio in an Arm Wrestling Challenge: photos | WWE.com

While his left arm is still injured, The Champ's right arm is at 100 percent.

Michael Cole introduces No. 1 contender to the World Heavyweight Championship ...

... Alberto Del Rio, who threw down an Arm Wrestling Challenge aimed at John Cena.

Never one to disappoint, The Champ makes his entrance!

The titleholder says he sees “a desperate man” in Del Rio ahead of their Survivor Series bout.

After making his points, the Cenation leader accepts Del Rio’s Arm Wrestling Challenge.

Cena stuns Del Rio, defeating his opponent in mere seconds!

Claiming he wasn’t ready, Del Rio pleads for one more try.

Cena is happy to oblige.

While Del Rio may have been ready this time, the outcome doesn’t change, as Cena once again defeats him ...

... but a cheap punch knocks Cena to the mat.

The Champ tries to fight back, but Del Rio, at the advantage, plants Cena through the table!

Will John Cena be 100 percent at Survivor Series with his title on the line against the devious Del Rio?
