My Name … is Alberto Del Rio!June 25, 2010Did you know that you’re not as smart as Alberto Del Rio? Or as rich? Or as skilled, brave and pious? Thanks to two long-winded weeks’ worth of introductory videos, the WWE Universe is made well aware of the arrogant Mexican Aristocrat’s stance on them, and his intentions in WWE. According to Del Rio, speaking in a tone reminiscent of the Dos Equis guy, capturing WWE gold is his “destiny.”

The Debut of DestinyAugust 20, 2010With more hype behind his debut than the egg at the 1990 Survivor Series, Del Rio sets high expectations. And he delivers. Porting his own personal ring announcer, Ricardo Rodriguez, and kicking off a tradition of driving to the ring in a different luxury automobile each week, Del Rio silences critics by making two-time World Heavyweight Champion Rey Mysterio tap out to his innovative cross armbreaker submission.

Del Rio Rules RumbleJanuary 30, 2011After less than one year in WWE, Alberto Del Rio accomplishes what eludes many Superstars over the course of an entire career – he wins the Royal Rumble. And, true to his larger-than-life bravado, Del Rio wins the largest Rumble in history, outlasting 39 other Superstars to win a World Championship Match at WrestleMania XXVII.

WrestleMania Main EventApril 3, 2011Alberto Del Rio’s first WrestleMania appearance is a main event championship match. Despite the loss to World Heavyweight Champion, Edge, Del Rio proves that he belongs in the main event picture.

Del Rio Is DineroJuly 17, 2011Again, facing odds capable of making a less assured Superstar buckle, Del Rio shines by clamoring atop seven other Superstars to win Raw’s Money in the Bank Ladder Match, and a guaranteed WWE Championship title opportunity.

Destiny DefinedAugust 14, 2011After CM Punk defeats John Cena to become the Undisputed WWE Champion, the shocking return of Kevin Nash leaves The Straight Edge Superstar in a heap of mid-ring unconsciousness. Enter the Mexican millionaire, who wastes no time cashing in his Money in the Bank title opportunity and fulfilling his destiny by capturing the WWE Championship.

Del Rio Conquers HellOctober 2, 2011One month after losing the WWE Championship to John Cena at Vengeance, Del Rio’s rematch takes place inside Hell in a Cell, with the added element of CM Punk thrown into the triple threat mix. Just like his first Royal Rumble, and first Money in the Bank match earlier in the year, Del Rio seals his first Cell triumph with a decisive victory, and nets his second WWE Title.
