WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: US TITLE MATCH; CONTRACT SIGNING; WYATT FAMILY IN ACTION; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-05-02 22:00:06

This week’s show starts off with a video package showing how we got to this Sunday’s match between Evolution and The Shield.

We are in Kansas City, Missouri and your announcers are John ‘Chief’ Layfield and Michael ‘Royal’ Cole.

Match Number Six: Dean Ambrose versus Curtis Axel versus Ryback versus Alberto Del Rio in a Fatal Four Way Match for the United States Championship

All three challengers surround Ambrose and Dean goes after Ryback and then Axel. Ambrose punches Axel but Del Rio kicks Ambrose and all three men work over Ambrose. Axel with a kick and Ryback with a punch. Del Rio chokes Dean in the ropes. Dean punches and chops Ryback but Axel and Del Rio work over Dean.

Ryback with kicks and then Del Rio sends Ambrose to the floor. Axel with a rollup on Del Rio for a near fall and Ryback wants to know why he did that. Del Rio with a rollup on Ryback for a near fall. Axel sends Del Rio to the floor and they go face to face until Ambrose attacks Ryback from behind. Dean kicks Axel in the back of the leg and Axel flips.

Ryback with a spinebuster and he sends Ambrose to the floor with some help from Axel. Axel and Ryback corner Del Rio and we go to commercial.

We are back and Ryback punches Del Rio in the corner and Axel with a punch as well followed by a chop. Ryback with a chop to Del Rio. Axel runs into an elbow from Del Rio and Del Rio avoids Ryback and Del Rio with a lungblower for a near fall. Axel kicks Del Rio. Axel with a knee to the head and he gets a near fall.

Ambrose punches Axel and Ryback punches Ambrose. Ambrose is sent to the floor and Ryback and Axel send Ambrose back first into the ringside barrier. Del Rio with a baseball slide to Ryback on the floor. Axel with a drop kick to Del Rio for a near fall. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker to Axel. Del Rio with a German suplex and bridge on Ryback for a near fall. Ambrose breaks up the cover. Ambrose punches Del Rio.

Ambrose with a Thesz Press and he punches Del Rio. Ryback picks up Ambrose but Ambrose slaps Ryback. Ryback goes over the top rope to the floor. Del Rio with a kick and punch. Ambrose with a kick and drop kick against the ropes. Axel with a forearm to the back of Ambrose’s head. Axel sets for the fisherman’s suplex but Ambrose escapes. Ambrose uses the ropes to rebound and he hits a lariat for a near fall.

Ryback pulls Ambrose to the floor and then he sends Ambrose into the Garcia Cubicle. Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri to Ryback. Axel with a fisherman’s suplex but Del Rio kicks out at two. Axel puts Del Rio on the turnbuckles and Axel with a chop before he tries for the superplex. Del Rio pushes Axel off and Axel is caught in the turnbuckles. Del Rio with a double stomp but Axel gets to the ropes to stop the count.

Del Rio pulls down the knee pad for the super kick but Axel moves. Ryback tries to clothesline Del Rio but Alberto moves and Ryback clotheslines Axel. Del Rio floats over into the cross arm breaker but Ryback picks up Del Rio and sets for the marching muscle buster and he hits it but Axel pulls Ryback off Del Rio.

Axel rolls into the ring and he crawls across the ring but Ambrose with a La Magistral on Axel for the three count.

Winner: Dean Ambrose

After the match, Ryback attacks Ambrose and so does Axel. Rollins and Reigns make their way to the ring and Rollins sends Axel to the floor while Reigns hits a spear on Ryback. Rollins with a suicide dive onto Axel. Reigns sets for the Superman Punch and Axel is rolled into the ring and Reigns hits the Superman Punch on Axel.

Ambrose and Rollins pick up Axel for the Cerberus Bomb.

We go to credits.
