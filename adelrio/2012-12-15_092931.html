SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Dec. 14, 2012

BY Alex Giannini

December 14, 2012

Can Kofi Kingston silence The Mexican Aristocrat?

Intercontinental Champion Kofi Kingston is set to take on Alberto Del Rio in what will assuredly be a blue brand blockbuster battle tonight on SmackDown. But can Kofi’s high-flying style counter The Mexican Aristocrat’s technical prowess? Or will Del Rio get a big win over the champion just days before Kofi will do battle against Wade Barrett with the title on the line at WWE TLC?
