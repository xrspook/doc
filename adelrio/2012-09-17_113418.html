WWE.com: World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio: photos

World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus is currently on a dominant run as titleholder. He prepares to defend his title against rival Alberto Del Rio.

The Mexican Aristocrat makes his entrance in his customary luxury automobile.

Before the match starts, SmackDown General Manager Booker T makes a shocking announcement: Sheamus' Brogue Kick is reinstated!

The Great White puts the Brogue Kick to use right away, nailing David Otunga with a big boot to the skull.

Del Rio goes off the top rope, as he lays it all on the line in his quest for WWE gold.

Del Rio grabs Sheamus' arm and torques it painfully in the excruciating Cross Armbreaker.

Del Rio takes the fight to Sheamus and nails the big man with a high kick against the ropes.

Del Rio again locks in the Cross Armbreaker, as Sheamus struggles to escape the brutal submission maneuver.

After hitting a searing Brogue Kick, Sheamus pins Del Rio to retain his title.

Ricardo Rodriguez helps Del Rio away from the ring.

Despite sustaining extensive damage and narrowly avoiding the Cross Armbreaker, Sheamus stands tall as World Heavyweight Champion.
