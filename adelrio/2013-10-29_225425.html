作者: xrspook

日期: 2013-10-29 22:54:25+08:00

分类: 点点文本

标签: alberto_del_rio, wwe, 2013, ppv, survivor_series, john_cena, whc, 消息, rey_mysterio

我觉得，rematch是在什么时候什么场合取决于Rey Mysterio何时归来。现在到Survivor Series 2013之间夹了一个欧洲巡演，欧洲巡演将在下周开始。如果Mysterio可以在下周归来，那么在这周Smackdown或下周的Raw就可以进行rematch了，但如果Rey Mysterio不能在欧洲巡演前归来，那么Alberto Del Rio的rematch就真的要拖到下个PPV SS。从WWE已发布的欧洲巡演信息看来，Mysterio不会参加欧洲巡演，so，你懂我意思的。

Plans For Alberto Del Rio's World Title Rematch, Mick Foley On If Damien Sandow Was Buried

By Marc Middleton | October 29, 2013

Word after WWE Hell In a Cell was that the Alberto Del Rio vs. John Cena rematch would take place before the Survivor Series pay-per-view on free TV. However, F4wOnline.com reports that word coming out of RAW last night was that Del Rio vs. Cena for the World Heavyweight Title will indeed take place at Survivor Series.
