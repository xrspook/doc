SmackDown Five-Point Preview: May 3, 2013

BY Alex Giannini

May 03, 2013

Can Ricardo Rodriguez silence Zeb Colter?

Coming off his epic Triple Threat Match victory over Zeb Colter and Big E Langston Monday on Raw — which allowed Alberto Del Rio to select the Ladder Match stipulation for the Triple Threat World Title Match at Extreme Rules — Ricardo Rodriguez heads into tonight’s SmackDown with the wind at his back to face Jack Swagger’s outspoken “Founding Father” one-on-one.

Can Ricardo once again silence — and dare we say, even humble — Colter tonight on the blue brand?
