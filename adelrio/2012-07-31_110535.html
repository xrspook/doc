WWE.com: U.S. Champion Santino Marella vs. Alberto Del Rio - Non-Title Match: photos

United States Champion Santino Marella prepares for a Non-Title Match against Alberto Del Rio.

Ricardo Rodriguez readies for his trademark introduction of The Mexican Aristocrat.

The top contender to Sheamus' World Heavyweight Championship approaches the squared circle.

Del Rio, a master of the Cross Armbreaker, methodically wears down Marella's arm.

After avoiding Marella's Cobra strike, Del Rio cinches in the Cross Armbreaker for the submission win.
