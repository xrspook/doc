WWE MAIN EVENT REPORT: TAG CHAMPS IN ACTION; DEL RIO ADDRESSES THE ANIMAL; KAITLYN'S SWAN SONG

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-01-08 21:00:00

We are in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and your announcers are Tom ‘Jim’s’ Phillips and The Miz.

Alberto Del Rio makes his way to the ring and we go to commercial.

We are back and we are now less than 2 weeks away from the return of Batista.

Match Number Two: Alberto Del Rio versus Justin Gabriel

Del Rio with a forearm and kicks to Gabriel. Del Rio with an Irish whip and kick in the corner. Del Rio chokes Gabriel in the corner. Del Rio with an Irish whip and Gabriel with a head scissors that sends Del Rio to the floor and Gabriel follows with a corkscrew pescado. Gabriel sends Del Rio back into the ring and he goes up top but Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri for a near fall.

Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Gabriel with punches to get out of the chin lock but Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall. Del Rio with punches to the head. Del Rio with a suplex and he floats over to get a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Gabriel with a forearm but Del Rio with a head butt.

Del Rio charges into the corner but Gabriel moves and Gabriel with a series of kicks to Del Rio. Gabriel with a spinning back heel kick and then he hits a cross body into the corner and goes to the apron for a springboard cross body and Gabriel gets a near fall.

Gabriel misses a splash into the corner and he gets caught in the ropes. Del Rio with a double stomp and then he waits for Gabriel to get up for the super kick and Del Rio gets the three count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Del Rio wants to know what is wrong with everyone. Everyone should be on their feet celebrating his victory. He calls everyone stupid. Every time Batista is on the Tron, you jump up and down and scream like little girls. It is just disgusting. He says that it is okay because at the end of the day, everyone is just like Batista . . . a bunch of filthy animals.
