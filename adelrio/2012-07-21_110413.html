WWE.com: Rey Mysterio returns to SmackDown: photos

SmackDown starts off with a bang as Rey Mysterio makes his Friday night return!

In his hometown of San Diego, Mysterio addresses the WWE Universe and says, officially, that he is back.

Alberto Del Rio crashes the party, though, telling Rey that he made a mistake by picking a fight with The Mexican Aristocrat Monday night on Raw SuperShow.

Business picks up when Dolph Ziggler arrives with his briefcase in tow.

Ziggler focuses in on Del Rio, saying that his plans to cash in at Money in the Bank were ruined by The Mexican Aristocrat.

When Ziggler and Del Rio turn their attention to Mysterio, The Ultimate Underdog gets a helping hand from World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus!

Sheamus and Mysterio clear the ring and send Del Rio and Ziggler scampering off.
