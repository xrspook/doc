MVP vs. Drew McIntyre

MVP defends the WWE legends.

Alberto Del Rio rolls onto SmackDown in style.

Del Rio says that Survivor Series will finally be good because of him.

When he boasts about beating WWE Hall of Famer Sergeant Slaughter...

...MVP takes exception.

Del Rio says that MVP holds the legends in such high esteem...

...because he will never be one himself.

When Del Rio says that he is already a legend, MVP is ready to fight.

Already facing Rey Mysterio later on, Del Rio says it's Drew McIntyre vs. MVP.

MVP and the "Chosen One" will be on opposing sides at Survivor Series

in the traditional Survivor Series Tag Team Elimination Match

captained by Mysterio and Del Rio.

With a huge boot to the head ...

... MVP scores an important win for Mysterio's team.

Survivor Series team captain Rey Mysterio comes before the WWE Universe.

He takes on the determined captain from the other team, Alberto Del Rio.

Before a winner can be decided, Del Rio's team attacks Mysterio.

Mysterio's team comes to the rescue.

SmackDown General Manager Theodore Long turns the match ...

... into a 10-Man Survivor Series Showdown Battle Royal.

Both Survivor Series teams look to punish their opponents ...

... before sending them over the top rope and out to the floor.

The Ultimate Underdog drops in on Swagger.

After a 619 by Mysterio ...

... Big Show takes out the trash.

Mysterio and Big Show give their Survivor Series team a huge "pick-me-up."
