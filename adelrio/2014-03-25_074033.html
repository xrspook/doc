WWE Live Event Results From NY (3/22): Cena In Action, Bob Backlund, Orton Defends

By Raj Giri | March 23, 2014 | Comments (34)

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader MTD for sending in these results from last night's WWE live event at Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Uniondale, NY:

Sheamus defeated Alberto Del Rio with the Brogue kick. This match was probably the match of the night (which isn't high praise folks). After the match, Sheamus calls into the ring a fan from the Make a Wish foundation, they both did the Sheamus chest bump; then Sheamus signed a lot of autographs for fans at ringside. Pretty cool to see a WWE wrestler do that for the fans. Sheamus seems to be a class act that is for sure, so much props to him.

3/22 WWE RESULTS FROM UNIONDALE, NY

By John Mantikas on 2014-03-22 22:19:13

WWE Live Event Results from Nassau Coliseum in Uniondale, NY

6) Del Rio v Sheamus. Sheamus comes out to a deafening reception This was a typical hard hitting match for Sheamus. At numerous points Del Rio attempted to run away, and on one occasion slammed Sheamus into the steel ring steps. Sheamus blocked a brogue kick that Del Rio attempted to steal. After a very entertaining match that lasted nearly 20 minutes, Sheamus picked up the win following the Brogue Kick. He celebrated the victory with a young kid from Make A Wish.

The venue was more than 3/4ths filled.
