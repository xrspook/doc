Ziggler Celebrates Birthday At WWE Event, RVD Teams With Punk & Sheamus, Cena Vs. Del Rio

By Marc Middleton | July 28, 2013

Triple H wasn't the only WWE Superstar to celebrate a birthday on Saturday as Dolph Ziggler turned 33 years old. After Ziggler lost a Street Fight to Alberto Del Rio at last night's live event in Macon, Dolph laid him out and began to celebrate. AJ Lee came out with a birthday cake and sang Happy Birthday. She tried to kiss Dolph but he smashed the cake in her face. Dolph then had Lilian Garcia and the fans sing Happy Birthday to end the show.
