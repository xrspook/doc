WWE MAIN EVENT REPORT: DEL RIO/ZIGGLER; KOFI/FANDANGO; TRIPLE H CLARIFIES THE TLC MATCH AT TLC; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-11-27 22:43:03

We are in Uncasville, Connecticut and your announcers are Josh ‘Irony of the location of tonight’s show’ Mathews and Alex ‘Happy Hanukkah’ Riley.

Match Number One: Dolph Ziggler versus Alberto Del Rio

They take some time before locking up as Ziggler plays to the crowd. The crowd starts to chant USA and Del Rio with a kick and side head lock. Del Rio holds on to the side head lock when Ziggler tries to send Del Rio off the ropes. Del Rio with a shoulder tackle followed by a kick. Del Rio with a snap mare and kick to the back for a near fall.

Del Rio with a side head lock. Ziggler with a punch followed by a drop kick. Ziggler with kicks in the corner followed by a snap mare. Ziggler with a series of elbow drops that he culminates with a delayed leaping elbow drop. Ziggler gets a near fall. Ziggler with a punch in the corner but he misses a splash when Del Rio moves. Del Rio with a kick to the head and he kicks Ziggler in the head.

Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio with a suplex and he floats over to get a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Ziggler with punches but Del Rio with head butts to Ziggler. Ziggler moves out of the way when Del Rio goes for a splash in the ropes. Del Rio goes to the apron and Ziggler with a drop kick that sends Del Rio to the floor.

Ziggler follows Del Rio to the floor and Del Rio Irish whips Ziggler shoulder first into the ringside barrier and we go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio with a double sledge from the turnbuckles and Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Ziggler with a punch but Del Rio with an Irish whip. Del Rio runs into an uppercut but Del Rio sends Ziggler into the air and Ziggler lands chest first on the mat. Del Rio mocks Ziggler and he kicks Dolph in the head.

Del Rio goes up top for another double sledge. Del Rio chokes Ziggler and he gets a near fall. Del Rio returns to the reverse chin lock. Ziggler with an elbow and Del Rio pulls Ziggler to the mat by his hair.

Del Rio goes up top and he comes off but Ziggler with a drop kick and both men are down. Del Rio and Ziggler exchange punches. Del Rio with a kick but Ziggler with a flying clothesline. Ziggler with a Stinger Splash and he connects. Ziggler stays on the turnbuckles for punches to Del Rio. Ziggler with a neck breaker and he gets a near fall.

Ziggler misses a Fameasser and Del Rio with a German suplex and bridge for a near fall. Del Rio with a kick to the head. Del Rio sets for the running step up enzuigiri but Ziggler ducks and then Dolph with a Fameasser for a near fall.

Ziggler tries for the DDT but Del Rio pushes him away. Del Rio with an enzuigiri for a near fall. Del Rio slams Ziggler’s knee into the mat and then he goes up top. Ziggler leaps to the turnbuckles and hits a Super X Factor but Del Rio kicks out. Ziggler mocks Del Rio’s super kick set up and he tries for a super kick but Del Rio avoids it and Del Rio gets a near fall. Ziggler with a DDT and he gets a near fall.

Ziggler goes up top and Del Rio crotches Ziggler. Del Rio with forearms to the back and then Del Rio tries for an inverted superplex and he hits it. Del Rio can only get a two count. Del Rio tries to figure out what he will have to do to win this match. Del Rio signals for the cross arm breaker.

Ziggler with a neck breaker for a near fall. Ziggler tries for a sleeper but Del Rio holds on to the ropes. Del Rio with a power bomb to counter a Fameasser and then he floats over into the cross arm breaker and Ziggler taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
