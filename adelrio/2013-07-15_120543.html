WWE.com: Dolph Ziggler vs. Alberto Del Rio - World Heavyweight Championship Match: photos

Chants of "Let's go, Ziggler" rain down on Dolph Ziggler from the moment he enters the Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia for his match against World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio.

Using the same strategy that worked for him at WWE Payback, Del Rio targets Ziggler's head throughout the contest.

A misstep by Del Rio opens the door for The Showoff to go on offense.

Ziggler catches Del Rio coming off the top rope with a dropkick ...

... but then, unexpectedly, Divas Champion AJ Lee skips to ringside to watch her "Ziggy."

Her presence immediately distracts Ziggler, who tells the pint-sized powder keg that he's got the match under control.

Del Rio takes advantage of The Showoff's gaffe.

The World Heavyweight Champion prepares to thrust kick Ziggler ...

... but before he can, AJ jumps in the ring and attacks him with her Divas Title, prompting the referee to disqualify Ziggler.

An upset Ziggler can't hide his frustration, yelling "I should be champion."

While the World Heavyweight Champion survives to fight another day ...

... the relationship between WWE's power couple turns icy.

As Ziggler exits the arena, the WWE Universe in Philadelphia chants "You screwed Ziggler" at AJ, who's left alone in the ring.
