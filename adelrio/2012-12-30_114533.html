SPOILERS For Monday's WWE RAW

By Marc Middleton | December 29, 2012

Thanks to Brandon Thomas and @kadium for the following WWE RAW spoilers from tonight's tapings in Washington, DC:

Big Show comes out and wants to defend his World Heavyweight Title against Ricardo Rodriguez. Alberto Del Rio is in the suit at ringside while Ricardo comes in driving his car. Del Rio ends up making the save and attacks Big Show. Ricardo did get some shots in but Show wins by DQ.

Complete WWE Monday Night Raw Taping Results (SPOILERS)

Posted by Larry Csonka on 12.29.2012

WWE is taping Monday's New Year's Eve edition of RAW tonight from the Verizon Center in Washington, DC. Here are the ongoing results:

World Heavyweight champion Big Show defeats Ricardo Rodriguez by disqualification after Alberto Del Rio interfered and attacked Big Show. In a bit of comedy, Del Rio came down to the ring in a suit before the match and Ricardo came out driving a fancy sports car.
