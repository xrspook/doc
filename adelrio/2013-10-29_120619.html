Raw results: Sandow rolls the dice, The Wyatts pick their targets and Kane chooses his destiny

BY ANTHONY BENIGNO

October 28, 2013

Alberto Del Rio addressed World Heavyweight Champion John Cena

Alberto Del Rio is not impressed. Still fuming from his loss at WWE Hell in a Cell, The Essence of Excellence didn’t mince words in the slightest when he promised a world of hurt for The Champ in their coming rematch. “I’m gonna make you pay,” said the seething Del Rio. “I’m gonna hurt you and I’m gonna hurt you bad … The World Heavywight Title belongs to me, Alberto Del Rio.”
