WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio to pick World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus’ opponent: photos

The Mexican Superstar says The Celtic Warrior will be very sorry when he captures his World Title.

World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus is ready to face Alberto Del Rio in 16 days at No Way Out.

As he talks about it with the WWE Universe, David Otunga interrupts.

The Harvard graduate orders The Great White to apologize for shoving GM John Laurinaitis on the May 21 edition of Raw SuperShow.

The proud Irish Superstar does not respond well to orders.

The new No. 1 contender to the World Title, Alberto Del Rio, joins the party.

Otunga informs Del Rio that he has been given the right to choose Sheamus' SmackDown opponent.

The Great White does apologize in the end, not for shoving Big Johnny, but for the Brogue kick he was about to hit Otunga with.
