What Happened After Raw Went Off the Air

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 09.18.2012

After Raw went off the air, WWE set up a steel cage match for the WWE Title.

WWE champion C.M. Punk beat John Cena in a steel cage match to retain the WWE Title. The match ended when Dolph Ziggler and Del Rio interfered, then Sheamus and Randy Orton interfered to even the odds. Punk then slipped out of the cage during the mayhem to retain.

Afterward, I left, but Ziggler was left alone in the ring presumably for the sole purpose of taking a mele of finishers from the faces. Everyone goes home happy!

Another Report From When Raw Went Off The Air

Posted by Larry Csonka on 09.18.2012

After Raw went off the air, the crowd got to see a cage match for the WWE title between Cena and Punk. It was a quick 5 minute match before Dolph Ziggler and Alberto Del Rio came into the cage and starting double teaming Cena while Punk tried to escape over the cage.

Super Cena got free from the double-team and stopped Punk. Sheamus and Orton came into the cage to help Cena and Punk and crawled out of cage like a p**sy and won the match.

Alberto climbed over the cage while Punk crawled out and the Ziggler took Cena, Orton and Sheamus finishing moves sending crowd home happy.

Sheamus stayed and signed autographs around ringside including one for my son.
