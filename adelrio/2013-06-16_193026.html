WWE House Show Result 06.15.13 - Bloomington, Illinois

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 06.16.2013

Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriquez defeated Big E Langston and Dolph Ziggler. A good match with lots of showboating at first with Del Rio holding Ziggler's shirt and Big E running at it like a bull. Yes, really. The crowd yelled Ole after each time. Ricardo had a nice bump or two leaping off the top rope and over the ropes to the outside onto Big E.

Notes: A decent turnout in Bloomington. Probably 5-6,000 people tops. A good warm up for the pay-per-view tomorrow night.
