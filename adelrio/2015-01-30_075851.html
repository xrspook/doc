Sydal, Lethal & El Patrón will be signing Autographs in Dearborn & Dayton

This weekend Ring of Honor returns to Dearborn, Michigan and Dayton, Ohio.

Prior to the beginning of both events there will be an autograph signing and we can now confirm who will take part ….

ROH World Television Champion Jay Lethal, #Reborn Matt Sydal and International Star Alberto El Patrón will be signing autographs & taking photos with the fans.

Pricing is as follows ... $20 per wrestler - you will receive one autographed item and a posed photo

Doors open at 6:00pm both nights and the autograph signing will run from 6:15pm to 7:15pm.
