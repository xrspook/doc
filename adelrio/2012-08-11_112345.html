WWE.com: Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio - World Heavyweight Championship Match: photos

Nine days before SummerSlam, SmackDown comes to Houston ...

... the hometown of SmackDown General Manager Booker T.

After a Spinaroonie ...

... Booker says, "Houston, we have a problem."

In regards to World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus "borrowing" Alberto Del Rio's car on Raw ...

... SmackDown's GM called for him to apologize.

The Mexican Aristocrat says that isn't good enough and that Booker had better do something about it before he does.

The Great White proposes that they have their World Heavyweight Title Match ...

... on SmackDown!

Del Rio agrees.

As a result, the first World Title Match of the Booker T era happens tonight!

Alberto Del Rio emerges for his World Heavyweight Title Match ...

... against The Celtic Warrior, Sheamus!

The introductions go as planned ...

.. but before the bell can ring, "police" arrive on the scene.

At first, Del Rio claims he doesn't want them there.

But, with a sudden smile by the challenger, it becomes clear that the men dressed as police ...

... are really just hired henchmen of The Mexican Aristocrat.

Though The Great White battles against the odds ...

... they eventually catch up with him.

Del Rio continues his sinister assault of the World Champion.

In the end, Sheamus is left on the canvas by the treachery of his ruthless attacker.
