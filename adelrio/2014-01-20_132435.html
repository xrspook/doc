Pictures By Me: WWE House Show [January 19th, 2014]

Match 6) Singles Match:

Sin Cara Vs. Alberto Del Rio

This match was actually really good, and really exciting. Del Rio played the heel exceedingly strong in this one, with Sin Cara playing the “clean, by-the-rules” babyface. Sin Cara did a lot of high-flying moves, as usual, and Del Rio did a lot of kicking and catching Sin Cara only to slam him. Del Rio won with the cross armbreaker.
