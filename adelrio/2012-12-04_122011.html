WWE Raw 2012-12-03 非官方战报

作者: xrspook

日期: 2012-12-04 12:20:11+08:00

分类: 点点文本

标签: alberto_del_rio, wwe, 2012, raw, sin_cara, 战报

COMPLETE WWE RAW REPORT: THE SHIELD ARE BUSY; IS CM PUNK A LIAR?; IS THE US TITLE IMPORTANT AGAIN?; MATCHES AT PPV COMBINED FOR TELEVISION; THE BRIEFCASE IS ON THE LINE IN LESS THAN TWO WEEKS; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-12-03 20:05:45

Previously on WWE Television, Big Show destroyed a chair. John Cena destroyed a bathroom. On Smackdown, Big Show, Sheamus, John Cena, and Dolph Ziggler were all in the ring. Who would have thought they will be in a tag match tonight? What will happen when the Shield reveals its goals? Is Punk the brains behind this new group? All of this and more, well more of this tonight on Raw.

This week, Raw is in Greensboro, North Carolina and your announcers are Michael ‘It is beginning to feel a lot like Starrcade’ Cole and Jerry ‘Crockett Cup’ Lawler.

Match Number Five: Sin Cara versus Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez)

They lock up and Del Rio backs Cara into the corner. Del Rio with a clean break and then he kicks Cara in the leg and head. Del Rio with a slam and he gets a near fall as Cara looks for his special lighting. Cara with an arm drag or two and an arm bar. Del Rio backs Cara into the corner and connects with shoulders until the referee warns him.

Del Rio with a savate kick for a near fall. Del Rio tries to send Cara to the floor but Cara bounces off the ropes and he hits an arm drag. Cara goes to the ropes and uses them for leverage on an arm drag. Cara gets a near fall. Cara with kicks to the chest. Cara with a running kick and he gets a near fall.

Del Rio with a suplex and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Del Rio sends Cara to the mat and he kicks Cara in the back. Del Rio chokes Cara in the ropes and tries to start to work on the arm. Cara with a drop kick to the head and then Del Rio sends Cara to the apron. Cara avoids a splash into the ropes and Cara with a kick to the midsection.

Cara with a rana off the apron and Del Rio goes into the ringside barrier. Ricardo checks on Del Rio. We go to commercial.

We are back and Cara with an arm bar but Del Rio with kicks and a punch. Del Rio misses a clothesline. Cara with a rana for a near fall. Del Rio goes to the floor. Cara kicks Del Rio in the leg and then he sends Del Rio back into the ring. Cara tries for a springboard move but Del Rio pushes Cara to the floor and Cara hits the announce table. Del Rio sends Cara into the ringside barrier.

Del Rio sends Cara back into the ring and Del Rio gets a near fall. Del Rio with a boot to the back and then he puts his knee in Cara’s back. Del Rio goes after Cara’s mask. Cara with punches but Del Rio with a German suplex and bridge for a near fall. Del Rio sets for a charge into the corner but Cara moves out of the way. Cara with a kick and then he hits a tornado DDT and both men are down.

Del Rio and Cara exchange punches and Del Rio gains control. Cara with a handspring back elbow and then he hits a head scissors take down. Del Rio sends Cara to the apron and Cara with an enzuigiri. Cara goes up top and hits a cross body for a near fall. Cara hits the Asai DDT and then he goes to the turnbuckles. Cara misses the Swanton but Cara has La Mistica blocked by Del Rio. Del Rio floats over and applies the cross arm breaker and Cara taps out.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
