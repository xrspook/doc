SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Feb. 01, 2013

BY Alex Giannini

February 01, 2013

How will Alberto Del Rio answer Big Show’s brutal Raw assault?

On Raw, the WWE Universe bore witness to one of the most contemptible attacks in recent memory, as Big Show laid waste to both World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio and his compadre Ricardo Rodriguez. After trapping Del Rio on the ropes with duct tape, The World’s Largest Athlete proceeded to take out his frustrations over losing his World Title rematch at Royal Rumble on Ricardo, before knocking the World Heavyweight Champion out cold with the KO Punch.

While it’s certain that Del Rio will attempt to make Big Show answer for his actions, the question of how the Mexican champion will do that remains. One thing’s for sure, though: The giant has made an already heated rivalry very, very personal, and now the possible repercussions have been amplified.
