SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Feb. 22, 2013

BY Jake Grate

February 22, 2013

How will Del Rio respond to Swagger and Colter’s incendiary verbal assault?

After Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter’s controversial “State of the Union” address on Monday’s Raw, World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio Touted that he will have a rebuttal to his No. 1 contender’s strong words tonight. What might “The Essence of Excellence” have to say in response to the “Real American” he will face at WrestleMania 29?

The proud Mexican champion — who is in the midst of an incredibly successful title reign after toppling Big Show at Elimination Chamber — could have more planned than simply exercising his First Amendment right to free speech against the vision of “Jack Swagger’s America.” But no matter what Del Rio has in store for Swagger, the rivalry between the two Superstars is sure to grow more intense on The Road to WrestleMania.
