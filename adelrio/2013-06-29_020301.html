SmackDown Five-Point Preview: June 28, 2013

BY Michael Burdick

June 28, 2013

Is "Fiesta Del Rio" destined to become an all-out slugfest?

Alberto Del Rio has announced he will throw a "Fiesta Del Rio" tonight in recognition of his recapturing of the World Heavyweight Championship at WWE Payback. However, one Superstar who is definitely not in a festive mood is the former titleholder, Dolph Ziggler.

Poised for a World Title rematch at Money in the Bank, the determined Showoff has promised to crash the titleholder’s party. And if the volatile back-and-forth melee that has taken place between the two competitors since their last pay-per-view contest is any indication, everybody had better prepare for a wild ride.
