SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Nov. 15, 2013

BY Jake Grate

November 15, 2013

Should Cena put himself in “arm’s” way?

World Heavyweight Champion John Cena has been challenged to an Arm Wrestling Contest on tonight’s SmackDown by his vicious No. 1 contender, Alberto Del Rio. The questions is: Will The Champ answer the call to “arms,” particularly after the Mexican Superstar attacked him on Raw and locked the Cenation leader’s injured arm in the devastating Cross Armbreaker through a steel chair?

The challenger’s talents – and mean streak – are unquestioned, and WWE could very well have a new World Heavyweight Champion after Survivor Series on Nov. 24. With that in mind, we feel it would not be wise for Cena to risk further injury by stepping up to Del Rio’s challenge on SmackDown – no matter how gutsy he may be.
