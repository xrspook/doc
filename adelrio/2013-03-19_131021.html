March 18, 2013

Need guidance when it comes to your love life? Unclear whether or not to pursue a red-hot romance? Fear not! WWE's Love Horoscopes will move those matters of the heart out of the darkness and into the light. Check out your horoscope to see what the future holds and to see which Superstars share your sign. You're just a click away from uncovering your desire's destiny.

GEMINI (May 22 - June 21)

Thanks to your dual nature, you’re not always the easiest person to get to know. That intrinsic mystery makes it difficult for potential suitors to get close. To facilitate a fulfilling courtship, you must put aside the cynicism and negativity inherent in your personality, much like Gemini Alberto Del Rio was able to do, and focus on all the positivity that resides within. Once you do, the world will open up to you, and potential love interests will be attracted to you like Superstars to a World Heavyweight Title.

Notable Superstars who share this sign: Daniel Bryan (May 22), Alberto Del Rio (May 25), Natalya (May 27), Drew McIntyre (June 6), Mark Henry (June 12)

Your WWE Horoscope for 2013

“What’s your sign?”

Rather than a cheesy pick-up line from the ’70s delivered through a mustache Cody Rhodes would be proud of, WWE.com really wants to know. After all, we consulted the stars and positions of the planets before formulating a series of horoscopes, applicable to both Superstars and the WWE Universe. The following mystical prophecies are based on traditional tropical zodiac signs. So, go forth, read and be amazed as WWE.com reveals what lies ahead for you in 2013.

http://www.wwe.com/inside/overtheropes/your-wwe-horoscope

GEMINI (May 22 – June 21)

The duality of your spirit is well illustrated by the astrological twins of Gemini. Like many WWE tag teams, the twins work in tandem to create a winning combination. Much like Gemini Daniel Bryan, you balance your easily distracted nature with an intellect and sense of humor that could pack an arena. You possess a knack for both style and elegance and tend to reach out to people using proficient social skills. In the year ahead, you will put those skills to the test to reach long awaited goals. Like any good ladder match, however, there will be obstacles on your journey to the top. Just remember to endure!

Notable Superstars who share this sign: Daniel Bryan (May 22), Alberto Del Rio (May 25), Natalya (May 27), Drew McIntyre (June 6), Mark Henry (June 12)
