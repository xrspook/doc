3/16 SMACKDOWN BRAND RESULTS FROM HAMMOND, IN

By Joe Costello Jr. on 2013-03-17 12:32:18

Quick results:

Match 5: Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) beats Big Show in a Last Man Standing

We are asked to tweet votes in on whether Ricardo Rodriguez should be allowed to stay at ringside during the Last Man Standing bout between Alberto Del Rio and The Big Show.

Ricardo Rodriguez was voted in the match by the WWE Universe.

A titan tron video pops up from Del Rio and Rodriguez, once again spoofing their vignette from Raw last Monday. In it, they talk about how the worst people to come across the border are Canadians, and they keep smuggling in Maple Syrup.

Match 5: Last Man Standing Match for the World Heavyweight Championship Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) vs. The Big Show

Ricardo asks us all to help introduce Alberto. We do. After a few minutes, Alberto gets good cheers for bringing a chair in to beat down Show for a bit. Later, Show gets good cheers for chokeslamming Del Rio through a table. Another table is brought in, and Show sets it up in the corner. Show attempted to powerbomb ADR through the table, instead, Del Rio turns it into a 'rana and sends Show through the table. Del Rio up around the 8 count, but Big Show was down for the count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

Hammond was a hot crowd tonight, and were into pretty much everything and everyone. Khali and Tatsu being over as much as they were surprised me, as was the heat for McGillicutty. Being this close to Chicago, though, shouldn't be surprised by how good the crowd was.

WWE returns to the area for Payback on June 16th.

WWE House Show Results 03.16.13 - Hammond, Indiana

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 03.17.2013

The arena was packed to the brim - Hammond Civic Center is not the most comfortable spot to watch an event, but nonetheless, legitimate sell-out. Probably 5,000.

(5) World Hvt. champion Alberto Del Rio beat Big Show in a Last Man Standing match to retain the World Title. Del Rio delivered a huracanrana, in which Big Show barely hit the base of the table and broke it. He couldn't meet the ten count.
