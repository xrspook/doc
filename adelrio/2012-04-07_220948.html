Sheamus vs. Alberto Del Rio

If Alberto Del Rio can defeat new World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus, he will earn a World Title opportunity.

This stipulation makes the match intense from the beginning.

The Great White is now a three-time World Champion and has no intention of letting it slip away.

Del Rio may be aristocratic, but he is still very dangerous.

The Celtic Warrior just likes to take heads off.

When Del Rio tries to use a chair, the World Heavyweight Champion ends up with it in his own hand.

It looks to the referee as if Sheamus hit Del Rio with it and he quickly disqualifies the Irish Superstar.

After losing the match, Sheamus takes things too far.

Del Rio gets the victory and with it a World Title opportunity ...

... while the referee suffers the temper of The Great White.
