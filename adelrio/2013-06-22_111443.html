Power Rankings: June 22, 2013

Alberto Del Rio

It was vicious. It was brutal. It was uncomfortable to watch. But at the end of the match, Alberto Del Rio held high the World Heavyweight Championship and Dolph Ziggler was left writhing in pain at his feet. The means by which Del Rio reclaimed the World Title — zoning in on The Showoff’s head to take advantage of Ziggler’s recently suffered concussion — may be questionable. But the result speaks for itself, and Del Rio once again has championship gold around his waist.
