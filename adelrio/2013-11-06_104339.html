What Happened After RAW Went Off The Air: Big Show Helped, John Cena Defends Title

By Raj Giri | November 04, 2013 | Comments (161)

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com readers Jorge Ziranda and Dustin Dula ( @DustDul ) for sending in these notes from tonight's RAW taping at the Bon Secours Wellness Arena in Greenville, South Carolina:

After RAW went off the air, medics tended to The Big Show while the heels posed at the top of the ramp.

Alberto Del Rio then came back out and demanded a title match against John Cena. Cena came out and said that Del Rio needs a fancy introduction, and since no one was here to do it, he would. Cena did his usual comedy routine. As he was rolling the r in rio, Del Rio attacked him. After a short match, Cena wins via the AA.

After the match, Cena said that the fans cheers are his real theme music. He thanked everyone for coming and it's over.
