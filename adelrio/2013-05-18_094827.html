WWE News: Plans For Dolph Ziggler & World Title, Reaction to Swagger Regarding Ziggler's Concussion

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 05.17.2013

WWE currently has no plans to strip the World Title from Dolph Ziggler, even if he has to miss an extended amount of time due to his concussion. There is still a plan to run the Triple Threat Ladder match that was set for Extreme Rules, perhaps at SummerSlam. Thus, expect Alberto Del Rio and Jack Swagger to stay in the World Title picture for the summer.

As far as Swagger goes, there is no heat on him backstage regarding Ziggler's concussion.
