WWE Raw 2013-07-22 非官方战报

作者: xrspook

日期: 2013-07-23 09:59:14+08:00

分类: 点点文本

标签: alberto_del_rio, wwe, 2013, sheamus, whc, raw, 战报

*WWE RAW REPORT: 40 PLUS MINUTES OF DANIEL BRYAN; CM PUNK MAKES IT OFFICIAL FOR SUMMERSLAM; WE HAD A CONTRACT SIGNING; MORE WITH MARK HENRY, THE USOS, AND THE SHIELD; TWO BIG MATCHES ANNOUNCED FOR NEXT WEEK'S RAW *

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-07-22 21:30:05

CM Punk was attacked by Paul Heyman at Money in the Bank. Paul explained why he did it on Raw. Then CM Punk and Brock Lesnar faced off in the ring and around the ring. We also found out that John Cena would be challenging Daniel Bryan at SummerSlam.

We are in Austin, Texas and your announcers are John ‘Deep in the Heart of Texas’ Layfield, Michael ‘What’ Cole, and Jerry ‘I am the once and current King’ Lawler.

Alberto Del Rio and Sheamus walk in the back and we go to commercial.

Match Number One: Alberto Del Rio versus Sheamus in a Non Title Match

They lock up and Sheamus with a side head lock and shoulder tackle. Sheamus with another shoulder tackle but Del Rio with a kick and punch and then he kicks Sheamus in the injured leg. They lock up and Del Rio with a kick to the leg and then he punches Sheamus but Sheamus with punches in the corner. Sheamus with a short arm clothesline and he gets a near fall.

Sheamus sends Del Rio into the turnbuckles and he connects with shoulders in the corner. Del Rio with an Irish whip but he runs into an elbow and he hits a swinging neck breaker for a near fall. Del Rio with a kick to the head. Sheamus punches Del Rio but Del Rio with a knee to the hip and he chokes Sheamus in the ropes.

Del Rio with punches but Sheamus with forearms to the back and then he hits a suplex and gets a near fall. Del Rio with a drop kick to the injured leg and then he kicks Sheamus until the referee pulls him out of the corner. Del Rio goes to the turnbuckles but Sheamus punches him. Del Rio tries for the cross arm breaker in the ropes but Sheamus pulls him up and punches him as we go to commercial.

We are back and Del Rio with a German suplex and bridge for a near fall. Del Rio kicks Sheamus in the head and we see footage from the break when Del Rio sent Sheamus’ injured leg into the ring steps.

Sheamus punches Del Rio but Del Rio with head butts that stagger Sheamus but Del Rio feels it too. Del Rio misses a kick to the head and Sheamus with a tilt-a-whirl power slam.

We see Damien Sandow watching in the back with the briefcase while Del Rio kicks Sheamus in the leg. Sheamus with punches but Del Rio with kicks. Sheamus blocks a kick and then he punches Del Rio in the corner. The referee pulls Sheamus out of the corner and Del Rio with a head butt. Sheamus with two double sledges followed by a shoulder in the corner and a knee lift and a flying back elbow for a near fall.

Del Rio with a lungblower and he gets a near fall. Del Rio kicks Sheamus in the head and then he chokes Sheamus in the ropes. Del Rio goes for a splash in the ropes but Del Rio stays on the apron. Sheamus puts Del Rio in the ropes and it is forearm time across the chest. Del Rio drop Sheamus on the top rope but Sheamus with a uranage back breaker but he uses the injured leg and he can only get a near fall.

Sheamus struggles to get back to his feet and then he goes up top but Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri and he gets a near fall. Sheamus gets Del Rio on his shoulders and he hits the Finlay slam but Sheamus cannot continue the momentum. Sheamus looks around and it is time to make his chest the same color as his leg. Sheamus misses the Brogue Kick and Del Rio with a super kick to the injured leg.

Del Rio tries to float over but Sheamus sends him into the turnbuckles. Sheamus cannot get Del Rio up for White Noise and Del Rio gets the three count with a rollup.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

We are back and we are told that Rob Van Dam will face Alberto Del Rio on Smackdown this week.
