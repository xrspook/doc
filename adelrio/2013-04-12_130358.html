SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Apr. 12, 2013

BY Michael Burdick

April 12, 2013

Who will step up to challenge The Showoff?

Speaking of returns, Dolph Ziggler will walk back onto SmackDown tonight as the new World Heavyweight Champion following his successful cash-in of the Money in the Bank contract.

But because there is no rest for the Superstar that sits on the top of the mountain, who will rise up to try and knock Ziggler back off again? Will Alberto Del Rio be clear of the leg injury that contributed to him losing the title Monday? What about Jack Swagger, the ruthless competitor who hurt the former champion in the first place?

Considering the firepower that currently frequents SmackDown, it is also worth mentioning a number of other candidates that will be in striking distance — including several former World Champions like Kane, Daniel Bryan, Big Show, Chris Jericho, Sheamus and Randy Orton.
