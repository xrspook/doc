WWE.com: Big Show, Randy Orton and Alberto Del Rio all look to challenge Sheamus for the World Heavyweight Title: photos

WWE.com: Randy Orton vs. Big Show – World Heavyweight Championship No. 1 Contender Match: photos

Four days after knocking out Brodus Clay and Tensai with the WMD, Big Show returns to SmackDown.

He explains how his 45-second World Heavyweight Title reign last year ...

... and Daniel Bryan losing that title in 18 seconds to Sheamus at WrestleMania ...

... was a turning point in his career.

In short, the giant wants the World Title back.

He is soon interrupted by Randy Orton ...

... the very Superstar he will face in a World Title No. 1 Contender's Match tonight.

Orton says that if Big Show thinks he will stand in the way of his 10th World Title reign ...

... he "won't be standing much longer."

Show says they will see whether someone will get "bit by the RKO or hit with a KO."

Alberto Del Rio jumps in, saying that the two Superstars need to get to the back of the line.

But, when he tried to get in the ring, The Viper hit the RKO on The Mexican Aristocrat.

Will Orton or Big Show face Sheamus for the World Heavyweight Title at WWE Hell in a Cell?

Big Show emerges for a World Title No. 1 Contender's Match against Randy Orton.

Instead of The Viper, Alberto Del Rio arrives, moments after taking Orton out in a vicious sneak attack.

The injured Apex Predator storms in, taking down his attacker.

He insists on battling The World's Largest Athlete for a chance to face Sheamus at Hell in a Cell.

From the opening bell, Big Show brutalizes the nine-time World Champion.

At every turn, Orton powers back, but the injury to his midsection is too great.

Despite escaping a chokeslam and hitting the RKO, The Viper eventually falls to his huge adversary.

After arguing with a member of the WWE Universe, Big Show returns to the ring ...

... laying out Orton with the WMD.
