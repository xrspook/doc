3/25 RAW Breakdown: Legends Segment With Rock And Cena, The Shield Six-Man, More

By Marc Middleton | March 28, 2013

As noted, the March 25th WWE RAW did a 3.21 cable rating with 4.61 million viewers.

In the segment breakdown, the show opened strong with CM Punk and The Undertaker doing a 3.39 quarter rating. Dolph Ziggler vs. Chris Jericho lost 441,000 viewers. Fandango attacking Chris Jericho and Sheamus, Randy Orton and Big Show brawling with The Shield gained 117,000 viewers. Mark Henry vs. The Usos lost 54,000 viewers. Alberto Del Rio vs. Antonio Cesaro with Jack Swagger's attack at 9pm gained 371,000 viewers for a 3.28 quarter rating.

Kane and Daniel Bryanvs. Darren Young and Titus O'Neil lost 241,000 viewers. Triple H's promo lost 6,000 viewers. The Miz vs. Wade Barrett gained 71,000 viewers. Justin Gabriel, Zack Ryder and The Great Khali vs. The Shield in the 10pm timeslot gained 187,000 viewers for a 3.29 quarter rating - a weak gain for 10pm.

Cody Rhodes and Damien Sandow vs. Brodus Clay and Tensai plus Ryback vs. 3MB lost 102,000 viewers. Kaitlyn vs. AJ Lee lost another 257,000 viewers. The Legends Q&A segment with The Rock and John Cena gained 615,000 viewers for a 3.47 rating.
