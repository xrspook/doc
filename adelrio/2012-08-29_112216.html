SPOILERS WWE SmackDown Results For This Friday

By Raj Giri August 28, 2012

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Gregory Cabual for sending in these results from tonight's WWE SmackDown taping in Green Bay, WI:

Alberto Del Rio is in the ring for a promo and talks trash about Sheamus. Kane enters the ring. Kane apologizes to Josh Matthews for throwing him over the barrier at SummerSlam. Kane vs. Del Rio is up next.

Del Rio pinned Kane after Ricardo Rodriguez interfered. Kane then attacked Matthews after the match and leaves him laying.

Dark Match

Sheamus defeated Randy Orton and Alberto Del Rio in a triple threat match. Sheamus pinned Del Rio after hitting him with a Brogue kick.

Smackdown Spoilers

In the ring, Josh Mathews interviewed Alberto Del Rio, who was accompanied by Ricardo Rodriguez. Del Rio drove a car in, put over his win last week against Orton, and his match with Sheamus. Del Rio went for Mathews until Kane came out. Mathews ran and Kane said his therapist wants him to apologize to Josh. Pretty funny. Teddy Long came out and booked an impromptu match.

Alberto Del Rio defeated Kane. Del Rio won thanks to a Ricardo distraction. After the match Kane attacked Josh again and gave him a chokeslam.

Complete WWE Smackdown Taping Results (SPOILERS)

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 08.28.2012

In the ring, Josh Mathews interviewed Alberto Del Rio, who was accompanied by Ricardo Rodriguez. Del Rio drove a car in, put over his win last week against Orton, and his match with Sheamus. Del Rio went for Mathews until Kane came out. Mathews ran and Kane said his therapist wants him to apologize to Josh. Pretty funny. Teddy Long came out and booked an impromptu match.

Alberto Del Rio defeated Kane. Del Rio won thanks to a Ricardo distraction. After the match Kane attacked Josh again and gave him a chokeslam. I remember seeing a hell of a match in the XWF here between Kid Kash and Josh Mathews, but he didn't take the slam well.

Dark Match

Sheamus defeated Randy Orton and Alberto Del Rio in a Triple Threat for the World Hvt. Championship. Not a bad dark match at all. It went 7 minutes. Del Rio accidentally kicked Ricardo, which the crowd loved, and Sheamus got the pin. Sheamus and Lilian went around the crowd ringside to close out the night.
