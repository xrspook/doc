WWE RAW REPORT: THE RANDY ORTON ELIMINATION CHAMBER CHALLENGE CONTINUES; TENSION BUILDS FOR SHIELD VERSUS WYATTS; THE HALL OF FAME HAS A NEW INDUCTEE; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-02-10 20:11:14

Tonight’s show starts off with a graphic in Memory of Frank Bullock.

We are in Los Angeles, California and your announcers are Michael ‘Sophia’ Cole, John ‘Dorothy’ Layfield, and Jerry ‘Blanche’ Lawler.

Match Number Four: Dolph Ziggler versus Alberto Del Rio

Del Rio with a kick to the leg and punches to the head. Ziggler with a pelvic thrust and strut followed by a drop kick. Ziggler with elbow drops to the chest and he finishes it off with a delayed leaping elbow drop. Ziggler gets a near fall. Ziggler goes for the Zig Zag but Del Rio pushes him off. Del Rio tries for the tilt-a-whirl back breaker but Ziggler avoids it. Ziggler with a Fameasser for a near fall.

Del Rio with an arm breaker followed by a super kick for the three count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio

After the match, Del Rio thinks about it and he puts Ziggler in the cross arm breaker.

Batista’s music plays and he skips to the ring and he takes Del Rio down and follows him to the floor. Batista sends Del Rio into the ring steps. Batista does some rearranging of the announce table and Batista sends Del Rio into the ringside barrier. Batista sets for a Batista Bomb through the table and he does it.

In case you want to know how to order the WWE Network, here are the Bellas to explain the network to you.

We go to commercial.

We are back and Batista is walking in the back. Hunter stops Batista and asks him if he has a second. He tells Dave that he cannot go off like he did on Del Rio. Batista says Del Rio deserved it.

Hunter says that things have changed. This is a publicly traded company now so if something bad goes on, it is his neck. He says that he is fitting Dave in on what is best for business.

Batista tells Hunter that Alberto is his business.

Hunter gives Batista a match against Alberto at Elimination Chamber.

Batista says that things must have really changed around here.
