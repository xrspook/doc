Alberto Del Rio Destroys Action Comics #1 At Comic Con

IGN’s Eric Goldman is currently at the San Diego Comic Con and had been live-tweeting the panel when Alberto made an apperance.

Alberto Del Rio just burst into panel and is facing off with Sheamus! Many “Si! Si! Si!” chants.

Del Rio is mocking audience for loving superheroes and wearing costumes, while he says he’s a real life superhero.

Del Rio takes out an Action Comics #1, saying he spent 2 million on it last week. “To you, it means everything! To me, it means nothing!”

And yes, he then tears it up, saying he’s giving a piece to everyone.
