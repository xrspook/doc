WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: SHIELD IN SINGLES ACTION; DAMIEN SANDOW MENTIONS THE MOMENT EDMONTON WOULD LIKE TO FORGET; RYBACK LIKES TABLES, BUT DOES KOFI?; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-05-31 21:58:16

Many people have fallen victim to the Shield in the last week. What will happen when the Shield wrestle in singles matches on this week’s episode of Smackdown. Seth Rollins faces Kane. Roman Reigns faces Daniel Bryan. Dean Ambrose faces Randy Orton.

We are in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada and your announcers are John ‘I might be a wrestling god, but I am nowhere near Wayne Gretzky in this town’ Layfield and Michael ‘Peter Pocklington’ Cole.

We see what happened on Raw when Alberto Del Rio used AJ Lee’s attempt at chicanery to help him beat Big E Langston.

Match Number Four: Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) versus Big E Langston (with AJ Lee)

They lock up and Langston with shoulders in the corner but he runs into boots from Del Rio. Del Rio with punches and a kick to the ribs. Langston drops Del Rio across the top rope and he gets a near fall. Langston with a waist lock but Del Rio with punches. Langston with a knee to the midsection followed by a gutbuster for a near fall. Langston with a punch to the midsection.

Del Rio kicks Langston from the turnbuckles and he hits a missile drop kick but it only drops Langston to one knee. Langston tries to take Langston down with two clothesline but they fail. Del Rio takes Langston down with the third clothesline. Del Rio ducks a clothesline from Langston and he tries for the lungblower but Langston counters with a belly-to-belly suplex.

Langston gets a near fall. The straps come down and he gets Del Rio on the shoulders but Alberto escapes and he hits the lungblower. Del Rio misses the step up enzuigiri and Langston with a waist lock but Del Rio with a German suplex. Del Rio with a super kick and he gets a near fall.

Del Rio floats over into the cross arm breaker but Langston gets to his feet and he picks up Del Rio. Langston blocks a kick and Langston with a punch and a running body block for a near fall. Del Rio applies the cross arm breaker in the ropes and Langston grabs the referee and AJ rakes the eyes on the floor.

Langston goes after Del Rio and he sends Alberto into the ring post. Langston rolls Del Rio into the ring and he hits the Big Ending for the three count.

Winner: Big E Langston
