Power Rankings: July 20, 2013

Alberto Del Rio

The World Heavyweight Champion moves up a spot on the rankings this week after successfully defending his illustrious title against Dolph Ziggler at Money in the Bank. Then, on Monday, Del Rio received a little help from another titleholder — Divas Champion AJ Lee — en route to downing Dolph once again.
