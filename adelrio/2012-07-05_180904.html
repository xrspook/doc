I Let the World Know My Angerby ~DBZandSonic

This comic was Hell to draw and color,but I finally got it done.*-*

So,if you watched Smackdown last night,you saw Sheamus getting his head slammed over and over again in the car engine and it's top.Then the ads came in,then you saw doctors helping Sheamus out of the arena with blood gushing down his face.Then...the next scene was Michael Cole being Michael Cole and interviewing A.J.Then Goatface comes out and interrupts,then Punk came out a few minutes after.When he got into the ring HE WAS PISSED.OFF.He told Cole to get out of the ring and he told him right then right there.So...it got me thinking,why would he be SO pissed off?

Then it struck me that of course,in my way of everything,he was mad at that Mexican Pinata hurting Sheamus badly.=3=

So thus this comic was created...enjoy..
