Backstage News On Alberto Del Rio Being More Aggressive, World Heavyweight Title Plans

By Marc Middleton | July 09, 2012

Earlier this year before Alberto Del Rio returned from injury, it was reported that Vince McMahon wanted to bring Del Rio back as a more aggressive heel.

WWE recently pulled the trigger on those plans and the idea going forward is that Del Rio will be more ruthless. Del Rio's recent attacks on Sheamus and Sin Cara are part of his new mean streak.

Del Rio is expected to win the World Heavyweight Title sometime this year.
