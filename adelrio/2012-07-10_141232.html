WWE Smackdown Taping Results (SPOILERS)

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 07.10.2012

Smackdown SPOILERS 7/13

Smackdown started off right after Raw went off the air.

(1) Alberto Del Rio beat Sin Cara via armbar submission.

(7) World Hvt. champion Sheamus beat Chris Jericho in a non-title match. Very exciting match - the best of Smackdown. After surviving not one, but two Walls of Jericho, Sheamus came back with a Brogue Kick to win. A great 18-minute match.

Alberto Del Rio came out to attack Sheamus at the top of the ramp. He gave him a cross-arm submission, which appeared to end the Smackdown episode.

Del Rio then he came to the ring and - probably just for the live crowd - trash-talked to the crowd. Sheamus then came back to the ring selling the left arm injury. After exchanging, Sheamus delivered a Brogue Kick to Del Rio to send us home happy. End of the Smackdown taping.
