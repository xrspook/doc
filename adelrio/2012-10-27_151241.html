Big Show vs. Alberto Del Rio

The Straight Edge Superstar puts the Mexican Superstar in the Anaconda Vise and won't let go until Del Rio agrees to a match at Survivor Series.

The WWE Champion greets the WWE Universe in Atlanta.

Big Show is Alberto Del Rio's opponent on Raw SuperShow.

Is Del Rio at a size disadvantage against the World's Largest Athlete?

The WWE Champion puts Big Show in a sleeper hold, trying to get him off his feet.

Del Rio's strategy is thwarted once Big Show gets his opponent outside of the ring.

The World's Largest Athlete uses his sheer size and strength to overpower the WWE Champion.

Del Rio's attack against Big Show is focused and aggressive.

But the angry giant prevails, defeating the WWE Champion.

CM Punk surprisingly emerges after the match.

Punk asks the WWE Champion, "Amigo, can you hear me?"

He tosses water in Del Rio's face, hoping to wake him.

Del Rio agrees and must defend the WWE Championship against Punk on Nov. 20.
