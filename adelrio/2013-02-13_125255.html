Spoilers WWE SmackDown Tapings Results For Friday

Submitted by Michael Bluth on February 12, 2013 - 9:45pm

WWE taped Friday's episode of SmackDown on Tuesday night from the Verizon Arena in Little Rock, Arkansas. Here are ongoing results:

The show begins with Big Show coming out for a promo. Big Show asked the crowd if they think it's funny when Del Rio messed with him, which got a huge pop. Big Show says Del Rio has never pinned him and never will. Chris Jericho interrupted him and said he's known Big Show for 16 years and can read him like a "big, stinky book." Big Show told Jericho to get out of here because he should have better things to do. Jericho plugged his Robot Fighting League show and says he's excited to be back with WWE.

Big Show defeated Chris Jericho. Big Show dominated early. Jericho fought back but got hit with a nasty spear and then a choke slam. Jericho rolled out of the ring to regroup. Back int he ring, Big Show went for a second rope elbow drop by nobody was home. Jericho went for the Walls of Jericho, but he couldn't lock it in. Big Show delivered a knockout punch and got pin.

After the match, World Heavyweight champion Alberto Del Rio came out and had a staredown with Big Show.
