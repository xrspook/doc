Lucha Underground Results: Prince Puma Defends, Alberto Unleashed, Catrina, More

By Sharon Glencross | July 01, 2015

Welcome to our WrestlingINC.com Live Lucha Underground Viewing Party. We will be providing live ongoing coverage of tonight's show, while you all can chime in with your thoughts throughout the show.

Marty the Moth is currently in the ring.

Marty the Moth vs. Alberto El Patron

Marty mocks Alberto and acts like he's not that intimidated. They brawl in the corner for a while and Alberto pretty much dominates him from the start. Good squush match that makes Alberto look good. Alberto hits an armbar and that's that.

Winner: Alberto

Afterwards Alberto gets on the mic and thanks Johnny Mundo for awakening something inside of him. He talks about his new sadistic streak and promises more at Ultima Lucha. Crowd is going crazy. This was a fun segment.
