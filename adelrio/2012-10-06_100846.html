WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: WWE CHAMPION STILL LOOKS FOR RESPECT; NO DEBATING THE WORLD TITLE CONTENDERS TONIGHT; TAG TOURNAMENT CONTINUES; DOES EVE CONTINUE TO PULL THE WOOL OVER BOOKER'S EYES?; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-10-05 21:59:50

We are live on tape from Tulsa, Oklahoma and your announcers are Michael ‘Who will be my co-mentor for the WWE Voice’ Cole and Josh ‘You couldn’t show me any love with a gig on Main Event’ Mathews.

We are back and David Otunga is in the ring and he introduces himself. He is here to talk on behalf of the WWE locker room. He mentions an incident that occurred after Smackdown went off the air. We see Alberto Del Rio’s attack on Randy Orton in and out of the ring.

On behalf of the WWE locker room, he wants to say thank you to Alberto Del Rio because Randy Orton has been a menace for more than a decade. He thanks Alberto for standing up to Randy.

Before David can continue, Randy Orton’s music plays but it is Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez. Del Rio makes his way to the ring in a very Vipery manner and even does Orton’s pose on the turnbuckles.

Alberto mocks Orton’s promo style and then he says that Orton would not be in the same building as him. He would end up on the floor, crying like a dying dog . . . like a dying snake. He cut off the head of that snake. Alberto says that he never lies. He is the new Apex Predator in the WWE.

Booker T’s music plays and he makes his way to the stage.

Booker wants to make sure that he understands what Del Rio said. Booker asks Alberto if he has some free time on his schedule tonight. Booker suggests a tag team match for Alberto and David. They will face . . . Kane and Daniel Bryan.

Match Number Six: Daniel Bryan and Kane versus David Otunga and Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez)

Bryan and Otunga start things off and they lock up. Otunga with a slam and then he poses. They lock up again and Bryan with kicks to Otunga. Bryan with a running clothesline and then he hits the running drop kick into the corner. Bryan poses because he am the Tag Team Champions. He poses for Kane and Otunga tries to attack him from behind but Bryan goes for the No Lock. Otunga goes to the apron and he pulls Bryan’s arm into the ropes.

Otunga with a neck breaker for a near fall. Del Rio tags in and he kicks Bryan in the ribs. Del Rio with more kicks and the referee warns him. Del Rio with a suplex for a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Bryan with elbows to Del Rio followed by a sunset flip for a near fall.

Del Rio with a clothesline and a near fall. Del Rio sends Bryan into the turnbuckles but Bryan with a drop toe hold that sends Del Rio into the turnbuckles. Kane tags in and he connects with a boot to the head and Irish whips and clotheslines into the corner followed by a side slam for a near fall.

Kane goes up top and hits the clothesline. Kane signals for the choke slam and he tells Bryan not to make the tag. Del Rio hits Kane from behind and Bryan goes to the floor. Del Rio with a step up enzuigiri for a near fall.

Otunga makes the blind tag and he hits the World’s Most Dangerous Spinebuster but only gets a two count. Kane grabs Otunga by the throat and Kane with a choke slam. Bryan tags in and he hits the diving head butt for the three count.

Winners: Kane and Daniel Bryan

After the match, Kane and Daniel Bryan argue who Am the Tag Team Champions.
