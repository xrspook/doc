WWE.com: WrestleMania XXX Press Conference: photos

WWE Chairman Vince McMahon and New Orleans Mayor Mitch Landrieu pose with a custom WWE Championship as WrestleMania XXX is officially announced for the Mercedes-Benz Superdome in New Orleans at a press conference.

"From all walks of life, [the WWE Universe will] experience the flavor, the hospitality, the music," Mr. McMahon guarantees. "There is no place in the world like New Orleans."

New Orleans Saints owner and vice chairman Rita Benson Leblanc takes the podium next. "I cannot express to you the joy I have that we are bringing WrestleMania XXX to our great city," she explains.

John Cena, who will challenge The Great One for the WWE Title at WrestleMania 29, follows.

The entire panel of Superstars, Divas and WWE officials says goodbye to the city of New Orleans, for now.

R-Truth provides the crowd with an interlude, performing his theme song "What's Up?" for the assembled members of the media.
