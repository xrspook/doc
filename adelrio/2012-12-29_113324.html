SmackDown Five-Point Preview: Dec. 28, 2012

BY Michael Burdick

December 28, 2012

What will Del Rio’s resolution be after being bested by Santa?

On Raw, Alberto Del Rio put the brakes on Santa Claus’ Christmas Eve gift-giving by accidently mowing down the jolly symbol of the holiday season with his car. Claiming it was an accident, Del Rio went on to fall to John Cena in a Miracle on 34th Street Fight Match — after Cena utilized a slew of gifts, including some “Saint Socko” help from a recovered Saint Nicholas himself, to help set up the victory.

How will The Mexican Aristocrat bounce back from such humiliation and grab some new-year momentum?
