Did Robbie E And Brooke Win $1 Million?, Alberto Del Rio Wrestling For Another Title, Hannibal

By Raj Giri | December 20, 2014 | 28 Comments

On January 6, 2015 at World Wrestling League's (WWL) Guerra de Reyes event in Puerto Rico, Alberto El Patron, f.k.a. Alberto Del Rio, will be challenging for the WWL World Title. El Patron will battle champion Shane Sewell and AAA's Ricky Banderas (former Judas Mesias in TNA) in a triple threat match. The event will take place at the Roberto Clemente Coliseum in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
