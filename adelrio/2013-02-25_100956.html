WWE House Show Results 02.23.13 - Waco, Texas

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 02.24.2013

Shamus and Undertaker defeated Damian Sandow and Wade Barrett. Taker walked out on the stage and slowly to the ring for a tag team match. The crowd chanted "this is awesome" during the match. Taker looked like he had never left.

Alberto Del Rio defeated Big Show in a No DQ match to retain the World Hvt. Championship. The crowd was able to vote for No DQ or best of three falls. Big Show was put through two tables and a kendo stick was used.

Tony Chimmel thanked everyone and that was it.

Notes: Overall, this was a very good house show. It was the first one in Waco with the new set. Undertaker was the shocker of the night. The crowd could not believe he was there. He was not advertised. I was working security for the building and never saw him arrive. No return date was given.
