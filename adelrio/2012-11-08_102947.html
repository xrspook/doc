WWE Alberto Del Rio, Ricardo Rodriguez, WWE NXT Memo Montenegro Oct 2012 (H)

Published on Nov 6, 2012 by jvarsallone

WWE superstar Alberto Del Rio teamed with his younger brother, WWE NXT superstar Memo Montenegro, for the first time. Part of a legendary wrestling family, they joined Ricardo Rodriguez in a six-man tag during a WWE NXT show at the (South Florida) West Palm Beach National Guard Armory. They battled WWE NXT's Corey Graves, Leo Kruger and Kassius Ohno.
