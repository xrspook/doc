WWE House Show Results 04.19.12 – Cardiff, Wales

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 04.19.2012

The live stage, lighting and ring set up was nice to see as it made the live event feel a bit more special considering it wasn't on TV. The arena was sold out and full which surprised me somewhat when you hear about the shares of WWE being in decline.

Alberto Del Rio (w/ Ricardo) defeated Percy Watson. Huge Ovation for Ricardo and his announcing in person is brilliant. Very mixed but loud ovation for Del Rio who played up to the crowd and they loved it. Percy Watson made his entrance to near silence. Squash match with Del Rio winning with the cross arm breaker. Was a shame because I would have liked to have seen something longer from Del Rio.
