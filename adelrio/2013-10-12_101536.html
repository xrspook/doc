Alberto Del Rio奇迹般地以WHC的身份还活着，而且还蝉联了POWER25的第一！

Power Rankings: October 12, 2013

Alberto Del Rio

The World Heavyweight Championship is most definitely NOT in a state of abeyance, as Alberto Del Rio once again successfully defended his gold against Rob Van Dam, this time at WWE Battleground. Even though the hardcore rules of the bout seemingly favored RVD heading into the match, Del Rio proved he can be as ruthless as it takes to walk away with his prized World Title. Throw in an impressive win over Mr. Money in the Bank, Damien Sandow, on SmackDown, and a momentary lapse in momentum against Ricardo Rodriguez on Raw is all but forgotten as the Mexican champion holds onto the top spot this week.
