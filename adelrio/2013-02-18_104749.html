WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio vs. Big Show - World Heavyweight Championship Match: photos

Del Rio plants Big Show to the canvas with a crushing kick to the skull.

The Elimination Chamber pay-per-view kicks off with World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio facing a very personal rival: The World's Largest Athlete, Big Show.

The ire between Del Rio and Big Show has only grown in recent weeks, and with the illustrious World Heavyweight Championship on the line, the stage is set for a slugfest.

Big Show uses his incredible power to manhandle the proud Mexican champion, but Del Rio refuses to stay down.

The World Heavyweight Champion locks in the excruciating submission maneuver the Cross Armbreaker, but his gigantic adversary manages to escape.

The giant eyes his foe as the hard-hitting contest takes its toll on both Superstars.

Capitalizing on Big Show being distracted by Ricardo Rodgriguez, The Essence of Excellence hits his super-sized opponent with a series of impressive and effective kicks.

Locking Big Show in the Cross Armbreaker, Del Rio coaxes the tap-out from the giant to retain his priceless title.

Now, Alberto Del Rio can set his sights on The Grandest Stage of Them All, WrestleMania 29, where he will defend his title against the winner of the Elimination Chamber Match.
