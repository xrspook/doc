WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio kisses Vickie Guerrero and mixes it up with Damian Sandow: photos

... Del Rio tries to charm his way out of the contest.

General Manager Vickie Guerrero kicks off SmackDown to a chorus of boos from the WWE Universe.

After Vickie shockingly made a WWE Hell in a Cell title match on Raw between World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio and a returning John Cena ...

Del Rio even goes so far as to give Vickie - at her request - a smooch on the lips!

The Queen Diva tells the World Champion where else he can plant one, because he's still facing Cena at the Oct. 27 pay-per-view!

"Mr. Money in the Bank," Damien Sandow, has seen enough and crashes the party.

After complimenting Vickie's superior intellect and Machiavellian tendencies, Sandow says he will prove himself a better man than Del Rio when he cashes in his contract.

After the GM creates a non-title match between the Superstars for later in the night, The Essence of Excellence cracks his opponent, initiating a brawl.

Brandishing his Money in the Bank briefcase, the Intellectual Savior forces the World Champion from the ring. Will he be as successful when they meet later in the night?
