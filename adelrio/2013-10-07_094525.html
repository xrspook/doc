WWE.com: Rob Van Dam vs. Alberto Del Rio – World Heavyweight Championship Match: photos

Rob Van Dam, who won the ECW TV Title in Buffalo, N.Y., in 1998, receives a hero's welcome in the city's First Niagara Center.

World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio insists Lilian Garcia announces him in Spanish.

Prior to the Battleground Hardcore Rules Match, Ricardo Rodriguez tweeted that this match conjured up conflicting emotions.

Before becoming RVD's second, Ricardo served as Del Rio's personal ring announcer.

RVD hits a moonsault off the ring barrier.

A steel ladder finds its way into the anything-goes match.

Del Rio batters his No. 1 contender with a steel chair.

A split-legged moonsault finds its mark.

The World Heavyweight Champion connects with a double stomp.

An attempt at Rolling Thunder ends in pain for "Mr. Pay-Per-View."

Ricardo uses the spit bucket to attack Del Rio, and when the Mexican Superstar demands he hand over the weapon ...

... Ricardo refuses!

The decision infuriates the ruthless Del Rio.

RVD, sensing opportunity, makes his move, attacking the World Champion with a modified Five-Star Frog Splash off the ring apron.

He tries for the Van Terminator, but it's crash and burn.

Del Rio takes advantage, locks in the Cross Armbreaker and retains the World Title.
