WWE Stars Visit Bullied Fan in Hospital

Several WWE stars recently visited a teenage wrestling fan who was hospitalized after being attacked by bullies, reports South Africa's Daily News. Sheamus and Alberto Del Rio were among the superstars who visited the fan in the hospital. It's being reported that the bullies performed a tombstone piledriver on the victim during the attack.
