WWE MAIN EVENT REPORT: SIX OF THE MEN IN THE BATTLEGROUND BATTLE ROYAL IN TAG TEAM ACTION; PAUL AND RENEE ARE REUNITED; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-07-01 20:46:14

We are in Newark, New Jersey and your announcers are Tom ‘Brodeur’ Phillips and Byron ‘Happy Canada Day’ Saxton.

Renee Young is in the interview area with Sheamus. Renee asks Sheamus about his United States Title Challenge for Raw. Sheamus says that this is America. The land of opportunity. He promised to be a fighting champion. He has proven it over the last few months. Sheamus says that is who he is. It is the birthday of the country that he loves. If you have what it takes to get into the ring with him, go ahead.

Alberto Del Rio says that not only will he become the Intercontinental Champion at Battleground, he will also take Sheamus’ United States Title. Then everyone will have something to celebrate.
