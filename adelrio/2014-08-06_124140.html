WWE Main Event results, Aug. 5, 2014: Swagger survives Del Rio en route to SummerSlam

BY Mitch Passero

August 05, 2014

Jack Swagger def. Alberto Del Rio

With Jack Swagger's Flag Match with Rusev at SummerSlam on the horizon, The Real American went to war with Alberto Del Rio in the Mexican border town of Laredo, Texas. In a fiercely passionate fight, Del Rio targeted Swagger's injured ribs throughout the match. But Swagger dug deep to withstand Del Rio's assault — including escaping a Cross Armbreaker — to lock him in The Patriot Lock to make The Mexican Aristocrat submit.
