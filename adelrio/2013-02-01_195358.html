SPOILERS FOR THE NEXT THREE WEEKS OF NXT, 1/31 TAPING RESULTS

By Andres Santiago on 2013-02-01 01:30:42

WWE NXT Taping - Full Sail, Orlando Florida – January 31, 2012

Post Taping

Alberto Del Rio came out and spoke about how talented the NXT roster is. When Del Rio mentioned that somewhere in the back is a future WWE or World Heavyweight Champion, Antonio Cesaro came out and challenged him to a Champion vs Champion match. Dusty Rhodes stopped Cesaro and made him face Big E Langston instead.

Antonio Cesaro vs Big E Langston

Langston won by DQ when Graves and O'Brian jumped him.

Initially they laid Langston out, but he came back and gave them both multiple Big Endings to close out the show.

Notes

A lot of people seemed to be disappointed that Del Rio didn't wrestle.

In-between Episode 1 and 2, Damien Sandow came out and gave the whole crowd some questions as part of his apprentice search. Dusty Rhodes interrupted him when Sandow started ripping on the crowd. Rhodes then announced Sandow vs Orton for the Feb 21st taping.

Emma's gimmick of being a bad / clumsy dancer seems to be catching on. I could see this eventually leading to a face turn for her if the crowd starts to get behind it.

After the show ended, Langston stayed at ringside signing autographs and taking pictures with fans for 15-30 minutes.
