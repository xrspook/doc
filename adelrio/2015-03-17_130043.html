AAA'S REY DE REYES POSTPONED AGAIN

By Kris Zellner on 2015-03-16 21:55:04

AAA President Joaquin Roldan came out for a press conference at Auditorio Benito Juarez de Zapopan with the roster and announced to everyone that due to there being a national holiday (Benito Juarez's birthday by coincidence) they couldn't get a permit to run a show in the building because the government was closed.

Roldan then said that the show would take place on Wednesday at 8 PM EST but due to other commitments Alberto el Patron & Brian Cage wouldn't be able to work the show and they hope to have a replacement match set for the show.

This has been quite the last 2 days for AAA.
