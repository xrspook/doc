WWE.com: COO Triple H presents an open forum: photos

As SmackDown begins, the WWE locker room has gathered at the top of the ramp ...

... after COO Triple H called them together to participate in an open forum.

Standing between The Game and the roster is The Shield.

The Game explains that, in essence, “Cody Rhodes fired Cody Rhodes” on Raw ...

... by showing disrespect and insubordination towards the office of COO, the WWE Universe and to his fellow Superstars ...

... and by failing to do his job by losing to WWE Champion Randy Orton.

He invites any and all Superstars and Divas to speak their minds on how business is being done.

While Damien Sandow approves of Rhodes being fired ...

... Triple H dismisses his comments as merely kissing up.

He then hears from Kofi Kingston ...

... Heath Slater ...

... Rob Van Dam ...

... and Ryback.

While The Game comments on Daniel Bryan not being among the competitors on the stage ...

... he rewards those that spoke up with matches, including RVD against the "Face of WWE," WWE Champion Randy Orton.
