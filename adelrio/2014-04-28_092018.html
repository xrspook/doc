The Shield vs. Alberto Del Rio, Bad News Barrett, Damien Sandow, Ryback & Titus O’Neil: photos | WWE.com

The Shield emerges for SmackDown's massive main event Handicap Match.

After a busy night of decimating their scheduled opponents in the 11-on-3 Handicap Match, The Hounds of Justice face Bad News Barrett, Damien Sandow, Alberto Del Rio, Titus O'Neil & Ryback.

The "architect" of The Shield, Seth Rollins, hits Ryback with a breathtaking dropkick.

Sandow, Del Rio, Ryback, Barrett & O'Neil take a moment to regroup at ringside.

The most eccentric Shield member, Dean Ambrose, tries to ground the powerful Bad News Barrett.

The match begins to turn into a wild free-for-all, and Barrett and Del Rio decide to take a walk on their teammates.

After Reigns catches up with Del Rio on the stage, he returns to the ring to help Ambrose and Rollins pulverize their remaining opponents.

Reigns flattens Sandow with a Spear for the huge victory for The Hounds of Justice.

The Shield isn't done yet! After the match, Reigns hits O'Neil and Ryback with a pair of Spears!

The black-clad trio ends SmackDown emphatically, hitting The Human Wrecking Ball with a devastating Triple Powerbomb!

Believe in The Shield!
