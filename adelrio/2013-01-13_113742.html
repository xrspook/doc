Alberto Del Rio: Aristocratic Acceptance

by *KamenRiderReaper

Just like the Miz before, it seems like Albero Del Rio has never seen a lot of work in my gallery. After last night on SmackDown, though, I felt like he was deserving of it. After drifting through the show has a heel without much motivation - albeit a cool ring announcer by his side (I am such a Ricardo mark, it's unreal haha) - it seems like this babyface turn has all of the potential to make him a bigger star than ever. I can see Del Rio being a working class hero kind of wrestler and I just think that his in-ring style is tailored more to a babyface role, though that's just my opinion.

It may be early to say but judging by what I saw last night, Del Rio's got a good shot and I really hope he cranks it up to eleven as the good guy from hereon out.
