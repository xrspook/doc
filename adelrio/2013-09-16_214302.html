Raw Five-Point Preview: Sept. 16, 2013

BY Mitch Passero

September 16, 2013

4. Does RVD have Del Rio’s number?

Former WWE and ECW Champion Rob Van Dam appeared poised to add World Heavyweight Champion to his illustrious resume at Night of Champions, but the cunning reigning titleholder, Alberto Del Rio, had other plans. After their brutal bout that resulted in Del Rio getting himself disqualified, RVD struck with a painful Van Daminator and strutted about the ring with the World Heavyweight Title. Is it only a matter of time until he claims the coveted prize for his own?
