Tribute to the Troops results: Honoring to those who serve

BY KEVIN POWER December 14, 2011

FORT BRAGG, N.C. – WWE’s Most Patriotic Show of the Year, Tribute to the Troops featured action-packed competition, celebrity appearances and top-notch musical performances in honor of America’s bravest men and women. Emanating from the home of the Army’s Airborne Forces and the Army Special Forces, the ninth annual Tribute to the Troops was an unforgettable experience.

John Cena, Big Show & WWE Champion CM Punk def. Alberto Del Rio, The Miz & World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry

Champions and WWE’s biggest titans faced off in a main event truly worthy of Fort Bragg. Teamwork reigned supreme as WWE Champions CM Punk and Big Show cleared The Miz and Del Rio from the ring and The World’s Largest Athlete punched World Heavyweight Champion Mark Henry right into an Attitude Adjustment from John Cena for the pinfall. The Cenation leader then thanked the troops for their service and sacrifice.
