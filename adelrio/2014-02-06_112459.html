WWE MAIN EVENT REPORT: MIZ GETS TO WRESTLE AFTER COMPLAINING ON MONDAY; A HOMECOMING FOR BIG E LANGSTON; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2014-02-05 22:05:55

We are in Des Moines, Iowa and your announcers are Tom ‘Field of Dreams’ Phillips and Alex ‘If you build it . . . ’ Riley.

Match Number Two: Alberto Del Rio versus Zack Ryder

They lock up and Ryder wants Del Rio to do something after breaking the hold. Del Rio with kicks and punches. Del Rio kicks and punches Ryder in the corner. Ryder with forearms but Del Rio runs into a boot from Ryder. Ryder with a flapjack and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a hot shot and then he dumps Ryder over the top rope to the floor.

Del Rio sends Ryder into the ringside barrier. Del Rio sends Ryder back into the ring and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a rear chin lock on Ryder with his knee in Zack’s back. Ryder with a jawbreaker but Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker for a near fall. Del Rio punches Ryder and he returns to the rear chin lock.

Del Rio with head butts to Ryder but Ryder sends Del Rio face first into the mat. Ryder with an elbow and a flying clothesline. Ryder with a kick and neck breaker for a near fall. Del Rio with an arm breaker and he sets for the float over but Ryder with a forearm into the corner followed by the Broski Boot for a near fall.

Ryder sets for the Rough Ryder but Del Rio sends Ryder over his head. Del Rio puts Ryder in the ropes and he hits the leaping double stomp. Del Rio waits for Ryder to get up and Del Rio with a super kick for the three count.

Winner: Alberto Del Rio
