Various News: Update On Alberto Del Rio's Father, Ricardo Rodriguez Still Training, More

Posted by Joseph Lee on 12.12.2013

Alberto Del Rio's father Dos Caras is continuing his international retirement tour. He wrestled in his Japanese retirement match on December 4, teaming with Mil Mascaras to defeat Nosawa and Mazada. They fought in front of 888 fans at Korakuen Hall. Dos Caras will return to Mexico for several more matches before finally retiring. One match will see him team up with his son.
