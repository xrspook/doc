WWE.com: Christian speaks out against Sheamus' Brogue Kick: photos

One week after getting the Brogue Kick banned, Alberto Del Rio and company open SmackDown.

Del Rio congratulates General Manager Booker T for his decision in outlawing Sheamus’ favorite finisher.

The injured Ricardo Rodriguez thanks his mom and his “fans” for all their support.

And the now-injured David Otunga speaks out against AJ Lee ...

... for putting him in a match against the World Heavyweight Champion on Raw.

To the surprise of the WWE Universe ...

... Del Rio presents Christian, via satellite, to give the opinion that the Brogue Kick should remain banned.

Daniel Bryan then emerges to reveal that he will face The Great White in a SmackDown main event, WrestleMania rematch.

He wants to prove that when Sheamus beat him for the World Title in 18 seconds, it was only because he was caught by surprise with the Brogue.

World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus joins the party.

He says that when he beats Bryan and Del Rio, they will learn that those who don't learn from history are destined to repeat it.

The Celtic Warrior then responds harshly when Otunga orders him to leave.
