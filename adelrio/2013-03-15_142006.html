WWE NXT Recap: Alberto Del Rio Appearance, Bo Dallas Vs. Bray Wyatt, More

By Christian Miller | March 14, 2013

WWE NXT Recap: Alberto Del Rio Appearance, Bo Dallas Vs. Bray Wyatt, More The show opens this week with a video about how Alberto Del Rio will be on NXT tonight.

Then Ricardo Rodriguez comes out and announces Alberto Del Rio, who comes out next. He talks about the WWE Universe at Full Sail University being fantastic. He says there is a lot of talent at NXT. Antonio Cesaro then comes out, and he says that in NXT there may be the next World Heavyweight Champion, but he is the greatest champion in the WWE. He challenges Del Rio to a champion vs. champion match.

Dusty Rhodes then comes out and he says they don't know how they do it in Sweden. Cesaro yells Switzerland and Rhodes apologizes. He says that Del Rio is a guest here, and Cesaro doesn't make matches. He says that if Cesaro wants a champion vs. champion match, then he can face Big E. Langston.

WWE NXT REPORT: KASSIUS OHNO PICKED THE WRONG ANNOUNCER TO PISS OFF; SUMMER RAE PICKED THE WRONG DIVA TO PISS OFF; A SOUTH AFRICAN CIVIL WAR?; A FAMILY IN CRISIS?; CHAMPION VERSUS CHAMPION; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-03-14 19:38:32

Tonight, NXT gets a visit from South of the Border when the current World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio makes his way to central Florida.

We are in Winter Park, Florida and your announcers are William ‘The Best Announcer in Orlando’ Regal and Tony ‘Universal Soldier’ Dawson.

We are back and Ricardo Rodriguez comes out and he introduces the World Champion Alberto Del Rio.

Alberto goes old school with the start of his comments. Thanks to everyone, he is the World Heavyweight Champion. This place is fantastic and amazing. Del Rio says that there is a lot of talent in the locker room. He is sure that back there we have the next WWE or World Champion.

Antonio Cesaro interrupts and he has his flag and a mic.

Antonio says that Alberto is right. The next World Champion might be in the back, but you also have the greatest United States Champion. He is the greatest champion in the WWE but he wants to challenge Alberto to a champion versus champion so he can prove that he is the greatest champion in the WWE Universe. Antonio makes the challenge.

Before Alberto can answer, Dusty Rhodes’ music plays and he makes his way onto the stage. Dusty says that he doesn’t know how they do it in Sweden. Antonio says that he is from Switzerland.

Alberto Del Rio is a guest here tonight. This is NXT and it is our house and Antonio does not make matches. He can stand there with his Sweden look on his face. If he wants a champion versus champion match, he can have one. He will face Big E Langston.
