Memo Montenegro

  • Height: 6'1"
  • Weight: 234 lbs.
  • Hometown: San Luis Potosi, Mexico
  • Twitter: @MemoMontengro

Driven by ambition and the will to live up to the high expectations of his family, Memo Montenegro is a second-generation Superstar from a lofty lineage of grapplers focused on making a dent in WWE.

Utilizing catlike quickness, uncanny agility and the lucha libre style, Montenegro plans to achieve career success on his own merits. This Mexican-born athlete is a perfect example of never judging a book by its cover. On the surface, this youthful competitor may appear distinguished and dignified, but on the inside is an aggressive, self-assured star.
