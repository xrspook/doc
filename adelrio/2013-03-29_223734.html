SmackDown Five-Point Preview: March 29, 2013

BY Alex Giannini

March 29, 2013

What will Jack Swagger’s next dastardly deed be?

Since his return to WWE, Jack Swagger has perpetrated some of the most sinister acts in recent memory. At the top of that list is the No. 1 contender’s snapping of Ricardo Rodriguez’s ankle courtesy of a devastating Patriot Lock. Guided by Zeb Colter, the “Real American” is a major threat to anyone who may get in his way on The Road to WrestleMania, and that road makes a stop in Hershey, Pa., tonight on SmackDown when the ruthless Superstar will take on The Great Khali.

What does Swagger have in store for The Punjabi Playboy? And will World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio play a part? Find out tonight at 8/7 CT, only on Syfy!
