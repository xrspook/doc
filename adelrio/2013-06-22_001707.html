SmackDown Five-Point Preview: June 21, 2013

BY Alex Giannini

June 21, 2013

Why did Brock Lesnar attack CM Punk?

What the hell was Brock Lesnar thinking?! When The Anomaly returned to Raw to unleash a brutal F-5 on fellow “Paul Heyman Guy” CM Punk, he left the WWE Universe in a state of utter shock. Heading into SmackDown, more questions than answers remain.

Will Heyman arrive on the blue brand with an explanation for his monstrous client’s stunning actions? For the matter, can the WWE Universe expect to hear from Punk or even the perpetrator himself, Brock Lesnar? Find out tonight on the blue brand!

SmackDown Five-Point Preview: June 21, 2013

BY Alex Giannini

June 21, 2013

Can Jericho tame the new World Heavyweight Champion?

Alberto Del Rio’s newfound mean streak has been on full display ever since the haughty Superstar viciously defeated Dolph Ziggler to reclaim the World Heavyweight Championship at WWE Payback. However, after walking out on his showdown against CM Punk on Monday, he will need every bit of his ruthlessness to overcome Y2J! Tonight on SmackDown, the down-to-earth Chris Jericho looks to bring Del Rio back down to earth.

Is a Codebreaker in store for the newly crowned champion? And after attacking the reigning titleholder on Raw, will former champion Dolph Ziggler return for more retribution?
