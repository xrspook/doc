WWE Live Event Results From Mannheim, Germany (4/27): Randy Orton Vs. The Big Show

By Raj Giri | April 29, 2013

Thanks to WrestlingINC.com reader Michal Petrgal, the owner of artofwrestling.cz, for sending in these results from Saturday's SmackDown live event in Mannheim, Germany:

Before the show I was at meet and greet with WWE Superstars, Hornswoggle and Jimmy Uso. Hornswoggle was absolutely without life and seemed upset. Jimmy was cool.

Alberto Del Rio (w/ Ricardo Rodriguez) defeated Jack Swagger (w/ Zeb Colter) via Submission with Cross-Armbreaker in a No Disqualification Match A Kendo stick and table were used. This was the longest and the best match of the night. It's very interesting that in Leipzig in November, Del Rio was the heel and was booed all over the arena. And after 6 months he was super over with crowd. Great development. Jack Swagger was 100% focused unlike the most of WWE Superstars at this event.

After the event, the Fandango theme song played for the fans.
