WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez poke fun at Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter: photos

WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio vs. Dolph Ziggler: photos

Jack Swagger, the No. 1 contender to Alberto Del Rio’s World Championship, kicks off Friday night with Zeb Colter by his side.

The No. 1 contender welcomes the SmackDown faithful to “Jack Swagger’s America.”

Swagger’s mentor, Zeb Colter, takes to the mic to address the WWE Universe.

Colter to the WWE Universe: “Admit that you all share the same values as Jack and me.”

Thankfully, World Heavyweight Champion Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez arrive to put a stop to Colter’s ranting.

Dressing up as Colter and Swagger, Ricardo and Del Rio poke fun at their rivals.

Colter and Swagger do not find the champion’s verbal attack to be funny in the least.
