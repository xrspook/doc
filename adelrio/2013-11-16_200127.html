WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: THE BEARD AND BEST EXPRESS; WWE LOGIC TO PLACE BLAME FOR MONDAY; CENA AND DEL RIO GO OVER THE TOP; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-11-15 22:00:16

We are in Manchester, United Kingdom and your announcers are Michael ‘Old King’ Cole and John ‘Robin Hood of Wrestling’ Layfield.

Alberto Del Rio walks in the back as we go to commercial. Get ready to go Over the Top.

We are back and Michael Cole is in the ring and he brings out the man who will challenge John Cena at Survivor Series, Alberto Del Rio. Alberto comes to the ring with his large Mexican flag.

Alberto says that he wants John Cena’s answer to his arm wrestling challenge.

No one comes out for a moment, but you don’t think John Cena is going to turn down a challenge because he makes his way to the ring.

John tells Alberto that they have an audience, referee, announcer, and a table; so let’s have an arm wrestling match. John asks a fan in the crowd dressed as Superman if he wants to see it. John tells Alberto he is going to break his arm.

They sit down at the table and John has turned his hat around so he is ready to Lincoln Hawk it up.

They lock up and Cena wins in seconds. Alberto demands a rematch and he says that he was not ready. Cena agrees to another one with Del Rio. Cena then shows Del Rio he cannot see him before locking up a second time. Del Rio still wants a second shot at John.

They lock up again and Cena wins again but Del Rio hits Cena and Cena goes down. Del Rio with a kick to the arm followed by a super kick and Cena is down. Alberto puts Cena’s right arm in the chair but Cena fights Del Rio off. Del Rio with shoulders and then he gives Cena a spinebuster through the table.
