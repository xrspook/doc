COMPLETE WWE RAW REPORT: PUNK GIVEN AN ULTIMATUM FOR HELL IN A CELL

By Richard Trionfo on 2012-10-08 21:02:07

We are live from Sacramento, California and your announcers are Michael ‘Hey Barsky, wanna join us’ Cole, Jim ‘Don’t touch the hat’ Ross, and John ‘For my next feat, I will climb a mountain of pennies for a gallon of gas’ Layfield.

Match Number Five: Daniel Bryan and Kane versus Dolph Ziggler (with Vickie Guerrero) and Alberto Del Rio (with Ricardo Rodriguez) in a Non Title Match

Before the match starts, Daniel Bryan interrupts the pyro from the ring posts and we get dueling ‘I am the Tag Team Champions’.

Bryan and Ziggler start things off and they lock up. Ziggler with an arm drag and he celebrates the move. They lock up again and Ziggler with a side head lock and shoulder tackle. Ziggler does a version of the Sexy Party but Bryan with a knee to the midsection and he works on the wrist and teases making the tag. Ziggler with a punch and Del Rio tags in. Bryan with a drop toe hold and Kane makes the tag.

Kane with a kick and punches to Del Rio. Kane kicks the ropes and continues to work on the wrist. Kane teases making a tag but Del Rio with a kick. Del Rio with a kick and Ziggler tags in and he runs into an uppercut. Kane with an uppercut and snap mare followed by a drop kick. Ziggler with a kick and punches. Kane sends Ziggler over the top rope to the floor and Bryan tags in when Kane goes to the apron.

Bryan with the running knee off the apron and we go to commercial.

We are back and Ziggler with a side head lock and Ziggler misses a Fameasser. Bryan tags in and hits a clothesline and then he hits the running drop kick into the corner. Bryan puts Ziggler on the top turnbuckle for a rana but Ziggler rolls through and gets a near fall with a sunset flip.

Del Rio hits Bryan from behind when the referee was distracted by Kane. Ziggler with a drop kick. Del Rio tags in and kicks Bryan and gets a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Bryan with elbows and a sunset flip for a near fall. Del Rio with a kick to the head and Bryan goes down.

Ziggler tags in and he hits a neck breaker after getting a little Rude. Ziggler with a leaping elbow drop for a near fall. Del Rio tags back in and he punches Bryan and hits a suplex and gets a near fall. Del Rio with a front face lock and kicks in the corner. Bryan with Kicks of No in the corner followed by an Irish whip and Del Rio moves when Bryan goes for the running drop kick. Del Rio kicks Bryan in the shoulder and makes the tag.

Ziggler with a near fall and then he applies a reverse chin lock. Bryan with a jawbreaker and Ziggler misses a splash into the corner and both men are down. Kane wants Bryan to make the tag. Ziggler grabs the ankle but Bryan crawls to his corner. Ziggler pulls Bryan back towards his corner and he tags in Del Rio.

Alberto with kicks to the shoulder to warm up for the cross arm breaker. Del Rio with a side slam for a near fall. Del Rio with a reverse chin lock. Del Rio with a tilt-a-whirl back breaker and Kane breaks up the cover. Ziggler tags back in and Del Rio traps the leg while Ziggler kicks Bryan. Ziggler and Bryan exchange punches. Del Rio tags back in and he kicks Bryan in the head and he gets a near fall. Del Rio with a rear chin lock.

Del Rio misses a charge into the corner and he hits the post with his shoulder. Bryan crawls to make the tag and he does. Kane with clotheslines followed by an Irish whip and clotheslines into the corner. Kane tries for a slam but Ziggler is tagged in. Kane sets for a double choke slam but Ziggler and Del Rio kick Kane and Ziggler with a Fameasser for a near fall.

Ziggler tries for a sleeper but Kane is in the ropes and he gets Ziggler off his back. Kane with a side slam and he goes up top but Bryan makes the blind tag and he goes up top for a missile drop kick and he gets a near fall.

Del Rio misses a splash into the ropes and he goes to the floor. Ziggler with a rollup but he can only get a near fall. Bryan with a round kick and he falls into the corner. Kane makes the tag and he sets for the choke slam and he connects and gets the three count.

Winners: Kane and Daniel Bryan

After the match, Kane and Daniel continue their debate about who am the tag team champions.

Alberto Del Rio and Ricardo Rodriguez are walking in the back and they are interrupted by Josh Mathews. Josh wants to ask Alberto about Randy Orton’s tweet. Alberto says that he is busy and he is working. Why should he care about a tweet. Alberto is told that the tweet is from Randy Orton. Alberto is told that Randy will be at Smackdown on Friday. Alberto and Ricardo walk away.

411's Instant Analysis 10.08.12: WWE Monday Night Raw

Posted by Chad Nevett on 10.09.2012

SEGMENT EIGHT: Dolph Ziggler & Alberto Del Rio vs. Team Hell No

Match Result: Kane pinned Dolph Ziggler

Match Length: Around 17 minutes

Some really good work from all men involved. Ziggler and Del Rio worked well as a team, while Bryan and Kane kept up their 'fight with each other almost as much as their opponents' gimmick that is nearing the end of its lifespan, I presume. There's only so long that the dysfunctional tag team can 'luck' into victory before it grows tiresome and, here, it seemed more like they simply outlasted Ziggler and Del Rio by hanging on long enough to take advantage of their fatigue. I have no real criticisms about this match. Entertaining from start to finish and I'd be ecstatic to see matches like this every week.

Rating: 8.5 out of 10
