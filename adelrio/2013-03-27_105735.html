SPOILERS For This Week's WWE SmackDown

By Marc Middleton | March 26, 2013

Thanks to Zach Border (@zachb1012) for the following WWE SmackDown spoilers from tonight's tapings in Hershey, PA:

Jack Swagger and The Great Khali wrestle in what was said to be a very bad match. They both get counted out. Swagger puts the Patriot Lock on Hornswoggle when Ricardo Rodriguez comes out and tells Swagger to come and get him. Alberto Del Rio sneaks in the ring and puts the armbreaker on Zeb Colter. Del Rio holds Zeb and Ricardo hits him with the crutch. Swagger comes back and Del Rio hits him.

3/26 WWE Smackdown TV taping spoilers: Full coverage of the taping for Friday's television show

Mar 26, 2013 - 07:33 PM

Dot Net readers Jeremy Hedenberg, Michael Kenney, and Aaron Jumper are at the WWE Smackdown taping in Hershey, Pa. and are sending the following report. Refresh the page for the latest updates.

Jack Swagger (w/Zeb Colter) fought The Great Khali (w/Natalya, Hornswoggle) to a double count out. Khali overpowered early, with Swagger getting in a few shots. Swagger locked Khali in the Patriot Lock on the outside and ignored the 10 count, resulting in a double count out. Afterwards, Ricardo came out to distract Swagger, who was ambushed by Alberto Del Rio, who sent Swagger and Colter packing. A good pop for Ricardo and a better one for Del Rio.
