WWE.com: "Miz TV" with 3MB: photos

WWE.com: The Miz, Alberto Del Rio & Brooklyn Brawler vs. 3MB: photos

The Miz introduces the most must-see show in WWE history, "Miz TV," for the first time live from Brooklyn.

The Awesome One introduces his guests for this installment of "Miz TV": Heath Slater, Jinder Mahal and Drew McIntyre, aka 3MB.

3MB claim to be a super group and McIntyre goes so far as to say they are even better than The Rolling Stones and even better than Brooklyn icon Jay-Z!

They announce their first live musical performance will be tomorrow night on Raw.

3MB leave the ring and confront the Spanish announcers, Carlos Cabrera and Marcelo Rodriguez.

When 3MB get physical with the announcers, they are interrupted by Ricardo Rodriguez ...

... and Alberto Del Rio!

When he gets dragged into the action, Miz helps Del Rio toss 3MB from the ring.

The Superstars of 3MB challenge Miz and Del Rio to find a third partner and face them in a match tonight.

Miz says he and Del Rio will find a partner and that the beating they will give 3MB will be ... "awesome."

3MB rocks out before their match at WWE TLC.

Ricardo Rodriguez prepares to introduce Alberto Del Rio amid the rubble of the TLC Match.

The Miz says he and Del Rio didn't have to look very far for a teammate, they found one of Kings County's finest, The Brooklyn Brawler!

The Brawler feeds off the energy of his hometown crowd, taking the fight to 3MB.

Heath Slater eventually regains control, battling back against the legendary fighter.

The Miz takes out Jinder Mahal with the Skull-Crushing Finale, then tags in The Brooklyn Brawler, who forces Mahal to tap out to a Brooklyn Crab.

The Brawler stands proud after picking up a victory during WWE's first-ever show in his hometown.
