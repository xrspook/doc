WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: A LOT OF VIDEO PACKAGES; A LOT OF AXXESS FOOTAGE; AND SOME WRESTLING TOO; FINAL 'SHOW' BEFORE WRESTLEMANIA

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-04-05 21:55:50

Sunday is Wrestlemania and we will see many videos about the matches on the pay per view as well as footage from WWE Axxess. We might even see a match or two on this week’s show from Washington, D.C.

We are in East Rutherford, New Jersey and Washington D.C. and your announcers are Josh ‘Leonsis’ Mathews and Jerry ‘Where is My Intern’ Lawler. Michael ‘Exit 16W’ Cole and John ‘You can see my penthouse from here’ Layfield are in East Rutherford.

We take a look at what happened on Monday night when Alberto Del Rio got beaten up by Zeb Colter and Jack Swagger with Ricardo Rodriguez’ crutches.

Alberto Del Rio is on the Axxess floor with Renee Young. Renee shows Alberto footage from Monday when he was attacked by Jack Swagger and Zeb Colter.

Alberto says that it is a good thing that he is at Axxess and not on Smackdown tonight. He still has the marks on back from Monday. He cannot wait until Wrestlemania. The match is not about Mexico, it is about the title. He will be proud to say that he is the World Champion after Wrestlemania.
