SmackDown Results: Bryan & Big Show overcame Batista & Kane in a SmackDown slugfest

BY Michael Burdick

March 07, 2014

Christian def. Dolph Ziggler

Four days after carrying out a fierce backstage assault on Sheamus on Raw, Christian battled Dolph Ziggler on SmackDown – with Alberto Del Rio sitting in on commentary.

At the height of the subsequent contest, a fast-talking Del Rio climbed up onto the announce table, bringing enough of a distraction for Christian to hit The Killswitch and topple The Showoff. The crafty move by the Mexican Aristocrat came as a direct response to “Need for Speed” star Aaron Paul’s distraction on Raw, which allowed Ziggler to defeat The Essence of Excellence.

As the smoke cleared, Sheamus interrupted the victorious Christian’s post-match interview with Renee Young, immediately engaging Captain Charisma and nearly Brogue Kicking his head off.
