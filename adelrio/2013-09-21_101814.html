SmackDown Results: Bryan & The Usos def. The Shield en route to WWE Battleground

BY Michael Burdick

September 20, 2013

RVD received a World Title rematch and suffered a sneak attack by Del Rio; Del Rio def. R-Truth

After apologizing to Rob Van Dam for Vickie Guerrero involving him in a Handicap Gauntlet Match, Triple H offered the high-flyer another opportunity at Alberto Del Rio’s World Heavyweight Championship at WWE Battleground. But, seconds after The Game left the room, Del Rio carried out a vicious sneak attack on his renewed No. 1 contender.

Moments later, The Mexican titleholder then stepped into the ring and overcame R-Truth with the Cross Armbreaker.
