WWE ANNOUNCES NEW JAPAN LINEUPS

By Steven Fernandes on 2012-05-26 20:15:23

The new main events for the WWE Japan tour in August are:

August 9:

Sheamus vs. Kane for the World Heavyweight Championship with John Ace as either special referee or special enforcer.

Randy Orton vs. Alberto Del Rio in a No-DQ match.

August 10:

Fatal 4-Way for the World Heavyweight Championship involving all four of the above.

They are also still advertising Big Show vs. Cody Rhodes for the IC Championship for both nights, but I guess it's an error on the website.
