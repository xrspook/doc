Sin Cara Returns to Action

Posted by Larry Csonka on 05.19.2012

According to Pwtorch.com, Sin Cara wrestled his first WWE match of 2012 at a WWE Supershow Saturday night in Florence, South Carolina. Cara suffered a knee injury near the end of 2011, and has been out of action since. Cara defeated Hunico in the opening match of the house show and is advertised for WWE's tour of Mexico next week.
