GEMINI (May 22 - June 21)

Compromise isn’t a dirty word. When push comes to shove during a disagreement with a coworker, rather than automatically throwing up defenses, like obstinate Gemini Alberto Del Rio, fight your stubborn instincts and take a breath. Hearing out an idea doesn’t necessarily mean waving the white flag of surrender. Instead, attempt to sway them with that Gemini charm. In the words of Chinese philosopher Sun Tzu, “Supreme excellence consists in breaking the enemy’s resistance without fighting.”

Notable Superstars who share this sign:

Daniel Bryan (May 22), Alberto Del Rio (May 25), Natalya (May 27), Drew McIntyre (June 6), Mark Henry (June 12)
