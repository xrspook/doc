WWE News: Stipulations Planned For Battleground Main Events, Rey Mysterio To Get a Manager?

Posted by Jeremy Thomas on 09.20.2013

Stipulations are reportedly planned for the main event matches at Battleground. One report notes that Daniel Bryan vs. Randy Orton may be an Iron Man Match, which could lead to an inconclusive finish which would allow for a rubber match at Hell in a Cell. Rob Van Dam vs. Alberto Del Rio is likely to get a stipulation too.

With Rob Van Dam set to take time off from WWE next month, it is possible that Ricardo Rodriguez may be paired with Rey Mysterio as his personal ring announcer.
