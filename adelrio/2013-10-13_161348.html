BRAY WYATT HURT: 10/12 WWE RESULTS FROM PROVIDENCE, RI

By Anthony Pires on 2013-10-13 00:50:30

My kids are at Dunkin Donuts Center and Bray Wyatt seemed to hurt his right leg pretty bad from a bump into the rail. Our seats had an excellent view of the Gorilla Position. After Rowan and Harper helped him to the back, they put him right on the ground while medical personnel checked him out.

The Great Khali beat Alberto Del Rio by DQ when Del Rio hit him with a chair. Damien Sandow came out to cash in but Khali chopped him.

The arena was half full with the upper deck blocked off but that may be a bad indication of fan interest because this is THE biggest night for a particular art festival in Providence, literally right next to the arena. The festival was part of the breast cancer awareness drive so there were TONS of people there. Bad night to schedule wrestling.
