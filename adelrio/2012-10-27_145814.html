Superstars confront WWE COO Triple H

12-Man Tag Team Match

Several Superstars are ready to air their grievances about WWE COO Triple H on Raw SuperShow.

Alberto Del Rio arrives with his newly won WWE Championship.

Del Rio says he couldn't celebrate his victory at WWE Hell in a Cell because of the attack by former Superstars The Miz and R-Truth.

Christian says Raw has become an unsafe place to work since Triple H took over the day-to-day operations.

According to Intercontinental Champion Cody Rhodes, every person in the ring has been a victim of Triple H's "reckless and negligent actions."

"We're actually going to do something about it," United States Champion Dolph Ziggler proclaims.

Jack Swagger says even his friends and family are concerned for his welfare.

"I feel especially vulnerable to Triple H being in charge," Vickie Guerrero says.

Harvard Law graduate David Otunga tells the WWE Universe he's studied cases like this.

"Either we can keep working and risk our livelihood, or we can band together collectively and contemplate legal action," he says.

The WWE COO finally emerges, ready to stand his ground against these Superstars.

"What happened to this place? What happened to WWE?" he contemplates.

"What I want you to do is stand in that ring and do what all of these people pay you to do, and that is shut up and fight!" he orders.

Despite their grievances, these six Superstars must participate in a 12-Man Tag Team Match on Raw SuperShow.
