WWE SMACKDOWN REPORT: ARE THERE CRACKS IN THE SHIELD?; WHY YOU DON'T TURN YOUR BACK ON YOUR OPPONENT; SOME BAD NEWS; AND MORE

By Richard Trionfo on 2013-12-06 22:00:31

We are in Tulsa, Oklahoma and your announcers are John ‘I wear a cowboy hat but I am not from Oklahoma’ Layfield and Michael ‘Did you know I talk to Triple H every week’ Cole.

We are back and Bad News Barrett is next to the announce table. He thanks the people of Oklahoma for a warm welcome. He says that he could come out here and tell everyone that they are loved, valued, and important. He is not going to do that because he has bad news. The people are cowardly, gullible sheep who are going to follow each other to the slaughter.

While Alberto Del Rio makes his way to the ring, we see footage from Monday when Sin Cara defeated Alberto Del Rio in a match that could change the face of the masked luchador.

Match Number Three: Alberto Del Rio versus Kofi Kingston

Before the match starts, Alberto attacks Kofi while he poses on the turnbuckles. Alberto sends Kofi to the floor and then he sends Kofi into the ringside barrier. Alberto kicks Kofi and then he sends him into the ring. Del Rio with a super kick to Kofi and then he punches Kofi. Alberto applies the cross arm breaker and Kofi taps while referees try to pull Del Rio off Kofi.

Del Rio leaves and we take a look at the carnage caused by Del Rio.
