WWE House Show Results 7.22.12: Omaha, Nebraska

Posted by Larry Csonka on 07.23.2012

Sheamus defeated Alberto Del Rio in a Last Man Standing match for the World Title. Lots of outside brawling in the crowd.

Loudest pops: CM Punk Sheamus Christian Ryback (still pretty audible Goldberg chants) McIntyre (only during the entrance)

Heel heat: Del Rio Daniel Bryan Cody Rhodes Damien Sandow

Great overall experience, other than a security guard yelling (and telling us "Out now!!") at me and my friend's "yes" chants. I'll chalk that up to a security guard that was unfamiliar with the product.
