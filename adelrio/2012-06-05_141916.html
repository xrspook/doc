WWE House Show 6.03.12: Savannah, GA

Posted by Larry Csonka on 06.04.2012

Matt Striker was our host and guest ring announcer. The new house show set up really adds a big show feel to the product. The building holds about 10,000, and it was maybe 60 percent full. On a side note, ringside seats were $101 a piece. Geez....

8. Sheamus beat Alberto Del Rio (w/Ricardo Rodriguez) in a World Heavyweight Championship match. Sheamus definitely got the biggest pop of the night. He hit White Noise early in the match, and Del Rio did lock in the arm breaker, but Sheamus went over with the Brogue Kick.
