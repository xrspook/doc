I try to post things in chronological order of when I draw them, and I tend to always have some sort of backlog, so things tend to get posted late. But with the news of Alberto Del Rio getting fired coming out, I felt he had to jump to the front of my list. It’s horrible timing, I just finished this a few days ago.Not gonna lie, I’m bummed ADR is gone. Yeah, his character was horribly stale, but I always liked the guy in the ring. And yeah, he might have been sleepwalking through his matches a bit lately, but with the way he was booked, can you really blame him? Let’s remember the good times, like when he first debuted. Or when he had a Chihuahua named Rey Mysterio. Dude was just gold.And just for fun, here’s the last time I drew Del Rio.
