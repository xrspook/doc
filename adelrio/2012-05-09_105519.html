**SPOILERS** For This Week's WWE SmackDown

By Marc Middleton | May 08, 2012

Thanks to Johnny P. and Lindsey for the following WWE SmackDown spoilers from tonight's tapings in Roanoke, Virginia:

* Sheamus and Randy Orton vs. Chris Jericho and Alberto Del Rio never happens as a big brawl breaks out. WWE officials and referees finally break them up.

* Randy Orton defeated Alberto Del Rio by DQ. Del Rio had the armbreaker applied and wouldn't break the hold, despite Orton reaching the ropes. This caused the DQ.

* Sheamus vs. Chris Jericho in the main event. Alberto Del Rio runs in for the disqualification. Randy Orton comes out and hits Del Rio with a RKO and Jericho escapes. Sheamus and Orton have a staredown to end the show.
