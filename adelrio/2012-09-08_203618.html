WWE.com: Alberto Del Rio attempts to have the Brogue Kick banned: photos

WWE.com: Sheamus vs. David Otunga: photos

Alberto Del Rio and legal expert David Otunga accompany Ricardo Rodriguez to the ring.

The personal ring announcer suffered undisclosed injuries ...

... resulting from World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus' Brogue Kick on Raw.

Del Rio talks about his wins over Randy Orton, Kane and John Cena in recent weeks.

But, he also talks about the unfair advantage The Great White has at Night of Champions ...

... comparing the Brogue Kick to being able to use a steal chair and describing its use as a "criminal act."

Otunga discusses their meeting with SmackDown General Manager Booker T ...

... in which they demanded, among other things, that the Brogue Kick be outlawed.

Unhappy that the trio brought the private meeting before the WWE Universe ...

... Booker T asks the fans whether the Brogue Kick should be banned.

After listening to their response, Booker determines that the Brogue Kick will still be legal.

World Heavyweight Champion Sheamus emerges for battle against David Otunga.

His Night of Champions No. 1 Contender, Alberto Del Rio, cruises in for a close look at the action.

All night long, Otunga and The Mexican Aristocrat have been trying to get the Brogue Kick banned ...

... after Ricardo Rodriguez got taken out with the earth-shattering maneuver on Raw.

The only Harvard-educated Superstar in WWE history tries to ground Sheamus.

That's one task that is easier said than done.

The Celtic Warrior dishes out his own brand of justice.

Sheamus hits White Noise, the maneuver that typically precedes the Brogue Kick.

But, before he can follow up with his devastating finisher, SmackDown GM Booker interrupts ...

… and, much the Del Rio’s pleasure, bans The Great White's big kick until further notice!

The resilient champion changes gears and locks Otunga in his new submission maneuver, the Cloverleaf!

Though he gets the impressive victory by submission ...

... the question remains whether Sheamus will win at Night of Champions without the Brogue Kick.
