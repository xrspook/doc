"Anita, you do not crack" the new challenge of Jorge Enrique Abello

Colombia.com (7/25/2004): The actor Jorge Enrique Abello, the same one who gained the affection of the public like Antonio, the protagonist of"La Costeña and the Cachaco" and that years before one had taken control famous of his personage of Armando Mendoza in"Yo I am Betty the ugly one", she will be now the protagonist of "Anita , you do not crack", the new novel of Telemundo.

After finishing"La Costeña and the Cachaco", Jorge Enrique Abello decided to take a rest. He dedicated himself to his family, specially to his small daughter who has it crazy enamored; he took advantage of the time to travel.

Nevertheless, recently Abello gave yes Telemundo. From the month of August it will return to the screens this time like Eduardo Contreras, an amused and Caribbean architect who lives in the city of Miami, in the United States, good wealthy and that he knows the love and he discovers a new but difficult world by which will have to fight.

"Anita, you do not crack" it is the new production of Telemundo and RTI, and will be directed by Put David. This pink history with comedy tone, counts the history of Anita Guerrero, a Mexican immigrant to whom it will give to life the Ivonne actress him Montero. The bad ones in this opportunity will be represented by the Venezuelan Natalia Streignard (wife of the Cuban Mario Cimarro) and Colombian actor Marcelo Cezán.
